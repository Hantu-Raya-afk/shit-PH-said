RM200 Million SMUE Fund Allegedly Misappropriated — MACC Probes Selangor Senior Citizens' Scheme

Said by: Amirudin Shari / MACC

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-10-16

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: The Malaysian Anti-Corruption Commission launched an investigation into the alleged misappropriation of RM200 million from Selangor’s Skim Mesra Usia Emas (SMUE) — the state’s elderly welfare scheme. Multiple officials linked to the scheme were questioned. The SMUE fund was designed to provide financial assistance to Selangor’s senior citizens, making the alleged misappropriation particularly egregious — money meant for the elderly allegedly diverted elsewhere under PH’s watch.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: RM200 million is not a rounding error — it’s a massive sum allegedly stolen from a welfare programme for senior citizens. Selangor is PH’s crown jewel, the richest state, run continuously by PKR/PH since 2008. If RM200 million can go missing from an elderly welfare fund in a PH-controlled state, what does that say about PH’s claim to be the ‘clean governance’ coalition? The fund was supposed to help the most vulnerable. Instead, it may have helped insiders.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: PH has governed Selangor for nearly 18 years. This isn’t an inherited BN problem — this happened on PH’s watch, under PH’s appointees, in PH’s state. The party that built its brand on anti-corruption is facing a corruption probe in its safest state. Amirudin’s office has been conspicuously quiet about the investigation. No public statements demanding accountability. No suspension of officials. No independent audit announced. Silence.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: This joins the sand mining probe, the MBI asset freeze, and the unexplained wealth allegations as part of a pattern. Selangor under PH isn’t a model of clean governance — it’s a state where MACC keeps finding things. At some point, one investigation is an outlier; four investigations is a pattern.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: RM200 million from a senior citizens’ fund. In your state. Under your watch. With your appointees. If BN did this, you’d hold a national rally. Since it’s your state, you hold a press conference about waste management. Demand a full public audit of SMUE. Suspend the officials. And tell the rakyat where the money went.

[BM] Rafizi Amaran: Dakwaan Farhash Jejaskan Kelayakan Antirasuah PM

Said by: Rafizi Ramli, Economy Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-10-15

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Rafizi secara terbuka menggesa Anwar untuk berhadapan dengan dakwaan terhadap Farhash Wafa, memberi amaran bahawa CPI Malaysia mendatar pada 50 mata — skor yang sama seperti 2015 semasa kemuncak skandal 1MDB. Kata ia ‘amat berbahaya jika pentadbiran dilihat tidak konsisten dalam memerangi rasuah.’

🔥 Kenapa Ia Penting

Apabila Menteri Ekonomi anda sendiri secara terbuka memberi amaran bahawa kelayakan antirasuah anda sedang runtuh, masalah itu tidak boleh dinafikan. Rafizi secara efektif memberitahu Anwar: keengganan anda bertindak terhadap Farhash secara terukur merosakkan skor rasuah negara. CPI 50 di bawah Anwar adalah sama seperti di bawah Najib semasa 1MDB — perbandingan itu sahaja sudah membinasakan.

💩 Kenapa Ini Karut

Amaran Rafizi diabaikan; beliau kemudian dipinggirkan daripada kabinet. Menteri yang berkata benar dihukum, sementara pembantu yang dituduh rasuah dilindungi. Ini bukan reformasi — ia adalah buku panduan lama dalam bungkusan baru.

🎯 Teguran Kepada PH

Menteri paling cekap anda memberi amaran secara terbuka bahawa melindungi Farhash sedang memusnahkan kelayakan antirasuah anda. Respons anda adalah meminggirkan menteri dan melindungi pembantu. Dalam mana-mana kerajaan reformasi, itu terbalik. Dalam kerajaan anda, ia adalah prosedur operasi standard.

[BM] Anwar Periksa Kerja Sendiri — PM Yang Juga Menteri Kewangan Bentangkan Bajet Dan Dakwa Ia Hebat

Said by: Opposition / Budget Analysts

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-10-10

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Bajet 2026 dibentangkan oleh Anwar Ibrahim — sebagai Menteri Kewangan — kepada parlimen, di mana beliau duduk sebagai Perdana Menteri. Beliau kemudian mempertahankan bajetnya sendiri di parlimen, memuji pencapaiannya, dan menolak kritikan. Orang yang menulis peperiksaan juga orang yang memberikan markah.

🔥 Kenapa Ia Penting

Dalam mana-mana demokrasi yang berfungsi, fungsi kewangan mempunyai pengawasan bebas daripada kepimpinan politik. Apabila satu orang mengawal kedua-dua agenda politik dan dompet negara, tiada semakan bermakna terhadap keputusan fiskal. Peruntukan bajet menjadi alat politik tanpa penelitian bebas. Peranan Parlimen sebagai badan pengawasan bajet terjejas apabila PM/MK mengawal kedua-dua eksekutif dan agenda fiskal.

💩 Kenapa Ini Karut

Anwar mengkritik Najib SECARA KHUSUS kerana memegang kedua-dua portfolio PM dan MK, berhujah ia membolehkan 1MDB dengan menghapuskan pengawasan. Kemudian Anwar mengambil KEDUA-DUA portfolio sendiri. Apabila ditanya mengenai ini, respons beliau pada dasarnya ialah ‘percayalah, saya berbeza.’ Itu bukan reformasi institusi — ia adalah pengecualian peribadi. Kelemahan struktur yang sama yang membolehkan 1MDB wujud di bawah Anwar.

📜 Kes Terdahulu

Najib sebagai PM+MK: 1MDB berlaku. Mahathir sebagai PM: peranan Menteri Kewangan menyediakan sedikit pemisahan. Di bawah prinsip tadbir urus baik, pengawasan fiskal memerlukan kebebasan institusi, bukan kepercayaan peribadi. Singapura memisahkan peranan tersebut. UK memisahkan peranan tersebut. Kebanyakan demokrasi yang berfungsi memisahkan peranan tersebut.

🎯 Teguran Kepada PH

Anda dipilih kerana PM terakhir yang memegang kedua-dua portfolio mencuri berbilion. Penyelesaian anda untuk mencegah 1MDB seterusnya ialah… meniru kelemahan struktur yang sama. Anda memeriksa kerja sendiri, dan tidak mengejutkan, anda terus memberi diri sendiri A+. Lantik Menteri Kewangan berasingan atau berhenti berpura-pura reformasi institusi penting bagi anda.

Anwar Marks His Own Homework — PM Who Is Also Finance Minister Presents Budget and Claims It's Great

Said by: Opposition / Budget Analysts

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-10-10

📌 What Happened

Budget 2026 was presented by Anwar Ibrahim — as Finance Minister — to parliament, where he sits as Prime Minister. He then defended his own budget in parliament, praised its achievements, and dismissed criticism. The person who wrote the exam is also the person who grades it.

🔥 Why It Matters

In any functional democracy, the finance function has oversight independent from the political leadership. When one person controls both the political agenda and the national wallet, there is no meaningful check on fiscal decisions. Budget allocations become political tools without independent scrutiny. Parliament’s role as a budget oversight body is undermined when the PM/FM controls both the executive and the fiscal agenda.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

Anwar criticised Najib SPECIFICALLY for holding both PM and FM portfolios, arguing it enabled 1MDB by removing oversight. Then Anwar took BOTH portfolios himself. When asked about this, his response was essentially ’trust me, I’m different.’ That’s not institutional reform — it’s personal exceptionalism. The same structural vulnerability that enabled 1MDB exists under Anwar.

📜 Previous Cases

Najib as PM+FM: 1MDB happened. Mahathir as PM: the Finance Minister role provided some separation. Under good governance principles, fiscal oversight requires institutional independence, not personal trust. Singapore separates the roles. The UK separates the roles. Most functional democracies separate the roles.

🎯 PH Callout

You were elected because the last PM who held both portfolios stole billions. Your solution to preventing the next 1MDB was… to replicate the exact same structural vulnerability. You’re marking your own homework, and unsurprisingly, you keep giving yourself A+. Appoint a separate Finance Minister or stop pretending institutional reform matters to you.

[BM] Tiong King Sing Hidang Arak Di Majlis Makan Malam Rasmi Pelancongan, Kemudian Berbohong Tentang Siapa Penganjurnya

Said by: Tiong King Sing, Tourism, Arts and Culture Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-10-05

📌 APA YANG BERLAKU

Menteri Pelancongan, Seni dan Budaya Tiong King Sing dikecam pada Oktober 2025 selepas minuman beralkohol dihidangkan di majlis makan malam gala semasa Global Travel Meet — acara yang dianjurkan di bawah bendera Tourism Malaysia. Klip video menunjukkan bir dan wain dihidangkan kepada tetamu Muslim, menyebabkan beberapa orang meninggalkan lebih awal.

Tiong pada mulanya menafikan tanggungjawab, mendakwa sektor swasta menganjurkan jamuan dan ia “bukan sebahagian daripada program rasmi Tourism Malaysia.” Tetapi bekas Menteri Kesihatan Khairy Jamaluddin menyiarkan tangkapan skrin di Instagram menunjukkan Tourism Malaysia disenaraikan sebagai penganjur majlis makan malam gala, mengejek Tiong dengan “Liar, liar pants on fire.”

Anwar akhirnya mengeluarkan “amaran tegas” kepada Tiong tetapi tidak memecatnya.

🔥 KENAPA IA PENTING

Ini adalah seorang menteri yang berbohong secara rasmi mengenai acara kementeriannya sendiri, ditangkap dengan bukti dokumentari, dan menghadapi sifar akibat selain “amaran tegas.” Dalam kerajaan yang menjanjikan integriti dan akauntabiliti, seorang menteri yang berbohong secara terbuka dan ditangkap sepatutnya menghadapi akibat sebenar — bukan tepukan di pergelangan tangan.

Insiden itu juga mendedahkan percanggahan mendalam dalam pengurusan gabungan Anwar. Tiong dari GPS (Gabungan Parti Sarawak), rakan gabungan kritikal. Memecatnya berisiko kehilangan sokongan Sarawak. Jadi akauntabiliti mengambil tempat belakang kepada aritmetik gabungan.

💩 KENAPA INI KARUT

Pembelaan Tiong adalah kelas induk dalam memanipulasi. Logo Tourism Malaysia ada di latar belakang acara. Kakitangan Tourism Malaysia hadir. Tetapi entah bagaimana ia adalah kesalahan “sektor swasta.” Apabila Khairy mengeluarkan bukti, Tiong beralih kepada menyatakan “penyesalan mendalam” tanpa benar-benar memohon maaf.

Dan respons Anwar? “Amaran tegas.” Anwar yang sama yang pernah berjanji menteri-menterinya akan dipegang kepada standard tertinggi. Seorang menteri yang berbohong secara terbuka mengenai acaranya sendiri mendapat tamparan ringan. Jika ini menteri BN di bawah Najib, PH akan menuntut peletakan jawatan dalam 24 jam.

📜 KES TERDAHULU

Tiong King Sing adalah menteri yang sama yang menjerit “Tahu tak siapa saya?” di lapangan terbang pada Februari 2025 — corak hak istimewa dan keangkuhan. Beliau juga sebelum ini terlibat dalam kontroversi berkaitan pengambilan alkohol sebelum insiden gala ini. Orang ini mempunyai rekod memalukan kerajaan dan menghadapi sifar akibat sebenar.

Di bawah BN, menteri yang berbohong mengenai acara awam dikoyak-koyak oleh pembangkang ketika itu. Amnesia terpilih PH mengenai standard akauntabiliti adalah tema berulang.

🎯 TEGURAN KEPADA PH

PH menjanjikan Kerajaan Madani — kerajaan bertamadun yang dibina atas integriti. Seorang menteri yang berbohong tentang siapa menganjurkan acara rasmi, ditangkap basah, dan hanya menghadapi “amaran tegas” bukan integriti. Ia adalah kekebalan. Sama ada standard anda terpakai kepada semua menteri anda — termasuk rakan gabungan dari Sarawak — atau ia tidak bermakna apa-apa. Tiong sepatutnya digugurkan daripada Kabinet. Hakikat bahawa dia tidak memberitahu rakyat Malaysia segala-galanya yang mereka perlu tahu tentang di mana kelangsungan gabungan berada berbanding akauntabiliti.

Tiong King Sing Serves Alcohol at Official Tourism Gala, Then Lies About Who Organised It

Said by: Tiong King Sing, Tourism, Arts and Culture Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-10-05

📌 WHAT HAPPENED

Tourism, Arts and Culture Minister Tiong King Sing came under fire in October 2025 after alcoholic beverages were served at a gala dinner during the Global Travel Meet — an event organised under the Tourism Malaysia banner. Video clips showed beer and wine being served to Muslim guests, prompting several to leave early.

Tiong initially denied responsibility, claiming the private sector organised the catering and that it was “not part of Tourism Malaysia’s official programme.” But former Health Minister Khairy Jamaluddin posted screenshots on Instagram showing Tourism Malaysia was listed as the organiser of the gala dinner, mocking Tiong with “Liar, liar pants on fire.”

Anwar eventually issued a “stern warning” to Tiong but did not sack him.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS

This is a minister lying on record about his own ministry’s event, getting caught with documentary evidence, and facing zero consequences beyond a “stern warning.” In a government that promised integrity and accountability, a minister who lies publicly and gets caught should face real consequences — not a pat on the wrist.

The incident also exposed the deep contradictions in Anwar’s coalition management. Tiong is from GPS (Gabungan Parti Sarawak), a critical coalition partner. Sacking him would risk losing Sarawak’s support. So accountability takes a back seat to coalition arithmetic.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT

Tiong’s defence was a masterclass in gaslighting. The Tourism Malaysia logo was on the event backdrop. Tourism Malaysia staff were present. But somehow it was the “private sector’s” fault. When Khairy produced the receipts, Tiong pivoted to expressing “deep regret” without actually apologising.

And Anwar’s response? A “stern warning.” The same Anwar who once promised that his ministers would be held to the highest standards. A minister who lies publicly about his own event gets a slap on the wrist. If this were a BN minister under Najib, PH would have demanded a resignation within 24 hours.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES

Tiong King Sing is the same minister who screamed “Do you know who I am?” at an airport in February 2025 — a pattern of entitlement and arrogance. He was also previously embroiled in controversies linked to alcohol consumption before this gala incident. The man has a track record of embarrassing the government and facing no real consequences.

Under BN, ministers who lied about public events were torn apart by the then-opposition. PH’s selective amnesia about accountability standards is a recurring theme.

🎯 PH CALLOUT

PH promised Kerajaan Madani — a civilised government built on integrity. A minister who lies about who organised an official event, gets caught red-handed, and faces only a “stern warning” is not integrity. It’s impunity. Either your standards apply to all your ministers — including coalition partners from Sarawak — or they mean nothing. Tiong should have been dropped from Cabinet. The fact that he wasn’t tells Malaysians everything they need to know about where coalition survival ranks versus accountability.
