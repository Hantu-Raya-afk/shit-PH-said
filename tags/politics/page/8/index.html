[BM] Anwar Amaran 'Golongan Kaya' Manipulasi Media — Sementara Kerajaannya Sendiri Lindungi Ketua SPRM Dari Akauntabiliti

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-07

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Pada 7 Mac 2026, Perdana Menteri Anwar Ibrahim menggunakan majlis iftar di Kulim untuk menuduh golongan elit Malaysia yang tidak dinamakan “secara rasuah menggunakan kekayaan mereka untuk mempengaruhi media antarabangsa, naratif dalam talian dan politik tempatan.” Beliau berkata beliau bukan merujuk kepada pembangkang sahaja tetapi kepada “semua orang,” dan menggesa rakyat Malaysia “kekal berwaspada” terhadap “skim dan konspirasi asing.”

🔥 Kenapa Ia Penting

Ini bukan kenyataan sekali sahaja. Ia sebahagian daripada naratif yang sengaja dan semakin meningkat oleh Anwar untuk membingkaikan semula penelitian sah terhadap kerajaannya — terutamanya mengenai skandal pegangan saham Azam Baki — sebagai konspirasi yang dibiayai asing oleh elit bayangan. Dengan memastikan tuduhan kabur (“bukan pembangkang sahaja — semua orang”), Anwar memberikan dirinya perlindungan retorik maksimum untuk menolak mana-mana pengkritik, wartawan, atau institusi yang meminta akauntabiliti kerajaannya.

💩 Kenapa Ia Karut

Anwar menghabiskan berdekad dalam pembangkang menuduh PM-PM sebelumnya perkara yang sama — menggunakan retorik “keselamatan negara” yang kabur untuk membungkam pengkritik dan mengelak akauntabiliti. Kini beliau melakukannya sendiri, dari pentas iftar lagi, sementara kerajaannya enggan menggantung ketua SPRM yang didakwa secara boleh dipercayai melanggar undang-undang pegangan saham. Lelaki yang pernah memimpin perhimpunan reformasi kini memberi amaran kepada rakyat Malaysia supaya tidak “terperangkap dan digunakan sebagai alat” — yang bermaksud: berhenti bertanya soalan sukar tentang mengapa agensi antirasuah saya kelihatan terjejas.

📜 Kes Terdahulu

Ini sesuai dengan corak setua politik Malaysia sendiri. Mahathir menyalahkan “spekulator Yahudi” semasa krisis kewangan Asia 1997. Najib menolak laporan Wall Street Journal mengenai 1MDB sebagai konspirasi oleh “orang tertentu” yang mahu “menjatuhkan kerajaan yang dipilih secara demokratik.” Anwar sendiri, ketika pembangkang, memanggil pengelakan ini autoritarian. Kini berkuasa, beliau mengamalkan rangka kerja retorik yang sama.

🎯 Panggilan PH

Anda berlari atas ketelusan, reformasi institusi, dan kebebasan media. Kini Perdana Menteri anda berdiri di podium menuduh elit yang tidak dinamakan mempersenjatai media — sementara laporan Bloomberg mengenai pegangan saham Azam Baki kekal tidak dijawab, sementara Bersih ditegur secara terbuka kerana memanggil SPRM alat politik. Jika “golongan kaya” adalah masalahnya, mulakan dengan mereka dalam Kabinet anda sendiri yang mendapat manfaat daripada sistem yang anda janji untuk runtuhkan.

[BM] Rafizi Beritahu Pengundi PH 'Tunggu Sampai Jun' — Bayangkan Pisah Dari PKR Dan 'Berlayar Sendiri'

Said by: Rafizi Ramli, Pandan MP / Former Economy Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-07

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Bekas Menteri Ekonomi Rafizi Ramli, yang meletak jawatan kabinet pada Mei 2025 selepas kalah pertandingan timbalan presiden PKR, memberitahu penyokong Pakatan Harapan dalam temu bual Sin Chew Daily supaya “tunggu sampai Jun,” membayangkan pilihan politik baharu akan diperkenalkan. Rafizi berkata beliau dan sekutunya terbuka untuk “berlayar sendiri” dan sedang menerokai “konfigurasi politik berbeza” — yang boleh melibatkan parti baharu, gabungan, atau platform politik. Beliau mengesahkan akan mempertahankan kerusi parlimen Pandan dalam pilihan raya umum seterusnya tetapi pasti bukan di bawah bendera PKR. Berasingan, Reuters melaporkan beberapa ahli parlimen PKR mempertimbangkan bertanding sebagai bebas, setelah hilang keyakinan terhadap kepimpinan Anwar.

🔥 Kenapa Ini Penting

Ini ancaman dalaman paling serius terhadap Pakatan Harapan sejak Langkah Sheraton. Rafizi bukan ahli parlimen belakang — beliau tokoh reformis paling popular dalam politik Malaysia dalam kalangan pengundi muda, orang yang mendedahkan skandal NFC dan 1MDB, dan arkitek kempen “Ayuh Malaysia” yang membantu menyampaikan PRU15 untuk Anwar. Jika Rafizi melancarkan kenderaan kuasa ketiga di Semenanjung Malaysia — berpotensi diselaraskan dengan Parti Warisan Shafie Apdal — ia boleh memecahkan asas pengundi bandar dan berminda reformasi PH tepat sebelum pilihan raya umum.

💩 Kenapa Ini Karut

Bukan langkah Rafizi — tindak balas Anwar terhadapnya. Bukannya menangani rungutan sah yang menolak Rafizi keluar (pilihan raya parti yang ditipu, keengganan bertindak terhadap Azam Baki, pengabaian reformasi), kem Anwar dilaporkan mempersenjatakan SPRM untuk mengganggu bekas pembantu Rafizi James Chai dan menggunakan jentera kerajaan untuk melemahkan Ahli Parlimen Pandan itu. Perdana Menteri yang membina kerjayanya atas reformasi kini menggunakan buku panduan yang sama tepat Mahathir dan Najib gunakan terhadapnya.

📜 Kes Terdahulu

Politik Malaysia pernah menyaksikan filem ini sebelumnya. Pada 1998, Anwar sendiri adalah orang dalam reformis yang memisahkan diri dan membentuk gerakan yang akhirnya menjadi PKR. Pada 2015, Muhyiddin Yassin dipecat kerana mempersoalkan Najib mengenai 1MDB dan kemudian membentuk Bersatu. Coraknya konsisten: perdana menteri Malaysia melayan bantahan dalaman sebagai pengkhianatan bukannya akauntabiliti. Yang menjadikan kes ini unik merosakkan PH ialah pemergian Rafizi sepenuhnya disebabkan oleh Anwar sendiri — beliau menukar semula pilihan raya dalaman PKR daripada undian ahli langsung kepada sistem perwakilan khusus untuk menyingkir Rafizi daripada jawatan timbalan presiden.

🎯 Seruan Kepada PH

Anwar, anda mencipta Rafizi. Beliau mendedahkan skandal yang anda berkempen atasnya. Beliau membina permainan lapangan yang membawa anda ke Putrajaya. Dan anda membalasnya dengan memanipulasi pilihan raya dalaman parti anda, menyingkirkannya daripada kabinet, dan menghala agensi antirasuah kepada orangnya. Kini beliau membina alternatif — dan anda ketakutan, kerana anda tahu lebih baik daripada sesiapa apa yang berlaku apabila reformis popular memisahkan diri daripada pertubuhan yang rosak. Anda menulis buku panduan itu pada 1998.

Anwar Warns of 'Wealthy Elites' Manipulating Media — While His Own Government Shields the MACC Chief From Accountability

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-07

📌 What Happened

On March 7, 2026, Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim used an iftar event in Kulim to accuse unnamed Malaysian elites of “corruptly using their wealth to exert influence on international media, online narratives and local politics.” He said he was not referring only to the opposition but to “everyone,” and urged Malaysians to “remain vigilant” against “foreign schemes and conspiracies.” The statement came just four days after he told Parliament about an alleged plot — involving a former senior minister’s wife, foreign PR firms, and Bloomberg — to destabilise his government.

🔥 Why It Matters

This is not a one-off remark. It is part of a deliberate, escalating narrative by Anwar to reframe legitimate scrutiny of his government — particularly over the Azam Baki shareholding scandal — as a foreign-funded conspiracy by shadowy elites. By keeping the accusation vague (“not just the opposition — everyone”), Anwar gives himself maximum rhetorical cover to dismiss any critic, journalist, or institution that holds his government accountable. This is the same playbook used by Najib Razak during the 1MDB scandal: when the evidence gets uncomfortable, blame foreign interference.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

Anwar spent decades in opposition accusing previous PMs of exactly this — using vague “national security” rhetoric to silence critics and avoid accountability. Now he’s doing it himself, from an iftar stage no less, while his government refuses to suspend an MACC chief credibly accused of breaching shareholding laws. The man who once led reformasi rallies is now warning Malaysians not to be “trapped and used as tools” — by which he means: stop asking hard questions about why my anti-corruption agency looks compromised. The hypocrisy is not subtle. It is structural.

📜 Previous Cases

This fits a pattern as old as Malaysian politics itself. Mahathir blamed “Jewish speculators” during the 1997 Asian financial crisis to deflect from governance failures. Najib dismissed the Wall Street Journal’s 1MDB reporting as a conspiracy by “certain people” who wanted to “topple a democratically elected government.” Anwar himself, when he was in opposition, called these deflections authoritarian. Now in power, he has adopted the identical rhetorical framework — foreign agents, shadowy elites, media manipulation — with only the names changed.

🎯 PH Callout

You ran on transparency, institutional reform, and media freedom. You told Malaysians that under your government, the press would not be intimidated and corruption would be fought without fear or favour. Now your Prime Minister is standing at a podium accusing unnamed elites of weaponising the media — while Bloomberg’s reporting on Azam Baki’s shareholdings remains unanswered, while Bersih gets publicly scolded for calling the MACC a political tool, and while Rafizi — your own former economy minister — has to take to the streets to demand accountability. If “wealthy elites” are the problem, start with the ones in your own Cabinet who benefit from the system you promised to dismantle.

Anwar's 'Perfect Storm': Anti-Graft Crisis, Party Fractures, Coalition Jitters

Said by: CNA Analysis

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-07

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: Multiple challenges are converging on Anwar Ibrahim that may force him to call early elections nearly two years ahead of the February 2028 deadline. An anti-graft crisis at MACC (Bloomberg exposés on Azam Baki), PKR internal fractures following Rafizi Ramli’s departure from cabinet, and coalition partners openly reconsidering their ties. CNA described it as a “perfect storm” of political instability. UMNO’s secret Bangkok meeting with opposition parties, DAP reviewing coalition ties, and the PKR factional war all threaten to bring down the Unity Government before its term ends.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: When CNA uses the phrase “perfect storm” to describe your political situation, you are in serious trouble. The convergence of MACC credibility collapse, PKR internal warfare, and coalition instability is unprecedented in Anwar’s tenure. Each crisis alone would be manageable; together, they threaten the government’s survival. If early elections are called, PH would face voters with most manifesto promises unmet, corruption perceptions unchanged, and the cost of living still rising. The political capital Anwar accumulated through years of struggle has been spent on coalition survival rather than reform delivery.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: Anwar’s response to every crisis has been to consolidate power rather than address root causes. MACC under fire? Defend Azam Baki. Rafizi raising legitimate concerns? Sideline him. DAP unhappy? Reshuffle them into weaker portfolios. Bersih criticising? Call them unfair. Consolidation only works when you have political capital to spend — and that capital is depleting fast. A PM who spends all his energy managing crises he created through inaction has no energy left for governance.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: PH 1.0 collapsed in 2020 through the Sheraton Move — also triggered by coalition instability and internal betrayal. Muhyiddin’s government fell in 2021 due to loss of confidence. Ismail Sabri lasted barely a year. Malaysia has had five PMs in eight years. The pattern of coalition governments imploding under internal pressure is not new — what’s new is that PH was supposed to break this cycle. Instead, it’s repeating it.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: You came to power promising stability and reform. Three years later, your coalition is fracturing, your anti-corruption agency is under fire, your own party is split, and analysts are talking about snap elections. This is not the “stability” you promised. Fix the MACC. Reconcile with Rafizi. Deliver on your manifesto. Or call elections and let the rakyat decide if three years of broken promises deserve a second term.

Rafizi Tells PH Voters to 'Wait Till June' — Hints at Breaking Away from PKR and 'Sailing His Own Ship'

Said by: Rafizi Ramli, Pandan MP / Former Economy Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-07

📌 What Happened

Former Economy Minister Rafizi Ramli, who quit the cabinet in May 2025 after losing the PKR deputy presidency contest, told Pakatan Harapan supporters in a Sin Chew Daily interview to “wait till June,” hinting that a new political option would be unveiled. Rafizi said he and his allies are open to “sailing their own ship” and are exploring “different political configurations” — which could involve a new party, coalition, or political platform. He confirmed he will defend his Pandan parliamentary seat in the next general election but definitively not under the PKR banner. Separately, Reuters reported that several PKR lawmakers are considering contesting as independents, having lost confidence in Anwar’s leadership.

🔥 Why It Matters

This is the most serious internal threat to Pakatan Harapan since the Sheraton Move. Rafizi isn’t a backbencher — he’s the most popular reformist figure in Malaysian politics among younger voters, the man who exposed the NFC and 1MDB scandals, and the architect of the “Ayuh Malaysia” campaign that helped deliver GE15 for Anwar. If Rafizi launches a third-force vehicle in Peninsular Malaysia — potentially aligned with Shafie Apdal’s Parti Warisan, which already swept Chinese votes in Sabah — it could split PH’s urban and reform-minded voter base right before a general election that sources say could come as early as late 2026.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

Not Rafizi’s move — Anwar’s response to it. Instead of addressing the legitimate grievances that pushed Rafizi out (the rigged party election, the refusal to act on Azam Baki, the abandonment of reform), Anwar’s camp has reportedly weaponized MACC to harass Rafizi’s former aide James Chai and used government machinery to undermine the Pandan MP. The Prime Minister who built his career on reformasi is now using the exact same playbook Mahathir and Najib used against him. The man who once stood trial for challenging his boss is now the boss who punishes anyone who challenges him.

📜 Previous Cases

Malaysian politics has seen this movie before. In 1998, Anwar himself was the reformist insider who broke away and formed a movement that eventually became PKR. In 2015, Muhyiddin Yassin was sacked for questioning Najib over 1MDB and later formed Bersatu. The pattern is consistent: Malaysian prime ministers treat internal dissent as treason rather than accountability. What makes this case uniquely damaging for PH is that Rafizi’s departure was entirely self-inflicted by Anwar — he reverted PKR’s party elections from a direct member vote to a delegate system specifically to remove Rafizi from the deputy president position, prioritizing dynastic loyalty over competence.

🎯 PH Callout

Anwar, you made Rafizi. He exposed the scandals you campaigned on. He built the ground game that got you to Putrajaya. And you repaid him by rigging your party’s internal elections, sidelining him from cabinet, and siccing the anti-corruption agency on his people. Now he’s building an alternative — and you’re terrified, because you know better than anyone what happens when a popular reformist breaks away from a corrupt establishment. You wrote that playbook in 1998. The difference is: Rafizi has data, youth support, and the receipts. You just have the machinery. And machinery didn’t save Najib either.
