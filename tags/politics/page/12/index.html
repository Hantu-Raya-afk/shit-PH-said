[BM] Anwar Guna 'Zionis' Dan 'Jurubank' Di Parlimen Untuk Burukkan Balu Daim — Peguam Panggil Ia 'Pembohongan Nyata'

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-03

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Pada 3 Mac, Perdana Menteri Anwar Ibrahim memberitahu Dewan Rakyat bahawa dakwaan komplot menggulingkan kerajaannya — yang disandarkan kepada Na’imah Abdul Khalid, balu bekas Menteri Kewangan Daim Zainuddin — melibatkan “kumpulan Zionis terkemuka” dan jurubank antarabangsa. Beliau mendakwa firma perhubungan awam berpangkalan di UK telah dilantik untuk melancarkan “serangan terselaras” terhadap institusi negara. Ini berlaku sehari selepas MalaysiaNow mendedahkan pemimpin PKR, termasuk tokoh rapat dengan Anwar, sendiri melantik perunding AS pada 2016 untuk membina tekanan White House terhadap PM ketika itu Najib Razak. Peguam Na’imah, Rajesh Nagarajan dan Sachpreetraj Singh, mengeluarkan kenyataan pada hari sama memanggil dakwaan Anwar “palsu, tidak masuk akal, keterlaluan, dan percubaan terang-terangan untuk mengelirukan Parlimen.”

🔥 Mengapa Ia Penting

Anwar menggunakan istilah paling berbaur dalam perbendaharaan politik Malaysia — “Zionis” — di bawah kekebalan parlimen, di mana beliau tidak boleh disaman. Ini bukan retorik sambil lewa. Di negara majoriti Islam di mana anti-Zionisme memikul berat emosi yang besar, menggunakan Zionis untuk memburukkan lawan politik adalah senjata sengaja sentimen awam. Ia buku panduan yang sama yang PH pernah tuduh UMNO dan PAS gunakan: menggunakan ketakutan dan identiti untuk mengalih perhatian daripada akauntabiliti institusi. Masa ia berlaku amat kritikal — Anwar sedang didesak mengenai dakwaan penyalahgunaan SPRM, pendedahan Bloomberg, dan desakan semakin kuat dari dalam gabungannya sendiri untuk reformasi sebenar. Naratif “komplot Zionis” membingkai semula tuntutan akauntabiliti dalam negeri sebagai sabotaj asing.

💩 Mengapa Ia Karut

Peguam Na’imah menyatakan dengan jelas: “Tiada satu perkataan pun tentang Zionisme atau Israel atau apa-apa berkaitan dengannya dalam keseluruhan pelan komunikasi.” Dokumen perhubungan awam itu, kata mereka, adalah untuk meyakinkan jurubank tentang kemampuan kewangan keluarga Daim — komunikasi krisis biasa bagi keluarga yang menghadapi rampasan aset. Anwar mereka-reka sudut Zionis dalam Parlimen, di mana beliau menikmati kekebalan undang-undang, dengan yakin beliau tidak akan mengulanginya di luar. Sementara itu, partinya sendiri melantik perunding asing untuk melobi terhadap perdana menteri Malaysia yang sedang memerintah pada 2016. Kemunafikan ini bukan halus — ia bersifat struktural. PKR melantik firma asing untuk menekan Putrajaya adalah strategi politik. Na’imah melantik firma perhubungan awam untuk melindungi hubungan perbankan keluarga adalah komplot Zionis. Bezanya? Satu melayani kepentingan Anwar. Yang satu lagi mengancamnya.

📜 Kes Terdahulu

Anwar mempunyai sejarah panjang menggunakan konspirasi apabila terdesak. Semasa krisis SPRM pada Februari-Mac 2026, beliau menuduh pengkritik sebagai sebahagian daripada komplot “golongan kaya” yang memanipulasi media. Beliau menolak desakan Bersih untuk akauntabiliti SPRM sebagai “tidak adil.” Beliau memberitahu orang ramai untuk tidak bertindak sebagai “pendakwa atau hakim” semasa krisis pembakaran KK Mart. Naratif “komplot asing” adalah pesongan autoritarian klasik — dan ia taktik sama yang digunakan Najib semasa skandal 1MDB, apabila beliau menyalahkan media asing, George Soros, dan sabotaj pembangkang atas pendedahan itu. Anwar menghabiskan sedekad mengejek buku panduan itu. Kini beliau menjalankannya.

🎯 Seruan Kepada PH

Anda berkempen atas ketelusan, akauntabiliti, dan reformasi institusi. Anda beritahu rakyat Malaysia bahawa zaman memfitnah pengkritik sebagai ejen asing sudah berakhir. Anda mengejek Najib kerana menyalahkan Soros. Anda mengejek Mahathir kerana menyalahkan “spekulator asing.” Dan kini, apabila peguam seorang balu menerbitkan dokumen perhubungan awam yang mengandungi sifar rujukan kepada Zionisme, anda berdiri di Parlimen — dilindungi kekebalan — dan menggunakan “Zionis” dan “jurubank” untuk menjatuhkan kredibilitinya. Anda tidak mempertahankan Malaysia daripada campur tangan asing. Anda menggunakan istilah paling sarat dalam leksikon politik negara untuk membungkam penelitian sah terhadap kelakuan kerajaan anda. Itu bukan reformasi. Itu bukan pusingan yang cekap. Itu politik era Najib dengan pelekat reformasi di atasnya.

[BM] Rang Undang-Undang Had Penggal PH Sendiri Gagal 2 Undi — 32 AP Tidak Hadir, 44 Berkecuali, Sifar Menentang

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister / Pakatan Harapan Coalition

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-03

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Pada 3 Mac 2026, parlimen Malaysia mengundi Rang Undang-Undang Perlembagaan (Pindaan) 2026, yang bakal menetapkan had dua penggal, 10 tahun untuk perdana menteri — salah satu janji reformasi utama Anwar Ibrahim sejak memegang jawatan pada November 2022. Rang undang-undang itu memerlukan majoriti dua pertiga sebanyak 148 undi. Ia mendapat 146. Yang Dipertua Dewan Tan Sri Johari Abdul mengumumkan keputusan: 146 menyokong, 32 tidak hadir, 44 berkecuali. Tiada seorang pun AP mengundi menentang rang undang-undang itu. Ia gagal kerana gabungan kerajaan sendiri tidak mampu menghadirkan ahlinya ke dewan.

🔥 Mengapa Ia Penting

Ini sepatutnya menjadi salah satu reformasi yang menentukan kerajaan Madani — semakan struktural terhadap kuasa eksekutif yang PH perjuangkan selama bertahun-tahun semasa pembangkang. Rang undang-undang itu tidak gagal kerana penentangan berprinsip. Ia gagal kerana 76 AP — hampir satu pertiga dewan — sama ada tidak hadir atau tidak bersusah payah mengambil pendirian. Dalam kerajaan yang menguasai majoriti besar, itu bukan nasib malang. Itu kegagalan pengurusan whip, kepimpinan, dan kehendak politik. Ia menandakan bahawa agenda reformasi PH bukan sekadar terbantut — ia dikhianati secara senyap dari dalam.

💩 Mengapa Ia Karut

Anwar Ibrahim menghabiskan tiga tahun memberitahu rakyat Malaysia bahawa reformasi memerlukan masa, gabungan itu kompleks, kesabaran diperlukan. Beliau mempunyai majoriti dua pertiga. Beliau telah menyediakan rang undang-undang, membentangkan, dan membahaskannya. Dan apabila tiba saat penting, ahlinya sendiri tidak hadir. Ini gabungan yang sama yang boleh menggerakkan seluruh bangku untuk undi keyakinan dan kelulusan belanjawan tetapi nampaknya tidak mampu berbuat demikian untuk reformasi demokrasi. Mesejnya jelas: mengehadkan kuasa perdana menteri bukan keutamaan bagi mereka yang mendapat manfaat daripada kuasa perdana menteri yang tidak terhad.

📜 Kes Terdahulu

Rekod PH mengenai reformasi struktural amat mengecewakan. Janji pemansuhan Akta Hasutan tidak pernah berlaku. Pelan untuk memisahkan kuasa pendakwaan Peguam Negara daripada kuasa pelantikan PM tidak ke mana. Cadangan untuk meliberalkan kemasukan universiti dengan mengiktiraf keputusan UEC telah ditangguhkan. Straits Times melaporkan bahawa DAP — parti terbesar PH — telah mempercepatkan kongres kebangsaan ke Julai 2026 khusus untuk menilai sama ada kadar reformasi membenarkan kekal dalam gabungan. Tony Pua, ketua disiplin DAP, memberi amaran bahawa “jika kita tidak berundur, parti akan kehilangan semua kredibiliti dan boleh jangkakan hukuman berat daripada pilihan raya.”

🎯 Seruan Kepada PH

Anda mempunyai angka. Anda mempunyai rang undang-undang. Anda mempunyai tiga tahun janji di belakang anda. Dan anda tidak mampu menghadirkan 148 orang ke dalam sebuah bilik. Pakatan Harapan memberitahu rakyat Malaysia pada 2022 bahawa undi untuk PH adalah undi untuk reformasi. Had penggal adalah Bukti A — bukti bahawa Malaysia Madani bermaksud perubahan struktural, bukan sekadar slogan. Pada 3 Mac 2026, di dalam dewan di mana tiada seorang AP pun mengundi tidak, reformasi mati kerana ketidakhadiran. Jika anda tidak mampu meluluskan reformasi tanda tangan anda sendiri dengan majoriti besar anda sendiri, apa sebenarnya gunanya anda?

Anwar Claims 'Zionist Plot' to Topple Government — Daim Family Sues, Calls It 'Abuse of Power'

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-03

📌 What Happened

On March 3, 2026, Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim told parliament that a suspect in an alleged plot to topple the government had engaged an international public relations firm to launch a coordinated attack aimed at undermining national institutions before the next general election. In a follow-up reported by FMT and Malay Mail, Anwar went further — claiming the alleged conspiracy involved “Zionists,” foreign lobbyist groups, bankers, and international media agencies, all driven by what he described as resistance to his government’s “mass scale” anti-corruption push. On March 6, the family of the late former Finance Minister Daim Zainuddin — whose widow Na’imah Abdul Khalid was widely identified as the investigation’s target — filed a lawsuit against Anwar, Inspector-General of Police Mohd Khalid Ismail, and the government, calling the probe “unlawful” and “tainted with bad faith.”

🔥 Why It Matters

This is not just a legal spat — it is the clearest sign yet that Anwar’s government is willing to deploy the language of existential threat and conspiracy to delegitimise political opponents and civil society scrutiny. By invoking “Zionists” — a loaded term in Malaysian politics — Anwar is borrowing from the same ethno-nationalist playbook he once condemned under Mahathir and Najib. The Daim family’s counter-lawsuit reframes the narrative: what the government calls national security, the targets call political persecution. This sets a dangerous precedent where any form of external engagement — hiring a PR firm, engaging media, consulting lobbyists — can be recast as subversion under Section 124B, which carries up to 20 years in prison.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

Anwar Ibrahim — the man who spent decades courting foreign media, international human rights organisations, and Western governments to champion his cause from prison — is now telling Malaysians that engaging a PR firm is tantamount to treason. The hypocrisy is industrial-grade. His own political survival was built on foreign advocacy and international pressure campaigns. Now that he’s in power, the same tools become evidence of a “Zionist” conspiracy. Na’imah Abdul Khalid publicly asked the obvious question: since when does hiring a PR firm constitute overthrowing a government? Anwar has no good answer, because the answer would expose the real purpose of this probe — intimidation.

📜 Previous Cases

Malaysia has a long history of prime ministers invoking shadowy foreign plots to silence critics. Mahathir Mohamad blamed “Jewish traders” and George Soros for the 1997 financial crisis. Najib Razak’s allies framed the 1MDB investigations as a foreign-backed attempt to destabilise Malaysia. Anwar himself was the target of politically motivated prosecutions under both Mahathir and Najib, with Sodomy I and II widely seen as tools of political persecution disguised as law enforcement. The “plot to topple” playbook is a staple of Malaysian authoritarian governance — and Anwar, despite all his reformist branding, is now running the same play from the other side of the desk.

🎯 PH Callout

You promised reformasi. You promised accountability. You promised Malaysia would never again see a government that criminalises dissent and weaponises law enforcement against political opponents. Three years in, your Prime Minister is standing in parliament invoking “Zionists” to explain why his government is being criticised — and your police are using Section 124B to investigate a widow for hiring a PR firm. This is not reform. This is the Najib playbook with better PR. If PH cannot tell the difference between legitimate political opposition and an actual coup, then PH has become exactly what it once fought against.

Anwar Invokes 'Zionists' and 'Bankers' in Parliament to Smear Daim's Widow — Lawyers Call It an 'Outright Lie'

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-03

📌 What Happened

On March 3, Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim told the Dewan Rakyat that the alleged plot to destabilise his government — attributed to Na’imah Abdul Khalid, the widow of former Finance Minister Daim Zainuddin — involved “prominent Zionist groups” and international bankers. He claimed a UK-based PR firm had been engaged to launch a coordinated attack on national institutions. This came one day after MalaysiaNow revealed that PKR leaders, including figures close to Anwar, had themselves engaged a US consultant in 2016 to build White House pressure on then-PM Najib Razak. Na’imah’s lawyers, Rajesh Nagarajan and Sachpreetraj Singh, issued a statement the same day calling Anwar’s claims “false, preposterous, outrageous, and a blatant attempt to mislead Parliament.”

🔥 Why It Matters

Anwar used the most incendiary term in Malaysian political vocabulary — “Zionist” — under parliamentary immunity, where he cannot be sued. This is not casual rhetoric. In a Muslim-majority country where anti-Zionism carries enormous emotional weight, invoking Zionists to discredit a political opponent is a deliberate weaponisation of public sentiment. It is the same playbook PH once accused UMNO and PAS of running: using fear and identity to distract from institutional accountability. The timing is critical — Anwar is under pressure over the MACC abuse allegations, Bloomberg exposés, and growing calls from within his own coalition for real reform. The “Zionist plot” narrative reframes domestic accountability demands as foreign sabotage.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

Na’imah’s lawyers stated unequivocally: “There is not one word about Zionism or Israel or anything related to it in the entire communications plan.” The PR document, they said, was about reassuring bankers of the Daim family’s financial viability — standard crisis communications for a family facing asset seizure. Anwar fabricated the Zionist angle in Parliament, where he enjoys legal immunity, knowing full well he would not repeat it outside. Meanwhile, his own party engaged foreign consultants to lobby against a sitting Malaysian prime minister in 2016. The hypocrisy is not subtle — it is structural. PKR engaging foreign firms to pressure Putrajaya was political strategy. Na’imah engaging a PR firm to protect her family’s banking relationships is a Zionist plot. The difference? One serves Anwar’s interests. The other threatens them.

📜 Previous Cases

Anwar has a long history of invoking conspiracy when cornered. During the MACC crisis in February-March 2026, he accused critics of being part of a plot by “wealthy elites” manipulating media. He dismissed Bersih’s call for MACC accountability as “unfair.” He told the public not to act as “prosecutor or judge” during the KK Mart firebombing crisis. The “foreign plot” narrative is a classic authoritarian deflection — and it is the exact tactic Najib used during the 1MDB scandal, when he blamed foreign media, George Soros, and opposition sabotage for the exposés. Anwar spent a decade mocking that playbook. Now he is running it.

🎯 PH Callout

You campaigned on transparency, accountability, and institutional reform. You told Malaysians that the days of smearing critics as foreign agents were over. You mocked Najib for blaming Soros. You mocked Mahathir for blaming “foreign speculators.” And now, when a widow’s lawyers publish a PR document that contains zero references to Zionism, you stand in Parliament — protected by immunity — and invoke “Zionists” and “bankers” to discredit her. You are not defending Malaysia from foreign interference. You are using the most loaded term in the country’s political lexicon to shut down legitimate scrutiny of your government’s conduct. That is not reform. That is not even competent spin. That is Najib-era politics with a reformasi sticker on it.

PH's Own Term Limit Bill Falls 2 Votes Short — 32 MPs Absent, 44 Abstained, Zero Voted Against

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister / Pakatan Harapan Coalition

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-03

📌 What Happened

On March 3, 2026, Malaysia’s parliament voted on the Constitution (Amendment) Bill 2026, which would have instituted a two-term, 10-year limit for prime ministers — one of Anwar Ibrahim’s flagship reform promises since taking office in November 2022. The bill needed a two-thirds supermajority of 148 votes. It got 146. House Speaker Tan Sri Johari Abdul announced the results: 146 in favor, 32 absent, 44 abstained. Not a single MP voted against the bill. It failed because the government’s own coalition could not get its members into the chamber.

🔥 Why It Matters

This was supposed to be one of the defining reforms of the Madani government — a structural check on executive power that PH campaigned on for years while in opposition. The bill didn’t fail because of principled opposition. It failed because 76 MPs — nearly a third of the house — were either absent or couldn’t be bothered to take a position. In a government that commands a supermajority, that is not bad luck. That is a failure of whipping, leadership, and political will. It signals that PH’s reform agenda is not just stalled — it is being quietly sabotaged from within.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

Anwar Ibrahim has spent three years telling Malaysians that reforms take time, that the coalition is complex, that patience is required. He has a two-thirds majority. He had the bill drafted, tabled, and debated. And when the moment came, his own people didn’t show up. This is the same coalition that can mobilize its full bench for confidence votes and budget approvals but apparently cannot do the same for democratic reform. The message is clear: limiting prime ministerial power is not a priority for the people who benefit from unlimited prime ministerial power.

📜 Previous Cases

PH’s record on structural reform is dismal. The promised repeal of the Sedition Act never happened. The plan to separate the Attorney-General’s prosecutorial power from the PM’s appointment authority has gone nowhere. The proposal to liberalize university admissions by recognizing UEC results has been shelved. 🎯 PH Callout

You had the numbers. You had the bill. You had three years of promises behind you. And you couldn’t get 148 people into a room. On March 3, 2026, in a chamber where not a single MP voted no, reformasi died of absenteeism. If you cannot pass your own signature reforms with your own supermajority, what exactly is the point of you?

Cukup adil. Tetapi rakyat juga tidak boleh lagi menelan hujah anda.

[BM] Gabungan Anwar Gagal Luluskan Rang Undang-Undang Reformasi Sendiri — Kalah Undi Had Penggal PM Dengan Dua Undi, Janji 'Cuba Lagi Pada Jun'

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister / Pakatan Harapan Coalition

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-02

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Pada 2 Mac, kerajaan perpaduan Anwar Ibrahim gagal meluluskan pindaan perlembagaan yang akan mengehadkan penggal Perdana Menteri kepada 10 tahun — janji reformasi utama manifesto PH pada PRU 2022. Rang undang-undang itu mendapat 146 undi, hanya dua kurang daripada 148 yang diperlukan untuk majoriti dua pertiga. Empat puluh empat AP berkecuali dan 32 tidak hadir. Timbalan Speaker Alice Lau sahkan lapan AP kerajaan dan bebas tidak mengundi. Kerajaan kini berjanji membentangkan semula pada Jun, dengan pemimpin gabungan menjanjikan AP mereka akan “benar-benar hadir kali ini.”

🔥 Kenapa Ini Penting

Ini sepatutnya kemenangan mudah. Had penggal PM popular, sarat simbolik, dan selamat dari segi politik — direka untuk buktikan PH masih mampu laksanakan reformasi. Sebaliknya, gabungan Anwar yang memegang supermajoriti dalam Dewan Rakyat 222 kerusi tidak mampu pastikan ahlinya sendiri hadir mengundi. Kegagalan ini dedahkan kerosakan lebih dalam: tiga tahun di Putrajaya, PH tidak mampu terjemahkan angka parlimen kepada hasil perundangan. Kalau gabungan ini gagal luluskan rang undang-undang yang dipromosikan berminggu-minggu, apa harapan untuk reformasi lebih sukar — penguatkuasaan anti-lompat, IPCMC, ketelusan perolehan, atau kebebasan kehakiman?

💩 Kenapa Ini Karut

Kerajaan dengan supermajoriti yang kalah undi rang undang-undang reformasi sendiri dengan dua undi bukan nasib malang — ini pengurusan teruk. Sama ada whip parti gagal buat kerja, atau sebahagian gabungan diam-diam sabotaj undian. Seorang AP PH tanpa nama beritahu SCMP: “Rang undang-undang itu sepatutnya mudah lulus. Rasa macam usaha tidak cukup untuk pastikan semua hadir.” Itu bukan penjelasan — itu pengakuan. Dan jawapan kerajaan? “Kami akan cuba lagi pada Jun.” Itu reformasi versi tekan butang snooze. Sementara itu, kamera tangkap Anwar ketawa selepas keputusan undi dibaca, berbual dengan timbalannya seolah-olah rang undang-undang utamanya tidak baru sahaja mati di lantai dewan.

📜 Kes-Kes Sebelum Ini

Ini satu corak. PH janji IPCMC — ditangguh. Janji kebebasan maklumat — tiada. Janji penguatkuasaan undang-undang anti-lompat — terpilih sahaja. Janji reformasi pembiayaan politik — terperangkap dalam jawatankuasa. Manifesto 2022 setebal 60 muka surat; tiga tahun kemudian, perkara paling sukar masih tidak disentuh dan yang mudah pun tergagap. Had penggal PM secara literal buah paling rendah — popular merentas parti, disokong sendiri oleh Anwar, dan hanya perlu supermajoriti yang PH sudah ada di atas kertas.

🎯 Panggilan Kepada PH

Kamu kata kamu berbeza. Kamu lari atas platform “Reformasi.” Kamu ada angka, mandat, dan sokongan rakyat. Dan kamu tak mampu pastikan AP sendiri masuk dewan untuk undi rang undang-undang yang kamu jual kepada negara berminggu-minggu. Kalau kamu tak boleh luluskan reformasi popular dan simbolik dengan supermajoriti, untuk apa sebenarnya majoriti itu? “Kami akan cuba lagi pada Jun” bukan agenda reformasi — itu pengakuan kamu tak ada kawalan ke atas gabungan sendiri.
