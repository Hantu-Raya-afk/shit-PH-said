Zafrul Testifying in Muhyiddin Corruption Trial — The Man Who Served Both Sides Takes the Stand

Said by: Tengku Zafrul Abdul Aziz / Muhyiddin Yassin Defence Team

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-09

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: Tengku Zafrul is scheduled to testify in the corruption trial of former PM Muhyiddin Yassin, who faces charges of soliciting RM232.5 million in bribes for Bersatu through the Jana Wibawa programme. Zafrul served as Muhyiddin’s Finance Minister during the period the alleged offences occurred (March 2020 to August 2021). The defence team has also signalled plans to call tycoon Syed Mokhtar to testify. Hearing dates are set through August 2026.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: Zafrul was Finance Minister when the Jana Wibawa contracts were allegedly used as a bribery vehicle. He is now simultaneously Anwar’s Senior Political Advisor and a witness in the trial of the PM whose government he served. The potential for conflicts of interest is staggering: the witness serves the current government, which has political interest in the conviction of the former PM. His testimony could be shaped — or perceived as shaped — by his current loyalties.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: In any other democracy, a senior government advisor being a key witness in a former boss’s corruption trial would trigger recusal protocols, independent counsel considerations, or at minimum a temporary leave from government duties. In Malaysia, nobody bats an eye. Zafrul will testify about financial decisions he oversaw as FM, then walk back to his office as Anwar’s advisor. The institutional boundaries that should prevent this don’t exist.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: Malaysia’s political-judicial overlap has a long, ugly history. The 1988 judicial crisis, Anwar’s own trials, and the Zahid DNAA all demonstrate what happens when political interests contaminate legal proceedings. Zafrul’s dual role as current advisor and trial witness is the latest chapter.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: Your senior advisor served as finance minister to the man you’re prosecuting. He oversaw the budget during the alleged corruption period. He is now your closest economic advisor. Malaysians are supposed to believe his testimony won’t be influenced by his current employer? Either put Zafrul on leave during the trial or explain how this isn’t a textbook conflict of interest.

[BM] Anwar Dakwa Lobi PKR Di AS Pada 2016 'Hanya Soal Pembebasannya' — Dosier 84 Muka Surat Tunjuk Ia Desak Washington Campuri Urusan Malaysia

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister / Nurul Izzah Anwar, PKR Deputy President / Sivarasa Rasiah, PKR Senior Leader

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-08

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Pada 8 Mac, MalaysiaNow mendedahkan kandungan dosier 84 muka surat bertajuk White Paper on the case of Anwar Ibrahim: Citizen of Malaysia v. Government of Malaysia, yang disediakan oleh pemimpin kanan PKR Sivarasa Rasiah bersama konsultan Amerika Jared Genser dari Perseus Strategies pada April 2016. Dokumen ini, yang disahkan seorang Ahli Parlimen kanan PKR telah dihantar kepada beberapa ahli Kongres AS, mendesak pentadbiran Obama untuk lebih campur tangan dalam politik Malaysia, menegur kerajaan Najib secara terbuka, dan menuntut pembebasan Anwar. Anwar sebelum ini mendakwa pengambilan Genser oleh PKR adalah “semata-mata untuk mendesak pembebasannya dari penjara.”

🔥 Kenapa Ini Penting

Pendedahan ini bersifat letupan dari segi politik kerana kerajaan Anwar kini sedang mendakwa keluarga mendiang Daim Zainuddin — khususnya balu beliau Na’imah Khalid — di bawah undang-undang berkaitan “menjatuhkan kerajaan” kerana melibatkan firma PR asing. Polis melancarkan siasatan di bawah Seksyen 124B terhadap Na’imah berdasarkan dakwaan bahawa kerja komunikasi beliau adalah sebahagian daripada komplot untuk menumbangkan Anwar. Namun PKR melakukan perkara yang sama tepat pada 2016: mengupah konsultan asing untuk melobi kerajaan asing agar menekan Perdana Menteri Malaysia yang sedang memerintah. Dosier itu jauh melampaui kes peribadi Anwar — ia menyerang tadbir urus, badan kehakiman, ekonomi, dan hala tuju dasar Malaysia merentasi 84 muka surat, dan dihantar terus kepada ahli-ahli Kongres AS.

💩 Kenapa Ini Karut

Dakwaan Anwar bahawa lobi 2016 itu “hanya tentang pembebasannya” runtuh di bawah berat dokumen itu sendiri. Dosier itu menyerang tadbir urus Najib dari setiap sudut — ekonomi, badan kehakiman, kebebasan akhbar, dan hala tuju dasar. Ia mendesak Presiden AS untuk menegur secara terbuka sebuah kerajaan berdaulat. Nurul Izzah menulis artikel bertajuk What Obama must do in Malaysia, menuduh Najib meninggalkan negara dengan “peningkatan perbalahan perkauman, ketidakseimbangan ekonomi dan suasana subur untuk pertumbuhan ekstremisme.” Genser menuduh Najib melakukan “gerrymandering melampau” dan mengekalkan kuasa melalui dasar-dasar yang memecahbelahkan. Ini bukan kempen tahanan hati nurani — ia adalah operasi politik menyeluruh yang direka untuk mendapatkan Washington bagi menjejaskan kestabilan kerajaan yang sedang memerintah. Perbuatan yang sama yang kini Anwar panggil pengkhianatan apabila dilakukan lawannya.

📜 Kes-Kes Sebelum Ini

Parti politik Malaysia mempunyai sejarah panjang meminta sokongan asing apabila menjadi pembangkang, kemudian menangis kedaulatan apabila berkuasa. Anwar sendiri memupuk hubungan rapat dengan badan pemikir Washington, media Barat, dan pertubuhan hak asasi antarabangsa semasa menjadi ketua pembangkang selama bertahun-tahun. Kemenangan PH pada PRU 2018 sebahagiannya dibantu oleh tekanan antarabangsa terhadap Najib berhubung 1MDB. Kini berkuasa, Anwar telah mempersenjatakan taktik yang sama terhadap keluarga Daim, mengerahkan siasatan polis di bawah undang-undang era hasutan dan secara terbuka mendakwa “Zionis” dan “jurubank” berada di sebalik rancangan PR Na’imah. Polanya setua politik Malaysia: lobi pembangkang adalah “advokasi untuk demokrasi,” tetapi saat anda pegang kuasa, ia menjadi “komplot untuk menjatuhkan kerajaan.”

🎯 Panggilan Kepada PH

Anda berkempen atas nama ketelusan, kedaulatan undang-undang, dan hak untuk bersuara. Parti anda sendiri mengupah konsultan asing untuk meminta kuasa besar campur tangan dalam politik Malaysia — lengkap dengan dosier 84 muka surat yang menyerang tadbir urus, badan kehakiman, dan hala tuju dasar negara anda sendiri, dihantar terus kepada ahli Kongres AS. Anda kini mendakwa seorang balu tua kerana mengupah firma PR, mendakwa “Zionis” berada di sebalik komplot untuk menumbangkan anda, dan menggunakan Seksyen 124B terhadap aktivis antirasuah dan masyarakat sivil. Jika melibatkan konsultan asing untuk menekan kerajaan Malaysia adalah jenayah terhadap demokrasi berparlimen, mulakan dengan parti anda sendiri. Jika tidak, gugurkan pertuduhan terhadap Na’imah Khalid dan berhenti menggunakan polis untuk menakut-nakutkan pengkritik anda.

[BM] PKR Keluarkan Surat Tunjuk Sebab Kepada Rafizi, Kemudian Berundur — Parti Tak Mampu Disiplinkan Pengkritik Paling Terkenalnya

Said by: Rafizi Ramli, Pandan MP / Fuziah Salleh, PKR Secretary-General

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-08

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Pada 19 Februari, AP Pandan Rafizi Ramli mendedahkan beliau menerima surat tunjuk sebab daripada Setiausaha Agung PKR Fuziah Salleh selepas memberitahu Sin Chew Daily beliau akan bertanding di Pandan pada PRU16 tetapi berkemungkinan bukan sebagai calon PKR. 🔥 Kenapa Ia Penting

Episod ini mendedahkan kereputan institusi dalam PKR — parti yang pernah melaungkan dirinya sebagai barisan hadapan reformasi. Ketidakmampuan PKR sama ada melibatkan kritikannya secara bermakna atau menguatkuasakan mekanisme disiplinnya sendiri menunjukkan parti yang lumpuh — terlalu takut memecat pengkritik paling terkenalnya, tetapi terlalu sombong untuk mengakui dia ada betulnya.

💩 Kenapa Ia Karut

PKR mahu Rafizi pergi tetapi tiada keberanian melakukannya. Seperti yang Rafizi sendiri tunjukkan, memecatnya tidak akan menjejaskan kerusi parlimennya — hanya peletakan jawatan sukarela akan mencetuskan kekosongan. Jadi PKR terperangkap: pecat dia dan dia kekal di Parlimen sebagai agen bebas yang memalukan mereka setiap hari, atau berundur dan kelihatan lemah. Mereka pilih lemah. “Peringatan ikut peraturan parti” adalah setara politik nota Post-it pasif-agresif. Ini parti yang sama yang memberi kuliah negara tentang reformasi institusi dan tadbir urus baik sementara ia tidak mampu menguruskan proses disiplin dalaman asas tanpa tunduk di bawah tekanan.

📜 Kes Terdahulu

PKR mempunyai sejarah panjang dan tidak membanggakan pembersihan puak yang berselindung di sebalik disiplin — dan disiplin yang runtuh menjadi komedi. Parti memecat timbalan presidennya sendiri Azmin Ali pada 2020 selepas Langkah Sheraton, tetapi hanya selepas kerosakan berlaku. Rafizi sendiri pernah menjadi anak emas PKR — pemberi maklumat yang mendedahkan skandal Perbadanan Fidlot Nasional. Kini beliau suara parti yang paling menyusahkan.

🎯 Panggilan PH

Anwar dan PKR menjanjikan rakyat Malaysia politik baru — telus, berprinsip, dan bertanggungjawab. Tetapi apabila bekas timbalan presiden anda sendiri secara terbuka mengatakan beliau tidak akan bertanding di bawah bendera anda, dan yang terbaik parti anda mampu hasilkan ialah surat tunjuk sebab diikuti emel peringatan yang lemah, anda bukan menjalankan parti politik — anda menguruskan situasi tebusan. ‘Reformasi’ pernah bermakna sesuatu. Kini ia bermakna menghantar emel bernada tegas dan berharap masalah hilang sendiri.

Anwar Claims PKR's 2016 US Lobbying Was 'Only About His Release' — 84-Page Dossier Shows It Called for Washington to Intervene Against Malaysia

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister / Nurul Izzah Anwar, PKR Deputy President / Sivarasa Rasiah, PKR Senior Leader

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-08

📌 What Happened

On March 8, MalaysiaNow published details of an 84-page dossier titled White Paper on the case of Anwar Ibrahim: Citizen of Malaysia v. Government of Malaysia, prepared jointly by PKR’s Sivarasa Rasiah and American consultant Jared Genser of Perseus Strategies in April 2016. The document, which a senior PKR MP confirmed was delivered to several US Congress members, called on the Obama administration to take a greater interest in Malaysia’s domestic politics, publicly rebuke the Najib government, and demand Anwar’s release. Anwar had previously stated that PKR’s hiring of Genser was “solely to press for his release from prison.”

🔥 Why It Matters

The revelation is politically explosive because Anwar’s government is currently prosecuting the family of the late Daim Zainuddin — specifically his widow Na’imah Khalid — under laws related to “toppling the government” for engaging a foreign PR firm. Police launched a probe under Section 124B against Na’imah based on claims that her communications work was part of a plot to overthrow Anwar. Yet PKR did precisely the same thing in 2016: hired a foreign consultant to lobby a foreign government to apply pressure on the sitting Malaysian prime minister. The dossier went far beyond Anwar’s personal case — it attacked Malaysia’s governance, judiciary, economy, and policy direction across 84 pages, and was hand-delivered to US lawmakers.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

Anwar’s claim that the 2016 lobbying was “only about his release” collapses under the weight of the document itself. The dossier attacked Najib’s governance across every front — economy, judiciary, press freedom, and policy direction. It called for the US president to publicly rebuke a sovereign government. Nurul Izzah wrote an op-ed titled What Obama must do in Malaysia, accusing Najib of leaving the country with “increased sectarian strife, economic imbalances and a ripe environment for the growth of extremism.” Genser accused Najib of “extreme gerrymandering” and maintaining power through divisive policies. This was not a prisoner-of-conscience campaign — it was a comprehensive political operation designed to get Washington to destabilize the sitting government. The same act Anwar now calls treason when done by his opponents.

📜 Previous Cases

Malaysian political parties have a long history of courting foreign backing when in opposition, then crying sovereignty when in power. Anwar himself cultivated deep ties with Washington think tanks, Western media, and international human rights organizations during his years as opposition leader. PH’s 2018 election win was partly aided by international pressure on Najib over 1MDB. Now in government, Anwar has weaponized the exact same tactics against the Daim family, deploying police investigations under sedition-era laws and publicly claiming “Zionists” and “bankers” were behind Na’imah’s PR plan. The pattern is as old as Malaysian politics: opposition lobbying is “advocacy for democracy,” but the moment you hold power, it becomes “a plot to topple the government.”

🎯 PH Callout

You campaigned on transparency, rule of law, and the right to dissent. Your own party hired a foreign consultant to ask a superpower to intervene in Malaysian politics — complete with an 84-page dossier attacking your country’s governance, judiciary, and policy direction, hand-delivered to US Congress members. You now prosecute an elderly widow for hiring a PR firm, claim “Zionists” are behind a plot to overthrow you, and use Section 124B against anti-corruption activists and civil society. If engaging foreign consultants to pressure a Malaysian government is a crime against parliamentary democracy, then start with your own party. If it isn’t, drop the charges against Na’imah Khalid and stop using the police to intimidate your critics.

On March 8, Rafizi publicly described this as a “U-turn,” saying the show-cause letter was issued “with the intention to give him the boot” but the party ultimately blinked.

🔥 Why It Matters

This episode exposes the institutional rot inside PKR — the party that once styled itself as the vanguard of reformasi. A show-cause letter is supposed to be the start of a disciplinary process, not a performative threat followed by a quiet retreat. Rafizi has been openly critical of Anwar and the PKR leadership on podcasts, in media interviews, and on social media for weeks. PKR’s inability to either meaningfully engage with his criticisms or enforce its own disciplinary mechanisms shows a party that is paralysed — too afraid to sack its most prominent critic, but too proud to admit he has a point.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

PKR wanted Rafizi gone but didn’t have the spine to do it. As Rafizi himself pointed out, expelling him wouldn’t cost him his parliamentary seat — only a voluntary resignation would trigger a vacancy. So PKR was trapped: sack him and he stays in Parliament as a free agent embarrassing them daily, or back down and look weak. They chose weak. The “reminder to follow party rules” is the political equivalent of a passive-aggressive Post-it note. This is the same party that lectures the nation about institutional reform and good governance while it cannot even manage a basic internal disciplinary process without folding under pressure.

📜 Previous Cases

PKR has a long and inglorious history of factional purges disguised as discipline — and discipline that collapses into farce. The party expelled its own deputy president Azmin Ali in 2020 after the Sheraton Move, but only after the damage was done. Before that, leaders like Zuraida Kamaruddin and Baru Bian walked out with minimal consequences. Rafizi himself was once PKR’s golden boy — the whistleblower who exposed the National Feedlot Corporation scandal. Now he is the party’s most inconvenient voice, and PKR’s playbook remains the same: threaten, posture, and then quietly pretend nothing happened when the political cost of following through becomes clear.

🎯 PH Callout

Anwar and PKR promised Malaysians a new politics — transparent, principled, and accountable. But when your own former deputy president publicly says he won’t contest under your banner, and the best your party can muster is a show-cause letter followed by a meek email reminder, you are not running a political party — you are managing a hostage situation. If PKR cannot enforce discipline on a single MP who has openly challenged the president, how does it expect the public to believe it can enforce standards on an entire government? “Reformasi” used to mean something. Now it means sending sternly worded emails and hoping the problem goes away.
