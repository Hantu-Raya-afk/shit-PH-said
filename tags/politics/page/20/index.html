[BM] Kad Laporan Tiga Tahun Anwar: Ekonomi Stabil, Janji Dimungkiri, Kredibiliti Reformasi Runtuh

Said by: Multiple analysts and media

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-12-01

📌 APA YANG BERLAKU: Ketika Anwar Ibrahim menandakan tiga tahun sebagai Perdana Menteri pada akhir 2025, pelbagai penganalisis dan media menyampaikan keputusan yang konsisten: ekonomi stabil, kredibiliti reformasi hancur. Pertumbuhan KDNK melebihi 5%, pelaburan asing mencecah rekod tertinggi, dan pengangguran mencapai paras terendah dalam satu dekad. Tetapi dalam reformasi — sebab sebenar PH dipilih — rekodnya amat mengecewakan: Akta Hasutan diperluaskan dengan 90 tangkapan, AUKU kekal utuh, ICCPR tidak diratifikasi, pemisahan PG-PP dihantar ke jawatankuasa selepas tiga tahun kelewatan, 47 pertuduhan rasuah Zahid digugurkan, SPRM diserang kerana politisasi, seorang wartawan ditangkap kerana bertanya soalan, dan Nurul Izzah dipasang sebagai No. 2 PKR.

🔥 KENAPA INI PENTING: Tiga tahun bukan “hari-hari awal.” Ia lebih separuh penggal parlimen. Jika reformasi akan datang, ia sudah sepatutnya bermula sekarang. Sebaliknya, apa yang Malaysia ada ialah kerajaan yang melaksanakan pengurusan ekonomi yang cekap sambil secara sistematik meninggalkan janji demokratik yang membawanya berkuasa. Pertukaran itu kini jelas: kamu dapat pertumbuhan KDNK, tetapi kamu kehilangan kebebasan sivil, kemerdekaan institusi, dan akauntabiliti.

💩 KENAPA INI KARUT: Pembelaan PH sentiasa “kami mewarisi keadaan huru-hara” dan “beri kami masa.” Tetapi mereka sudah ada masa — lebih masa daripada yang PH 1.0 pernah dapat. Akta Hasutan bukan sahaja dibiarkan dalam buku undang-undang — ia dipersenjatakan lebih agresif daripada di bawah Najib. AUKU bukan sahaja dikekalkan — pelajar yang membantah diabaikan. Pemisahan PG-PP bukan sahaja ditangguhkan — ia dibentangkan dan kemudian segera dihantar ke jawatankuasa untuk mati senyap. Setiap janji reformasi utama telah sama ada ditinggalkan, ditangguhkan, atau diterbalikkan.

📜 KES TERDAHULU: PH 1.0 (2018-2020) mempunyai 22 bulan dan mencapai reformasi separa sebelum runtuh melalui Langkah Sheraton. PH 2.0 mempunyai 36 bulan dengan majoriti lebih besar dan mencapai lebih sedikit reformasi.

🎯 SERUAN PH: Kamu dipilih atas reformasi. Kamu menyampaikan ekonomi. Itu bukan perkara yang sama. BN juga menyampaikan ekonomi — selama berdekad — sambil melumpuhkan institusi. Kamu kini melakukan perkara yang sama dengan PR yang lebih baik. Mansuhkan Akta Hasutan. Mansuhkan AUKU. Ratifikasi ICCPR. Pisahkan PG dan PP. Pulihkan kemerdekaan SPRM. Atau berhenti berpura-pura kamu berbeza daripada orang yang kamu gantikan.

Tetapi Zafrul telah menaikkannya menjadi satu seni. Keterangannya dalam perbicaraan Muhyiddin sambil berkhidmat sebagai penasihat Anwar mewujudkan konflik yang akan mendiskualifikasi dalam kebanyakan negara demokrasi.

Jika kerajaan Muhyiddin tidak sah, kenapa Menteri Kewangan Muhyiddin menjadi penasihat ekonomi paling dipercayai kamu? Jawapannya: prinsip adalah untuk kempen; kuasa adalah untuk tadbir urus.

Anwar's Three-Year Report Card: Steady Economics, Broken Promises, Collapsing Reform Credibility

Said by: Multiple analysts and media

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-12-01

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: As Anwar Ibrahim marked three years as Prime Minister in late 2025, multiple analysts and media outlets delivered a consistent verdict: steady economics, shattered reform credibility. GDP growth exceeded 5%, foreign investment hit record highs, and unemployment reached decade lows. But on reform — the actual reason PH was elected — the record is dismal: the Sedition Act was expanded with 90 arrests, UUCA remains intact, ICCPR is unratified, AG-PP separation was sent to committee after three years of delay, Zahid’s 47 corruption charges were dropped, MACC is under fire for politicisation, a journalist was arrested for asking a question, and Nurul Izzah was installed as PKR No. 2.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: Three years is not “early days.” It is more than half a parliamentary term. If reform was coming, it would have started by now. Instead, what Malaysia has is a government that performs competent economic management while systematically abandoning the democratic promises that brought it to power. The trade-off is now explicit: you get GDP growth, but you lose civil liberties, institutional independence, and accountability. The question is no longer “will Anwar reform?” The question is: was reform ever the goal?

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: PH’s defence is always “we inherited a mess” and “give us time.” But they’ve had time — more time than PH 1.0 ever got. The Sedition Act wasn’t just left on the books — it was weaponised more aggressively than under Najib. UUCA wasn’t just kept — students who protested were ignored. The AG-PP separation wasn’t just delayed — it was tabled and then immediately sent to a committee to die quietly. Every single flagship reform promise has been either abandoned, delayed, or reversed. Economic competence is not a substitute for democratic accountability — it’s what authoritarian governments use to justify staying in power.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: PH 1.0 (2018-2020) had 22 months and accomplished partial reforms before collapsing via the Sheraton Move. PH 2.0 has had 36 months with a larger majority and accomplished fewer reforms. Mahathir’s first tenure showed how economic success can coexist with democratic regression for decades. The pattern is clear: Malaysian PMs use economic performance as a shield against reform demands, and it works — until it doesn’t.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: You were elected on reform. You delivered economics. Those are not the same thing. BN also delivered economics — for decades — while gutting institutions. You are now doing the same thing with better PR. Repeal the Sedition Act. Abolish UUCA. Ratify ICCPR. Separate the AG and PP. Restore MACC independence. Or stop pretending you’re different from the people you replaced — because after three years, the only difference is the slogan.

[BM] PH Menang 1 Daripada 20 Kerusi Di Sabah — Anwar Kata Kekalahan Takkan Henti Penyampaian

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-11-30

📌 APA YANG BERLAKU: Pada 30 November 2025, Pakatan Harapan dihancurkan dalam pilihan raya negeri Sabah. Gabungan itu memenangi hanya 1 daripada 20 kerusi yang dipertandingkan — turun daripada 7 sebelumnya. Itu kadar kekalahan 95%. Parti-parti berasaskan Sabah mendominasi, didorong sentimen anti-persekutuan dan tuntutan autonomi negeri yang lebih besar — terutama selepas keputusan Mahkamah Tinggi menyokong tuntutan Sabah terhadap 40% hasil persekutuan. Penganalisis politik Lee Kuok Tiung berkata terus terang: “Keputusan pilihan raya Sabah menunjukkan gabungan Anwar kalah teruk.” Jawapan Anwar: kekalahan itu “takkan menghalang penyampaian” janji kepada Sabah.

🔥 MENGAPA IA PENTING: Ini adalah ujian pilihan raya pertama menjelang PRU16 — dan PH gagal dengan teruk. Rakyat Sabah bukan sekadar menolak PH — mereka menolak keseluruhan idea ditadbir oleh parti berasaskan Semenanjung. Keputusan ini mendedahkan jurang asas: keutamaan Putrajaya tidak bergema di Malaysia Timur. Anwar merayu keputusan Mahkamah Tinggi mengenai bahagian hasil Sabah, terus menentang pengundi yang diperlukan. Apabila anda melawan tuntutan undang-undang sesebuah negeri terhadap sumbernya sendiri dan kemudian meminta negeri yang sama mengundi untuk anda, hasilnya boleh dijangka.

💩 MENGAPA IA KARUT: Jawapan Anwar — “kekalahan takkan henti penyampaian” — adalah seperti kalah perlawanan 19-1 dan tetap berkeras program latihan sedang berkesan. Jika PH tidak boleh memenangi kerusi di Sabah, ia tidak boleh mendakwa mandat untuk mentadbir Sabah. Janji untuk “menyampaikan” kepada negeri yang menolak anda secara besar-besaran bukan jaminan — ia pekak telinga. Rakyat Sabah memberitahu PH tepat apa yang mereka fikir. Jawapan PH adalah mengabaikan mesej itu dan mengulangi retorik yang sama. Itu bukan ketabahan; itu penafian.

📜 KES TERDAHULU: Masalah PH di Malaysia Timur bukan baharu. Dalam PRU15, PH memenangi kerusi minimum di Sabah dan Sarawak, bergantung kepada GPS dan GRS untuk bilangan gabungan. Coraknya konsisten: parti Semenanjung melayan Malaysia Timur sebagai bank undi dan bukan rakan setara. BN melakukan ini selama berdekad. PH berjanji akan berbeza — semakan Perjanjian Malaysia 1963, penurunan kuasa, pembahagian hasil. Tiga tahun kemudian, Sabah mendapat keputusan mahkamah memihak kepadanya dan PH merayunya. Pengajaran dari setiap pilihan raya Sabah sama: hormati autonomi negeri atau kalah.

🎯 SERUAN PH: Anda menang 1 daripada 20 kerusi. Satu. Rakyat Sabah memberikan mesej paling jelas: anda tidak mewakili mereka. Daripada mendengar, anda kata “kekalahan takkan henti penyampaian.” Tarik rayuan terhadap keputusan bahagian hasil Sabah. Laksanakan komitmen MA63. Turunkan kuasa sebenar kepada negeri-negeri Malaysia Timur. Atau terus menang 1 daripada 20 dan berpura-pura semuanya baik — kerana PRU16 akan memberikan keputusan sama jika tiada perubahan.

PH Wins 1 Out of 20 Seats in Sabah — Anwar Says Defeat Won't Stop Delivery

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-11-30

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: On 30 November 2025, Pakatan Harapan was demolished in the Sabah state election. The coalition won just 1 out of 20 seats it contested — down from 7 previously. That is a 95% loss rate. Sabah-based parties dominated, driven by anti-federal sentiment and demands for greater state autonomy — particularly after a High Court ruling supporting Sabah’s claim to 40% of federal revenue. Political analyst Lee Kuok Tiung said plainly: “The Sabah election outcome shows Anwar’s coalition lost badly.” Anwar’s response: the defeat “won’t stop delivery” of pledges to Sabah.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: This was the first electoral test ahead of GE16 — and PH failed it catastrophically. Sabahans didn’t just reject PH — they rejected the entire idea of being governed by Peninsular-based parties. The results exposed a fundamental disconnect: Putrajaya’s priorities do not resonate in East Malaysia. Anwar appealed the High Court ruling on Sabah’s revenue share, directly antagonising the voters he needed. When you fight a state’s legal claim to its own resources and then ask the same state to vote for you, the outcome is predictable.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: Anwar’s response — “defeat won’t stop delivery” — is the political equivalent of losing a match 19-1 and insisting the training programme is working. If PH cannot win seats in Sabah, it cannot claim a mandate to govern Sabah. The promise to “deliver” to a state that overwhelmingly rejected you is not reassurance — it’s tone-deafness. Sabahans told PH exactly what they thought. PH’s response was to ignore the message and repeat the same platitudes. That’s not resilience; it’s denial.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: PH’s East Malaysia problem is not new. In GE15, PH won minimal seats in Sabah and Sarawak, relying on GPS and GRS for coalition numbers. The pattern is consistent: Peninsular parties treat East Malaysia as a vote bank rather than an equal partner. BN did this for decades. PH promised to be different — the Malaysia Agreement 1963 review, devolution of powers, revenue sharing. Three years later, Sabah got a court ruling in its favour and PH appealed it. The lesson from every Sabah election is the same: respect state autonomy or lose.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: You won 1 out of 20 seats. One. Sabahans gave you the clearest possible message: you do not represent them. Instead of listening, you said “defeat won’t stop delivery.” Drop the appeal against Sabah’s revenue share ruling. Implement MA63 commitments. Devolve real power to East Malaysian states. Or keep winning 1 out of 20 and pretending it’s fine — because GE16 will deliver the same verdict if nothing changes.

[BM] Setiausaha Politik PM Didapati Keluarkan Surat Sokongan Untuk Kontraktor Hospital — Anwar Kata Teguran Sudah Cukup

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-11-18

Anwar menggambarkan surat tersebut sebagai “kabur.” Pada 25 November, Shamsul meletak jawatan selepas tekanan media dan desakan Rafizi Ramli untuk siasatan SPRM. Pada 4 Disember 2025, SPRM mendakwa Shamsul dengan rasuah berjumlah RM176,829 berkaitan projek hospital itu.

🔥 MENGAPA IA PENTING: Pejabat PM sendiri yang mengeluarkan surat sokongan kontraktor. Inilah paya yang Anwar janjikan untuk dikosongkan. Shamsul Iskandar bukan tokoh kecil — beliau veteran PKR sejak penubuhan parti, beroperasi dalam Jabatan Perdana Menteri dengan pengaruh langsung ke atas perolehan kerajaan. Apabila setiausaha politik anda sendiri menyalurkan nama kontraktor kepada pegawai perolehan dan menerima RM152,000 dalam sogokan untuk cerut premium dan sut bertempah, jenama antirasuah bukan lagi rosak — ia menjadi bahan jenaka.

💩 MENGAPA IA KARUT: Naluri pertama Anwar adalah meminimumkan. “Surat itu kabur.” “Teguran sudah memadai.” The Edge melaporkan surat sokongan menyenaraikan kontraktor tertentu mengikut nama — bercanggah dengan dakwaan Anwar. Menamakan syarikat tertentu dalam surat sokongan kerajaan bukan kabur — ia cadangan, dan sesiapa dalam perolehan tahu betul apa maksudnya. Garis masa itu mendakwa: Shamsul keluarkan surat → Anwar tegur secara peribadi → laporan media muncul → Anwar pertahan teguran di Parlimen → ahli perniagaan dedahkan bayaran RM152K → Shamsul letak jawatan → SPRM dakwa. Pada setiap peringkat, Anwar ketinggalan — bertindak balas terhadap tekanan dan bukannya memimpin akauntabiliti.

📜 KES TERDAHULU: Surat sokongan daripada pejabat politik adalah salah satu mekanisme naungan tertua Malaysia. Di bawah BN, setiausaha politik secara rutin mengkomersialkan kedekatan mereka dengan kuasa. Di bawah Muhyiddin, pembantu Ahmad Maslan menghadapi tuduhan rasuah. Coraknya konsisten merentasi pentadbiran: pembantu politik beroperasi dalam zon kelabu pengaruh, dan PM memejamkan mata sehingga tekanan media memaksa tindakan.

🎯 SERUAN PH: Setiausaha politik kanan anda didakwa rasuah kerana mengeluarkan surat sokongan daripada pejabat anda. Jawapan pertama anda teguran peribadi. Jawapan kedua anda panggil surat itu “kabur.” Jawapan ketiga anda — selepas sogokan RM152,000 terdedah — menerima peletakan jawatan yang sepatutnya dituntut berminggu sebelumnya. Laksanakan perisytiharan harta wajib untuk semua lantikan politik. Haramkan surat sokongan dari pejabat menteri. Atau terus menemui rasuah dalam pejabat sendiri selepas media melakukan kerja anda.

PM's Political Secretary Caught Issuing Support Letters for Hospital Contractors — Anwar Says a Reprimand Is Enough

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-11-18

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: On 18 November 2025, Anwar confirmed in Parliament that he had reprimanded his senior political secretary, Shamsul Iskandar Mohd Akin, over support letters listing six contractors for a hospital project in Johor. Anwar described the letters as “vague.” On 25 November, Shamsul resigned after mounting media pressure and calls from Rafizi Ramli for a MACC investigation. A Sabah businessman then claimed he provided RM152,000 to Shamsul — money allegedly spent on home furniture, premium cigars, and tailored suits. On 4 December 2025, MACC charged Shamsul with corruption over RM176,829 in gratification related to the hospital project.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: The PM’s office was literally issuing contractor support letters. This is the swamp Anwar promised to drain. Shamsul Iskandar was not a minor figure — he was a PKR veteran since the party’s founding, operating within the Prime Minister’s Department with direct influence over government procurement. When your own political secretary is funnelling contractor names to procurement officers and receiving RM152,000 in kickbacks spent on premium cigars and tailored suits, the anti-corruption brand is not just damaged — it is a punchline.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: Anwar’s first instinct was to minimise. “The letters were vague.” “A reprimand is sufficient.” The Edge reported that the support letters listed specific contractors by name — contradicting Anwar’s claim. Naming specific companies in a government support letter is not vague — it is a recommendation, and anyone in procurement knows exactly what it means. The timeline is damning: Shamsul issues letters → Anwar reprimands privately → media reports emerge → Anwar defends the reprimand in Parliament → businessman reveals RM152K payments → Shamsul resigns → MACC charges him. At every stage, Anwar was behind the curve — reacting to pressure rather than leading on accountability. If Rafizi had not publicly demanded action, would Anwar have done anything beyond a private reprimand? The evidence suggests no.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: Support letters from political offices are one of Malaysia’s oldest patronage mechanisms. Under BN, political secretaries routinely monetised their proximity to power. Najib’s aide Jho Low operated with impunity for years before 1MDB collapsed. Under Muhyiddin, aide Ahmad Maslan faced corruption charges. The pattern is consistent across administrations: political aides operate in a grey zone of influence, and PMs look the other way until media pressure forces action. PH promised to end this culture. Instead, they replicated it — different party, same premium cigars.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: Your senior political secretary was charged with corruption for issuing support letters from your office. Your first response was a private reprimand. Your second response was to call the letters “vague.” Your third response — after RM152,000 in kickbacks surfaced — was to accept a resignation you should have demanded weeks earlier. Implement mandatory asset declarations for all political appointees. Ban support letters from ministerial offices. Publish procurement records for every project where support letters were issued. Or keep discovering corruption in your own office after the media does your job for you.

Jika kekayaan itu sah, isytiharkan. Jika pelantikan GLC berganda berasaskan merit, terbitkan kriteria pemilihan. PH menjanjikan ketelusan — mengugut tindakan undang-undang terhadap orang yang bertanya soal kekayaan adalah bertentangan dengan ketelusan.

📜 KES TERDAHULU: Kekayaan tidak dapat dijelaskan dalam kalangan pembantu politik adalah corak berulang dalam politik Malaysia. Di bawah BN, setiausaha politik dan pegawai secara rutin mengumpul kekayaan melalui kedekatan dengan kuasa. PH mengkritik ini tanpa henti ketika dalam pembangkang. Di bawah Amirudin, corak itu berterusan — gabungan berbeza, buku panduan sama.

🎯 SERUAN PH: Jika tuduhan itu palsu, buktikan dengan perisytiharan harta. Jika benar, siasat. Tetapi mengugut wartawan dan pentadbir WhatsApp dengan tindakan undang-undang sambil enggan menjawab soalan sebenar — ‘bagaimana penjawat awam mampu gaya hidup ini?’ — adalah tepat apa yang BN biasa lakukan. Anda panggil ia rasuah ketika itu. Apa anda panggil ia sekarang?

Selangor MB's Aide Accused of Unexplained Wealth — RM3M Bungalow, Luxury Trips, and Too Many GLC Posts

Said by: Amirudin Shari, Selangor Menteri Besar

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-11-15

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: In November 2025, a newly created WhatsApp group named ‘MB Selangor’ — comprising state assembly members and leaders — triggered controversy by accusing a PKR leader close to Selangor Menteri Besar Amirudin Shari of unexplained wealth. The administrator questioned how the leader could afford a RM3 million bungalow, luxury overseas trips, and high-end hotel stays while holding numerous positions in state-linked agencies. The group asked: ‘Why is one person appointed to so many positions, as if there are no other qualified people in Selangor?’

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: The allegations dropped while Amirudin was tabling the Selangor Budget 2026 — timing that suggests deliberate political pressure, but that doesn’t make the questions invalid. When a single aide holds multiple GLC positions and displays a lifestyle inconsistent with public service income, the public deserves answers. The Selangor MB’s office dismissed the claims as ‘baseless’ and ‘politically motivated’ — the standard deflection used by every Malaysian politician since independence.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: ‘We will lodge a report with the authorities’ is not an answer to ‘how did your aide afford a RM3M bungalow.’ Filing a police report against accusers is intimidation, not accountability. If the wealth is legitimate, declare it. If the multiple GLC appointments are merit-based, publish the selection criteria. PH promised transparency — threatening legal action against people who ask questions about wealth is the opposite of transparency.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: Unexplained wealth among political aides is a recurring pattern in Malaysian politics. Under BN, political secretaries and officers routinely accumulated wealth through proximity to power. PH criticised this relentlessly in opposition. Under Amirudin, the pattern continues — different coalition, same playbook.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: If the allegations are false, prove it with asset declarations. If they’re true, investigate. But threatening reporters and WhatsApp administrators with legal action while refusing to answer the actual question — ‘how does a public servant afford this lifestyle?’ — is exactly what BN used to do. You called it corruption then. What do you call it now?
