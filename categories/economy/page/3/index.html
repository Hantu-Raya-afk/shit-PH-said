Toll Abolition Promise Broken — Govt Pays RM591M to FREEZE Hikes Instead

Said by: Fahmi Fadzil, Communications Minister

Category: Economy

Date: 2025-12-18

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: PH promised to abolish highway tolls in both GE14 and GE15. Malaysian drivers continue paying tolls AND their taxes now subsidise the freeze. Festive toll-free periods were also cancelled.

1MDB Debt Still RM50 Billion — Malaysia's Largest Fiscal Burden Under Anwar's Watch

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister

Category: Economy

Date: 2025-12-03

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: As of December 2025, Malaysia’s 1MDB debt still stands at approximately RM50 billion — the largest single fiscal burden in the country’s history. The rakyat continues to pay the debt while the man responsible serves a comfortable sentence.

Said by: Johor state government and Anwar Ibrahim

Category: Economy

Date: 2025-11-18
