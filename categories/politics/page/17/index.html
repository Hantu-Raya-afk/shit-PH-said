DAP Special Congress July 12 — Party Questions Its Own Role in Government While Staying in Government

Said by: Chow Kon Yeow, Penang Chief Minister / DAP

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-02-21

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: On 21 February 2026, Penang CM Chow Kon Yeow announced that DAP would hold a special congress on 12 July 2026 to decide the party’s role in Anwar’s government. Chow insisted DAP was ’not withdrawing support from the Madani government’ but was discussing ‘whether to continue holding ministerial posts and certain positions.’ DAP’s 40 MPs would continue supporting the government. All members, including leaders, must abide by the congress decision.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: DAP is threatening to leave government without actually leaving government. The special congress is political theatre — a way to pressure Anwar for better terms while maintaining the safety of coalition membership. If DAP genuinely had policy disagreements, they’d articulate them publicly. Instead, they’re holding an internal vote on ‘whether to hold ministerial posts’ — which is a negotiation tactic dressed up as democratic deliberation.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: This is the political equivalent of threatening to quit your job while continuing to cash the paycheck. DAP wants credit for independence (‘we’re questioning our role!’) while enjoying the benefits of power (‘we’re not actually leaving!’). The special congress gives DAP negotiating leverage for the next cabinet reshuffle without any real risk. If the congress votes to stay — which it will — DAP gets to say ’the members decided.’ If they vote to leave cabinet — which they won’t — it would collapse the government. Both outcomes benefit DAP; neither benefits the rakyat.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: DAP has a history of internal drama over its role in coalition governments. In the Mahathir 2.0 era, DAP faced similar tensions over compromises with PPBM. The pattern is consistent: DAP enters coalitions, compromises on principles, faces internal backlash, holds dramatic internal events, then stays. The cycle repeats because the alternative — opposition — is worse for the party apparatus.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: If you have policy disagreements with the government, state them publicly. If you think the coalition isn’t delivering for your voters, say why. But don’t hold a special congress to ‘decide your role’ while simultaneously promising to keep supporting the government. That’s not democratic deliberation — it’s a negotiating ploy. The rakyat can see through it.

[BM] Anwar Lantik Bekas AG Kontroversi Ke Suruhanjaya Pelantikan Kehakiman — Majlis Peguam Saman Untuk Batalkan

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister / Ahmad Terrirudin Mohd Salleh, Federal Court Judge

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-02-16

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Pada 16 Februari 2026, Majlis Peguam Malaysia — mewakili 24,000 peguam — memfailkan permohonan semakan kehakiman di Mahkamah Tinggi untuk membatalkan pelantikan Perdana Menteri Anwar Ibrahim terhadap hakim Mahkamah Persekutuan Ahmad Terrirudin Mohd Salleh ke Suruhanjaya Pelantikan Kehakiman (SPK). Terrirudin, bekas Peguam Negara Anwar, dilantik ke SPK pada 15 November 2025 walaupun merupakan hakim paling junior di bangku Mahkamah Persekutuan — melanggar konvensyen lama bahawa jawatan itu diberikan kepada hakim kanan. Majlis Peguam menamakan Anwar, Terrirudin, SPK, dan kerajaan sebagai responden.

🔥 Kenapa Ini Penting

SPK adalah badan yang mencadangkan siapa yang menjadi hakim di Malaysia. Meletakkan bekas AG yang berkait rapat secara politik — yang pelantikannya sendiri ke Mahkamah Persekutuan pun kontroversi — ke dalam suruhanjaya yang mengawal pelantikan kehakiman adalah krisis integriti institusi. Majlis Peguam tidak memfailkan saman sesuka hati; ini adalah badan 24,000 profesional undang-undang yang memberitahu PM bahawa pelantikannya tidak sah. Jika orang yang menapis hakim dipilih tangan oleh PM atas kesetiaan politik, bukan kekananan, kebebasan seluruh badan kehakiman terjejas.

💩 Kenapa Ini Karut

Anwar berkempen atas dasar kebebasan kehakiman. Manifesto PRU15 beliau berjanji untuk menyahpolitikkan pelantikan kehakiman dan memperkukuh SPK. Sebaliknya, beliau melantik bekas AG sendiri — seorang yang baru dinaikkan ke Mahkamah Persekutuan beberapa bulan sebelumnya — ke badan yang menentukan siapa jadi hakim. Ini bukan reformasi; ini rampasan. Konvensyen melantik hakim kanan ke SPK wujud tepat untuk menghalang penyusunan politik sebegini. Bila Majlis Peguam kata pelantikan kamu tidak sah, kamu tidak layak panggil diri kerajaan reformasi lagi.

📜 Kes-Kes Sebelum Ini

Ini mengikut corak yang jelas. Kontroversi pelantikan kehakiman 2025 menyaksikan pilihan Anwar untuk Ketua Hakim Negara mencetuskan kemarahan. Sebelum itu, pengasingan AG-pendakwa raya — satu lagi janji reformasi kehakiman — telah ditangguhkan berulang kali, terbaru ditolak lagi tiga bulan pada Mac 2026. Setiap kali Anwar menyentuh sistem keadilan, beliau meraih kawalan bukannya kemerdekaan. Skandal pita Lingam di bawah Mahathir menunjukkan apa berlaku bila eksekutif menawan badan kehakiman. Anwar berjalan di laluan yang sama dengan PR yang lebih baik.

🎯 Panggilan Kepada PH

Kamu janji rakyat Malaysia badan kehakiman yang bebas. Kamu janji reformasi SPK. Kamu janji pisahkan AG dan pendakwa raya. Sebaliknya, kamu lantik bekas AG sendiri ke suruhanjaya yang pilih hakim, dan seluruh Majlis Peguam menyaman kamu kerananya. Bila 24,000 peguam kata pelantikan kamu tidak sah, itu bukan bunyi pembangkang — itu profesion undang-undang memberitahu kamu bahawa kamu sudah melampaui batas. Betulkan ini, atau berhenti berlakon reformasi bermakna apa-apa untuk kamu.

Anwar Appoints Controversial Ex-AG to Judicial Appointments Commission — Malaysian Bar Sues to Quash It

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister / Ahmad Terrirudin Mohd Salleh, Federal Court Judge

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-02-16

📌 What Happened

On February 16, 2026, the Malaysian Bar — representing 24,000 lawyers — filed a judicial review application at the High Court to quash Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim’s appointment of Federal Court judge Ahmad Terrirudin Mohd Salleh to the Judicial Appointments Commission (JAC). Terrirudin, Anwar’s former Attorney General, was appointed to the JAC on November 15, 2025 despite being the most junior judge on the Federal Court bench — breaking long-standing convention that the position goes to a senior apex court judge. The Bar named Anwar, Terrirudin, the JAC, and the government as respondents.

🔥 Why It Matters

The JAC is the body that recommends who becomes a judge in Malaysia. Putting a politically connected former AG — one whose own appointment to the Federal Court was controversial — into the commission that controls judicial appointments is an institutional integrity crisis. The Malaysian Bar doesn’t file suits lightly; this is a body of 24,000 legal professionals telling the PM his appointment is unlawful. If the person vetting judges was hand-picked by the PM for political loyalty rather than seniority, the independence of the entire judiciary is compromised.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

Anwar campaigned on judicial independence. His GE15 manifesto promised to depoliticise judicial appointments and strengthen the JAC. Instead, he appointed his own former AG — a man he elevated to the Federal Court just months earlier — to the very body that decides who gets to be a judge. This isn’t reform; it’s capture. The convention of appointing senior judges to the JAC exists precisely to prevent this kind of political stacking. When the Malaysian Bar says your appointment is unlawful, you don’t get to call yourself the reform government anymore.

📜 Previous Cases

This follows a pattern. The 2025 judicial appointment controversy saw Anwar’s pick for Chief Justice spark outrage. Before that, the AG-public prosecutor separation — another judicial reform promise — has been delayed repeatedly, most recently pushed back another three months in March 2026. Every time Anwar touches the justice system, he reaches for control rather than independence. The Lingam tape scandal under Mahathir showed what happens when the executive captures the judiciary. Anwar is walking the same path with better PR.

🎯 PH Callout

You promised Malaysians an independent judiciary. You promised to reform the JAC. You promised to separate the AG and public prosecutor. Instead, you appointed your own former AG to the commission that picks judges, and the entire Malaysian Bar is suing you for it. When 24,000 lawyers say your appointment is unlawful, that’s not opposition noise — that’s the legal profession telling you that you’ve crossed the line. Fix this, or stop pretending reform means anything to you.

[BM] Tiong King Sing Tarik Kad 'Tahu Tak Siapa Saya?' Sambil Membebel Kakitangan Soal Biadap

Said by: Tiong King Sing, Tourism, Arts and Culture Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-02-12

📌 APA YANG BERLAKU

Pada 12 Februari 2026, Menteri Pelancongan Tiong King Sing melancarkan kempen ‘Mesra Malaysia’ di KL Sentral — kempen tentang bersikap mesra dan bertimbang rasa kepada pelancong. Semasa pelancaran, Tiong mengadu tentang sikap biadap daripada kakitangan sektor awam dan swasta, berkata: “Sesetengah pegawai rasa mereka mempunyai banyak kuasa. Tetapi apabila orang tidak faham sesuatu isu, kita mesti jelaskan.”

South China Morning Post menyiarkan cerita dengan tajuk yang memusnahkan: “Menteri Malaysia bidas kakitangan biadap yang menjejaskan pelancongan: ’tahu tak siapa saya?’” — menangkap ironi seorang ahli politik berkuasa membebel orang lain tentang keangkuhan sambil memancarkan tenaga yang sama persis.

🔥 KENAPA INI PENTING

Tahun Melawat Malaysia 2026 sepatutnya keutamaan nasional. Sektor pelancongan penting untuk ekonomi Malaysia. Dan menteri yang bertanggungjawab menjadikan Malaysia mesra kepada dunia membuat tajuk berita kerana masalah sikapnya sendiri.

Ini bukan kontroversi pertama Tiong. Pada 2025, Anwar terpaksa memberi amaran kepadanya selepas alkohol dihidangkan di makan malam gala Tourism Malaysia — kegagalan sensitiviti di negara majoriti Muslim. Coraknya: seorang menteri yang menjana lebih banyak tajuk berita negatif berbanding ketibaan pelancong.

💩 KENAPA INI KARUT

Anda melancarkan kempen bertajuk ‘Mesra Malaysia’ sambil bersikap seperti orang paling tidak mesra dalam bilik. Ketidakselarasan kognitif ini menakjubkan. Seorang menteri mengadu tentang penjawat awam yang “rasa mempunyai banyak kuasa” — sambil menggunakan kuasa kementerian persekutuan untuk mempermalukan pekerja secara terbuka — adalah puncak ironi politik Malaysia.

Tenaga ’tahu tak siapa saya’ tepat seperti sikap yang pelancong dan rakyat benci tentang birokrasi Malaysia. Dan menteri yang ditugaskan memperbaikinya adalah penjelmaan sikap itu.

📜 KES TERDAHULU

Tiong King Sing ialah ahli politik usahawan dari gabungan GPS Sarawak. Kontroversi alkohol di makan malam gala 2025 sudah menunjukkan seorang menteri yang terputus daripada sensitiviti. Sektor pelancongan Malaysia juga dibelenggu pemandu teksi penipu, caj berlebihan di tempat pelancongan, dan masalah visa — isu sistemik yang memerlukan penyelesaian sistemik, bukan sidang akhbar tentang sikap.

🎯 SERUAN KEPADA PH

Anda letakkan Tiong King Sing bertanggungjawab atas pelancongan. Beliau kini membuat tajuk berita antarabangsa atas keangkuhan sambil melancarkan kempen kemesraan. Menteri yang bertanggungjawab atas imej Malaysia merosakkan imej Malaysia. Jika anda mahu Tahun Melawat Malaysia 2026 berjaya, mulakan dengan memastikan menteri anda tidak menjadi jenaka yang pelancong ceritakan bila pulang.
