Parliament Reduced to Rubber Stamp — PM Whips Ensure Bills Pass Without Genuine Debate

Said by: Opposition MPs / Parliamentary Reform Advocates

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-11-01

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: Despite PH’s promise to strengthen parliamentary democracy, the Dewan Rakyat continues to function as a rubber stamp rather than a genuine deliberative body. Government bills pass with minimal amendment, committee work is perfunctory, and the government whip ensures coalition MPs vote in bloc. The PM term limit bill — one of PH’s signature reform promises — failed in Parliament because PH couldn’t even muster enough of its own MPs to support it. Fahmi Fadzil blamed “opposition confusion” for the failure, when 8 of PH’s own MPs didn’t show up to vote.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: Parliament is supposed to be the check on executive power. When parliament is reduced to a venue for the PM’s speeches and pro-forma bill passage, democratic governance degrades into elected authoritarianism. Important legislation passes without meaningful debate. Bad policies go unchallenged. The PM term limit bill failure is the most damning example: PH had the numbers, had the mandate, and still couldn’t deliver on one of the simplest democratic reforms imaginable — because its own MPs couldn’t be bothered to attend.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: PH explicitly promised parliamentary reform: stronger select committees, an independent Speaker, greater allocation for opposition MPs, and meaningful debate on legislation. Some incremental changes were made, but the fundamental dynamics haven’t changed. The government controls the agenda, determines which bills are debated, and uses coalition arithmetic to ensure outcomes. Under BN, Najib’s government once passed 11 bills in a single day without proper debate. PH criticised this as democratic malpractice. Under PH, the pace is slightly slower but the outcomes are equally predetermined.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: Parliamentary rubber-stamping is a Malaysian tradition that predates PH. Under Mahathir 1.0, parliament was a formality. Under Najib, the OSA, SOSMA, and NSC Act were rammed through with minimal debate. PH 1.0 (2018-2020) made some improvements — reviving select committees and increasing opposition funding — but these reforms were incomplete and have stalled under PH 2.0. The East Asia Forum documented how “Malaysia’s reform aspirations clashed with political realities in 2024,” noting that parliamentary independence remained a promise rather than a practice.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: You control the Dewan Rakyat. You have a supermajority with BN’s support. You couldn’t even pass a PM term limit bill — your own signature reform. You had the numbers, you had the mandate, and you still couldn’t deliver. If you can’t limit your own power when you have the votes to do it, you were never going to limit your own power at all. Strengthen select committees with real investigative authority. Give the Speaker genuine independence. Allow free votes on reform bills. Or keep running a parliament that functions exactly like the one you inherited — just with different faces in the government benches.

[BM] RTM Salah Kenal Pasti TIGA Pemimpin ASEAN Secara Langsung — Panggil PM Singapura 'Lee Hsien Loong' Di Sidang Kemuncak Yang Malaysia Anjurkan

Said by: RTM / Communications Ministry under Fahmi Fadzil

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-10-26

📌 APA YANG BERLAKU

Pada 26 Oktober 2025, semasa siaran langsung majlis pembukaan Sidang Kemuncak ASEAN ke-47 — yang Malaysia sendiri menjadi tuan rumah — penyiar kebangsaan Radio Televisyen Malaysia (RTM) salah mengenal pasti tiga pemimpin Asia Tenggara:

  • PM Singapura Lawrence Wong dipanggil Lee Hsien Loong (pendahulunya)
  • PM Thailand Anutin Charnvirakul dipanggil Srettha Thavisin (pendahulunya)
  • Presiden Indonesia Prabowo Subianto dipanggil Jokowi (pendahulunya) RTM mengeluarkan dua kenyataan permohonan maaf berasingan. Jabatan penyiaran berkata ia “mengambil perkara ini dengan serius” dan “tindakan sewajarnya telah diambil.”

🔥 KENAPA IA PENTING

Ini adalah detik Malaysia di pentas dunia. Sidang Kemuncak ASEAN adalah acara diplomatik utama rantau ini, dan Malaysia menjadi tuan rumah. Seluruh rantau sedang menonton. Dan penyiar kebangsaan Malaysia tidak mampu menyebut nama tiga ketua negara jiran dengan betul.

Ini bukan kesilapan kecil. Mereka bukan pegawai yang tidak dikenali — mereka adalah pemimpin Singapura, Thailand, dan Indonesia, jiran terdekat Malaysia. Hakikat bahawa pengulas RTM menggunakan maklumat lapuk menunjukkan tiada siapa di penyiar tersebut yang ambil peduli untuk mengemaskini bahan taklimat mereka. Dalam zaman Google, ini tidak boleh dimaafkan.

💩 KENAPA INI KARUT

RTM berada di bawah Kementerian Komunikasi, diketuai Fahmi Fadzil. Ini adalah kementerian yang sama yang bercakap tentang transformasi digital, regulasi AI, dan membina keupayaan media Malaysia. Mereka tidak mampu pun memberitahu pengulas mereka siapa pemimpin negara jiran semasa.

Permohonan maaf itu — dikeluarkan dalam dua kenyataan berasingan kerana pada mulanya mereka hanya mengakui dua daripada tiga kesilapan — adalah puncak segala-galanya. Anda tidak boleh memalukan Malaysia secara langsung di sidang kemuncak yang Malaysia anjurkan dan kemudian berkata “tindakan sewajarnya telah diambil” tanpa memberitahu sesiapa apa tindakan itu.

📜 KES TERDAHULU

RTM telah menjadi bahan jenaka selama berdekad-dekad. Kurang dana, pengurusan lemah, dan dilayan sebagai corong kerajaan dan bukannya penyiar profesional. Di bawah PH, ada perbincangan tentang mereformasi media kerajaan. Sebaliknya, mereka mendapat Menteri Komunikasi yang menumpukan kepada mengawal TikTok untuk remaja sementara RTM tidak mampu menamakan tiga pemimpin dunia dengan betul.

🎯 TEGURAN KEPADA PH

Anda menjadi tuan rumah Sidang Kemuncak ASEAN. Anda mahu dunia melihat Malaysia sebagai sebuah negara yang serius, moden, dan cekap. Dan penyiar kebangsaan anda memanggil tiga pemimpin jiran dengan nama pendahulu mereka secara langsung. Seluruh rantau mentertawakan kita. Mothership di Singapura menyiarkannya. CNA menyiarkannya. SCMP menyiarkannya. Ini adalah infrastruktur komunikasi anda. Ini adalah jenama Madani anda. Betulkan.

RTM Misidentifies THREE ASEAN Leaders on Live TV — Calls Singapore's PM 'Lee Hsien Loong' at Summit Malaysia Hosted

Said by: RTM / Communications Ministry under Fahmi Fadzil

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-10-26

📌 WHAT HAPPENED

On 26 October 2025, during the live broadcast of the opening ceremony of the 47th ASEAN Summit — which Malaysia was hosting — the national broadcaster Radio Televisyen Malaysia (RTM) misidentified three Southeast Asian leaders:

  • Singapore PM Lawrence Wong was called Lee Hsien Loong (his predecessor)
  • Thailand PM Anutin Charnvirakul was called Srettha Thavisin (his predecessor)
  • Indonesia President Prabowo Subianto was called Jokowi (his predecessor) RTM issued two separate apology statements. The broadcasting department said it “took the matter seriously” and that “appropriate action had been taken.”

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS

This was Malaysia’s moment on the world stage. The ASEAN Summit is the region’s premier diplomatic event, and Malaysia was the host. The entire region was watching. And Malaysia’s national broadcaster couldn’t get the names of three neighbouring heads of state right.

This isn’t a minor slip. These weren’t obscure officials — they were the leaders of Singapore, Thailand, and Indonesia, Malaysia’s closest neighbours. The fact that RTM’s commentators were working from outdated information suggests nobody at the broadcaster bothered to update their briefing materials. In the age of Google, this is inexcusable.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT

RTM falls under the Communications Ministry, led by Fahmi Fadzil. This is the same ministry that talks about digital transformation, AI regulation, and building Malaysia’s media capabilities. They can’t even brief their commentators on who the current leaders of neighbouring countries are.

The apology — issued in two separate statements because they initially only acknowledged two of the three errors — was the cherry on top. You don’t get to embarrass Malaysia on live television at the summit Malaysia is hosting and then say “appropriate action has been taken” without telling anyone what that action was.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES

RTM has been a running joke for decades. Underfunded, poorly managed, and treated as a government mouthpiece rather than a professional broadcaster. Under PH, there was talk of reforming state media. Instead, they got a Communications Minister who focuses on regulating TikTok for teenagers while RTM can’t name three world leaders correctly.

🎯 PH CALLOUT

You hosted the ASEAN Summit. You wanted the world to see Malaysia as a serious, modern, competent nation. And your national broadcaster called three neighbouring leaders by their predecessors’ names on live TV. The entire region laughed at us. Mothership in Singapore ran it. CNA ran it. SCMP ran it. This is your communications infrastructure. This is your Madani brand. Fix it.

[BM] Dana SMUE RM200 Juta Didakwa Diseleweng — SPRM Siasat Skim Warga Emas Selangor

Said by: Amirudin Shari / MACC

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-10-16

📌 APA YANG BERLAKU: Suruhanjaya Pencegahan Rasuah Malaysia melancarkan siasatan terhadap dakwaan penyelewengan RM200 juta daripada Skim Mesra Usia Emas (SMUE) Selangor — skim kebajikan warga emas negeri itu. Beberapa pegawai yang dikaitkan dengan skim itu disoal siasat. Dana SMUE direka untuk menyediakan bantuan kewangan kepada warga emas Selangor, menjadikan dakwaan penyelewengan itu amat keji — wang yang dimaksudkan untuk warga emas didakwa dialihkan ke tempat lain di bawah pemantauan PH.

🔥 KENAPA IA PENTING: RM200 juta bukan kesilapan pembundaran — ia adalah jumlah besar yang didakwa dicuri daripada program kebajikan untuk warga emas. Selangor adalah permata mahkota PH, negeri terkaya, ditadbir secara berterusan oleh PKR/PH sejak 2008. Jika RM200 juta boleh hilang daripada dana kebajikan warga emas di negeri yang dikawal PH, apa yang ia katakan tentang dakwaan PH sebagai gabungan ’tadbir urus bersih’? Dana itu sepatutnya membantu golongan paling lemah. Sebaliknya, ia mungkin telah membantu orang dalam.

💩 KENAPA INI KARUT: PH telah mentadbir Selangor selama hampir 18 tahun. Ini bukan masalah warisan BN — ini berlaku di bawah pemantauan PH, di bawah lantikan PH, di negeri PH. Parti yang membina jenamanya atas antirasuah menghadapi siasatan rasuah di negeri paling selamatnya. Pejabat Amirudin sangat senyap mengenai siasatan itu. Tiada kenyataan umum menuntut akauntabiliti. Tiada penggantungan pegawai. Tiada audit bebas diumumkan. Senyap.

📜 KES TERDAHULU: Ini menyertai siasatan perlombongan pasir, pembekuan aset MBI, dan dakwaan kekayaan yang tidak dapat dijelaskan sebagai sebahagian daripada corak. Selangor di bawah PH bukan model tadbir urus bersih — ia adalah negeri di mana SPRM terus menemui sesuatu. Pada satu ketika, satu siasatan adalah pengecualian; empat siasatan adalah corak.

🎯 TEGURAN KEPADA PH: RM200 juta daripada dana warga emas. Di negeri anda. Di bawah pemantauan anda. Dengan lantikan anda. Jika BN melakukan ini, anda akan mengadakan perhimpunan kebangsaan. Oleh kerana ia negeri anda, anda mengadakan sidang akhbar tentang pengurusan sisa. Tuntut audit awam penuh SMUE. Gantung pegawai-pegawai tersebut. Dan beritahu rakyat ke mana wang itu pergi.

RM200 Million SMUE Fund Allegedly Misappropriated — MACC Probes Selangor Senior Citizens' Scheme

Said by: Amirudin Shari / MACC

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-10-16

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: The Malaysian Anti-Corruption Commission launched an investigation into the alleged misappropriation of RM200 million from Selangor’s Skim Mesra Usia Emas (SMUE) — the state’s elderly welfare scheme. Multiple officials linked to the scheme were questioned. The SMUE fund was designed to provide financial assistance to Selangor’s senior citizens, making the alleged misappropriation particularly egregious — money meant for the elderly allegedly diverted elsewhere under PH’s watch.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: RM200 million is not a rounding error — it’s a massive sum allegedly stolen from a welfare programme for senior citizens. Selangor is PH’s crown jewel, the richest state, run continuously by PKR/PH since 2008. If RM200 million can go missing from an elderly welfare fund in a PH-controlled state, what does that say about PH’s claim to be the ‘clean governance’ coalition? The fund was supposed to help the most vulnerable. Instead, it may have helped insiders.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: PH has governed Selangor for nearly 18 years. This isn’t an inherited BN problem — this happened on PH’s watch, under PH’s appointees, in PH’s state. The party that built its brand on anti-corruption is facing a corruption probe in its safest state. Amirudin’s office has been conspicuously quiet about the investigation. No public statements demanding accountability. No suspension of officials. No independent audit announced. Silence.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: This joins the sand mining probe, the MBI asset freeze, and the unexplained wealth allegations as part of a pattern. Selangor under PH isn’t a model of clean governance — it’s a state where MACC keeps finding things. At some point, one investigation is an outlier; four investigations is a pattern.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: RM200 million from a senior citizens’ fund. In your state. Under your watch. With your appointees. If BN did this, you’d hold a national rally. Since it’s your state, you hold a press conference about waste management. Demand a full public audit of SMUE. Suspend the officials. And tell the rakyat where the money went.

Category: Politics

Rafizi secara terbuka menggesa Anwar untuk berhadapan dengan dakwaan terhadap Farhash Wafa, memberi amaran bahawa CPI Malaysia mendatar pada 50 mata — skor yang sama seperti 2015 semasa kemuncak skandal 1MDB. Kata ia ‘amat berbahaya jika pentadbiran dilihat tidak konsisten dalam memerangi rasuah.’

Your most competent minister warned you publicly that protecting Farhash was destroying your anti-corruption credentials. Your response was to sideline the minister and protect the aide. In any reform government, that’s backwards. In yours, it’s standard operating procedure.

[BM] Anwar Periksa Kerja Sendiri — PM Yang Juga Menteri Kewangan Bentangkan Bajet Dan Dakwa Ia Hebat

Said by: Opposition / Budget Analysts

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-10-10

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Bajet 2026 dibentangkan oleh Anwar Ibrahim — sebagai Menteri Kewangan — kepada parlimen, di mana beliau duduk sebagai Perdana Menteri. Beliau kemudian mempertahankan bajetnya sendiri di parlimen, memuji pencapaiannya, dan menolak kritikan. Orang yang menulis peperiksaan juga orang yang memberikan markah.

🔥 Kenapa Ia Penting

Dalam mana-mana demokrasi yang berfungsi, fungsi kewangan mempunyai pengawasan bebas daripada kepimpinan politik. Apabila satu orang mengawal kedua-dua agenda politik dan dompet negara, tiada semakan bermakna terhadap keputusan fiskal. Peruntukan bajet menjadi alat politik tanpa penelitian bebas. Peranan Parlimen sebagai badan pengawasan bajet terjejas apabila PM/MK mengawal kedua-dua eksekutif dan agenda fiskal.

💩 Kenapa Ini Karut

Anwar mengkritik Najib SECARA KHUSUS kerana memegang kedua-dua portfolio PM dan MK, berhujah ia membolehkan 1MDB dengan menghapuskan pengawasan. Kemudian Anwar mengambil KEDUA-DUA portfolio sendiri. Apabila ditanya mengenai ini, respons beliau pada dasarnya ialah ‘percayalah, saya berbeza.’ Itu bukan reformasi institusi — ia adalah pengecualian peribadi. Kelemahan struktur yang sama yang membolehkan 1MDB wujud di bawah Anwar.

📜 Kes Terdahulu

Najib sebagai PM+MK: 1MDB berlaku. Mahathir sebagai PM: peranan Menteri Kewangan menyediakan sedikit pemisahan. Di bawah prinsip tadbir urus baik, pengawasan fiskal memerlukan kebebasan institusi, bukan kepercayaan peribadi. Singapura memisahkan peranan tersebut. UK memisahkan peranan tersebut. Kebanyakan demokrasi yang berfungsi memisahkan peranan tersebut.

🎯 Teguran Kepada PH

Anda dipilih kerana PM terakhir yang memegang kedua-dua portfolio mencuri berbilion. Penyelesaian anda untuk mencegah 1MDB seterusnya ialah… meniru kelemahan struktur yang sama. Anda memeriksa kerja sendiri, dan tidak mengejutkan, anda terus memberi diri sendiri A+. Lantik Menteri Kewangan berasingan atau berhenti berpura-pura reformasi institusi penting bagi anda.
