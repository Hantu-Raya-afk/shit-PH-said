[BM] Hukuman Penjara Najib Dipotong Separuh — Lembaga Pengampunan Di Bawah Pengawasan Anwar

Said by: Pardons Board / King of Malaysia

Category: Politics

Date: 2024-02-02

📌 APA YANG BERLAKU: Pada 2 Februari 2024, Lembaga Pengampunan — yang beroperasi di bawah kuasa perlembagaan Yang di-Pertuan Agong, dengan PM sebagai ahli — memotong separuh hukuman penjara 12 tahun Najib Razak kepada 6 tahun atas peranannya dalam skandal 1MDB. Denda RM210 juta Najib juga dikurangkan kepada RM50 juta. Keputusan itu datang di tengah kontroversi “adendum diraja,” dengan pihak Najib mendakwa dokumen tambahan wujud yang memberikan tahanan rumah. Reuters melaporkan pengampunan itu datang “di tengah tuduhan kemunduran reformasi.” Pengkritik memanggilnya urusan bilik belakang untuk mengekalkan UMNO dalam gabungan — UMNO telah menjadikan pembebasan Najib sebagai prasyarat untuk sokongan berterusan kepada kerajaan Anwar.

🔥 MENGAPA INI PENTING: Najib disabitkan dengan skandal kewangan terbesar dalam sejarah Malaysia — berbilion dicuri dari dana kekayaan negara, dicuci melalui bank global, dibelanjakan untuk kapal layar, barang kemas, dan filem Hollywood. Sabitan itu adalah pencapaian utama PH di bawah PH 1.0. Memotong separuh hukuman di bawah PH 2.0 menghantar mesej yang dahsyat: di Malaysia, penalti rasuah boleh dirunding jika anda mempunyai cukup leveraj politik. Pengampunan itu bukan berdasarkan bukti baharu atau merit undang-undang — ia berdasarkan aritmetik gabungan. Sokongan UMNO kepada PH datang dengan harga tersirat, dan rakyat menyaksikan harga itu dibayar.

💩 MENGAPA INI KARUT: Anwar menghabiskan seluruh kerjaya politiknya menuntut akauntabiliti bagi Najib. “Malu Apa Bossku” adalah slogan yang diejek PH tanpa henti. Kini hukuman Najib dipotong separuh, dendanya dikurangkan 76%, dan penyokongnya mendesak untuk tahanan rumah — semua di bawah pengawasan Anwar. Lembaga Pengampunan termasuk PM. Keputusan itu tidak boleh dipisahkan daripada konteks politik: UMNO mahu hukuman Najib dikurangkan, UMNO penting kepada majoriti PH, dan hukuman itu dikurangkan. Memanggil ini apa-apa selain urusan politik memerlukan tahap kenaifan yang tiada rakyat Malaysia miliki.

📜 KES TERDAHULU: Malaysia mempunyai sejarah pengampunan yang mudah secara politik. Anwar sendiri diampunkan oleh Yang di-Pertuan Agong selepas kemenangan PH 1.0 pada 2018 — pengampunan yang disambut sebagai keadilan. Perbezaannya: sabitan Anwar dilihat secara meluas sebagai bermotif politik. Sabitan Najib ditegakkan oleh setiap peringkat badan kehakiman berdasarkan bukti yang sangat kuat. Mengampunkan banduan politik dan mengurangkan hukuman kleptokrat yang disabitkan adalah dua tindakan yang secara asas berbeza — tetapi kesediaan PH memudahkan kedua-duanya mencadangkan bahawa proses pengampunan adalah alat politik, bukan mekanisme keadilan.

🎯 SERUAN KEPADA PH: Anda membina gerakan anda atas menuntut akauntabiliti Najib. Kemudian anda potong separuh hukumannya untuk menyenangkan UMNO. Rakyat berpawai dalam perhimpunan Bersih, mengundi dalam PRU14 dan PRU15, dan menunggu bertahun untuk keadilan — dan anda menukarnya dengan kestabilan gabungan. Jika jenayah Najib bernilai 12 tahun apabila PH dalam pembangkang, ia tidak menjadi bernilai 6 tahun hanya kerana UMNO dalam gabungan anda. Setiap ringgit yang dicuri Najib masih hilang. Setiap tahun yang dibuang daripada hukumannya adalah diskaun atas akauntabiliti. Rakyat akan ingat.

Najib's Jail Sentence Halved — Pardons Board Under Anwar's Watch

Said by: Pardons Board / King of Malaysia

Category: Politics

Date: 2024-02-02

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: On 2 February 2024, the Pardons Board — which operates under the constitutional authority of the King, with the PM as a member — halved Najib Razak’s 12-year jail sentence to 6 years for his role in the 1MDB scandal. Najib’s RM210 million fine was also reduced to RM50 million. The decision came amid a “royal addendum” controversy, with Najib’s camp claiming an additional document existed granting house arrest. Reuters reported the pardon came “amid accusations of backsliding on reforms.” Critics called it a backroom deal to keep UMNO in the coalition — UMNO had made Najib’s release a precondition for continued support of Anwar’s government.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: Najib was convicted of the largest financial scandal in Malaysian history — billions stolen from a sovereign wealth fund, laundered through global banks, spent on yachts, jewellery, and Hollywood films. The conviction was PH’s signature achievement under PH 1.0. Halving the sentence under PH 2.0 sent a devastating message: in Malaysia, corruption penalties are negotiable if you have enough political leverage. The pardon was not based on new evidence or legal merit — it was based on coalition arithmetic. UMNO’s support for PH came with an implicit price, and the rakyat is watching that price get paid.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: Anwar spent his entire political career demanding accountability for Najib. “Malu Apa Bossku” was the slogan PH mocked relentlessly. Now Najib’s sentence is halved, his fine reduced by 76%, and his supporters are pushing for house arrest — all under Anwar’s watch. The Pardons Board includes the PM. The decision cannot be separated from the political context: UMNO wanted Najib’s sentence reduced, UMNO is essential to PH’s majority, and the sentence was reduced. Calling this anything other than a political deal requires a level of naivety that no Malaysian possesses.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: Malaysia has a history of politically convenient pardons. Anwar himself was pardoned by the King after PH 1.0’s victory in 2018 — a pardon that was celebrated as justice. The difference: Anwar’s conviction was widely seen as politically motivated. Najib’s conviction was upheld by every level of the judiciary based on overwhelming evidence. Pardoning a political prisoner and reducing the sentence of a convicted kleptocrat are fundamentally different acts — but PH’s willingness to facilitate both suggests that the pardons process is a political tool, not a justice mechanism.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: You built your movement on holding Najib accountable. Then you halved his sentence to keep UMNO happy. The rakyat marched in Bersih rallies, voted in GE14 and GE15, and waited years for justice — and you traded it for coalition stability. If Najib’s crime was worth 12 years when PH was in opposition, it doesn’t become worth 6 years just because UMNO is in your coalition. Every ringgit Najib stole is still missing. Every year removed from his sentence is a discount on accountability. The rakyat will remember.

[BM] Amnesty International 2024: Anwar 'Mengkhianati Janji Reformasi Yang Dibuat Kepada Pengundi Malaysia'

Said by: Amnesty International / Shayna Bauchner

Category: Politics

Date: 2024-01-11

📌 APA YANG BERLAKU: Pada Januari 2024, laporan tahunan Amnesty International menyatakan bahawa Anwar Ibrahim telah “mengkhianati janji reformasi yang dibuatnya kepada pengundi Malaysia supaya beliau boleh mengekalkan gabungannya.” Laporan itu, ditulis oleh penyelidik Shayna Bauchner, mengenal pasti tujuh bidang di mana Malaysia gagal memenuhi tanggungjawab hak asasi manusianya, termasuk kebebasan bersuara, kebebasan berhimpun, kebebasan media, hak pendatang, hak LGBTQ+, dan hukuman mati. Amnesty menyatakan bahawa kerajaan bukan sahaja gagal memajukan reformasi tetapi secara aktif menggunakan undang-undang menindas yang dijanjikan untuk dimansuhkan.

🔥 MENGAPA INI PENTING: Apabila Amnesty International — pertubuhan hak asasi manusia terbesar dunia — menggunakan perkataan “mengkhianati,” ia bukan membuat kritikan biasa. Ia mendokumentasikan kerajaan yang secara eksplisit menjanjikan reformasi tertentu, menerima undi atas dasar itu, dan kemudian berpatah balik. Pilihan perkataan itu penting: “terbantut” membawa makna kelewatan; “mengkhianati” membawa makna niat. Amnesty mengatakan bahawa peninggalan reformasi oleh PH bukan disebabkan kekangan atau kerumitan — ia adalah pilihan sengaja untuk mengutamakan kestabilan gabungan berbanding komitmen hak asasi manusia.

💩 MENGAPA INI KARUT: Pembelaan PH — “kekangan gabungan memerlukan kompromi” — adalah tepat-tepat hujah yang digunakan BN selama berdekad. UMNO berkata ia tidak boleh mereformasi kerana rakan gabungan. PAS berkata ia tidak boleh bersederhana kerana asasnya. Kini PH berkata ia tidak boleh menyampaikan hak kerana UMNO ada dalam gabungan. Alasan itu serupa; hanya nama parti yang bertukar. Jika anda tahu UMNO akan menyekat reformasi apabila anda membentuk gabungan, maka janji manifesto anda adalah palsu dengan sengaja. Jika anda tidak menjangkakannya, anda tidak cekap. Walau bagaimanapun, penggunaan “mengkhianati” oleh Amnesty adalah tepat.

📜 KES TERDAHULU: Amnesty telah mendokumentasikan kegagalan hak asasi manusia Malaysia merentas setiap pentadbiran. Di bawah Mahathir 1.0, tumpuan adalah penahanan ISA dan tindakan keras terhadap perbezaan pendapat. Di bawah Najib, ia adalah penindasan berkaitan 1MDB dan Akta Hasutan. Di bawah PH 1.0, Amnesty mencatatkan kemajuan separa sebelum Langkah Sheraton. Di bawah PH 2.0, keputusan adalah yang paling keras — kerana jurang antara janji dan penyampaian tidak pernah lebih lebar. PH membuat komitmen hak asasi manusia paling bercita-cita tinggi dalam sejarah Malaysia, dan memungkirinya secara lebih menyeluruh daripada mana-mana pendahulu.

🎯 SERUAN KEPADA PH: Amnesty International mengatakan anda “mengkhianati” pengundi anda. Bukan “mengecewakan.” Bukan “tidak memenuhi.” Mengkhianati. Perkataan itu akan mengikut kerajaan ini ke setiap forum antarabangsa, setiap pertemuan diplomatik, dan setiap pilihan raya masa depan. Anda masih boleh mengubah trajektori: mansuhkan Akta Hasutan, lindungi hak berhimpun, jamin kebebasan akhbar, dan berhenti menggunakan undang-undang menindas terhadap pengkritik. Atau biarkan “mengkhianati” menjadi ringkasan satu perkataan era Anwar — kerana sekarang, Amnesty dan HRW bersetuju bahawa ia memang begitu.

Amnesty International 2024: Anwar 'Betrayed the Reform Pledges He Made to Malaysian Voters'

Said by: Amnesty International / Shayna Bauchner

Category: Politics

Date: 2024-01-11

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: In January 2024, Amnesty International’s annual report stated that Anwar Ibrahim had “betrayed the reform pledges he made to Malaysian voters so he could hold his coalition together.” The report, authored by researcher Shayna Bauchner, identified seven areas where Malaysia fell short of its human rights obligations, including freedom of expression, freedom of assembly, media freedom, migrant rights, LGBTQ+ rights, and the death penalty. Amnesty noted that the government had not only failed to advance reforms but had actively used repressive laws it had promised to repeal.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: When Amnesty International — the world’s largest human rights organisation — uses the word “betrayed,” it is not making a casual criticism. It is documenting a government that explicitly promised specific reforms, received votes on that basis, and then reversed course. The word choice matters: “stalled” implies delay; “betrayed” implies intent. Amnesty is saying that PH’s abandonment of reform was not due to constraints or complexity — it was a deliberate choice to prioritise coalition stability over human rights commitments.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: PH’s defence — “coalition constraints require compromise” — is exactly the argument BN used for decades. UMNO said it couldn’t reform because of coalition partners. PAS said it couldn’t moderate because of its base. Now PH says it can’t deliver rights because UMNO is in the coalition. The excuse is identical; only the party name has changed. If you knew UMNO would block reforms when you formed the coalition, then your manifesto promises were knowingly false. If you didn’t anticipate it, you’re incompetent. Either way, Amnesty’s use of “betrayed” is accurate.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: Amnesty has documented Malaysian human rights failures across every administration. Under Mahathir 1.0, the focus was ISA detentions and crackdowns on dissent. Under Najib, it was 1MDB-related repression and the Sedition Act. Under PH 1.0, Amnesty noted partial progress before the Sheraton Move. Under PH 2.0, the verdict is the harshest yet — because the gap between promise and delivery has never been wider. PH made the most ambitious human rights commitments in Malaysian history, and broke them more comprehensively than any predecessor.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: Amnesty International says you “betrayed” your voters. Not “disappointed.” Not “fell short.” Betrayed. That word will follow this government into every international forum, every diplomatic meeting, and every future election. You can still change the trajectory: repeal the Sedition Act, protect assembly rights, guarantee press freedom, and stop using repressive laws against critics. Or let “betrayed” become the one-word summary of the Anwar era — because right now, Amnesty and HRW agree that it is.

[BM] SPRM Rampas Ilham Tower Daim Dalam Siasatan Pengubahan Wang Haram — Pendakwaan Terpilih Atau Akauntabiliti Tulen?

Said by: Azam Baki, MACC Chief Commissioner / Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2023-12-21

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Pada 21 Disember 2023, SPRM merampas Ilham Tower 60 tingkat di Kuala Lumpur — bernilai anggaran RM1 hingga RM1.5 bilion — di bawah Seksyen 51 Akta Pencegahan Pengubahan Wang Haram (AMLA). Rampasan ini sebahagian daripada siasatan yang semakin meluas terhadap bekas menteri kewangan Tun Daim Zainuddin berhubung urus niaga korporat 1997 bernilai RM2.3 bilion melibatkan Renong Bhd dan United Engineers Malaysia (UEM), kedua-duanya dikaitkan dengan UMNO. Ketua Pesuruhjaya SPRM Tan Sri Azam Baki mengesahkan rampasan itu. Daim menafikan sebarang salah laku dan mengekalkan pendirian bahawa siasatan bermotif politik. SPRM juga mendakwa Daim dan isterinya Naimah Abdul Khalid kerana gagal mengisytiharkan harta, dan mengeluarkan notis untuk mengesan empat orang anak Daim.

🔥 Kenapa Ini Penting

Di atas kertas, menyiasat kekayaan tersembunyi bekas menteri kewangan adalah tepat apa yang kerajaan antirasuah patut buat. Tetapi konteksnya penting: Daim adalah sekutu utama Mahathir, yang kini menentang Anwar secara politik. Sekutu Mahathir — Daim, pelbagai tokoh pembangkang — menghadapi penelitian SPRM di bawah Anwar, manakala rakan gabungan Anwar sendiri Zahid mempunyai 47 pertuduhan digugurkan beberapa bulan sebelum itu. Coraknya tidak boleh disangkal: SPRM keras terhadap lawan politik dan lembut terhadap sekutu politik.

💩 Kenapa Ini Karut

Ketua SPRM yang sama, Azam Baki, yang agresif mengejar Daim adalah orang yang sama yang menghadapi skandal pegangan saham sendiri dan dilindungi kerajaan Anwar. Pejabat AG yang sama yang meluluskan siasatan Daim juga menandatangani penggugaran 47 pertuduhan Zahid. Kalau SPRM benar-benar bebas, rakyat mungkin meraikan siasatan Daim sebagai kemenangan. Tetapi apabila ketua agensi antirasuah sendiri dibelenggu kontroversi, dan kerajaan memilih rasuah mana untuk didakwa berdasarkan penjajaran politik, seluruh latihan ini kelihatan seperti kuasa negara berpakaian akauntabiliti.

📜 Kes-Kes Sebelum Ini

Pendakwaan terpilih mempunyai sejarah panjang di Malaysia. Di bawah Najib, SPRM dituduh menyasar tokoh pembangkang sambil mengabaikan rasuah berkaitan BN. Di bawah Mahathir 2.0, pemimpin UMNO didakwa mengikut kepentingan politik. Di bawah Anwar, senarai sasaran berubah lagi: sekutu Mahathir diserbu, sekutu Anwar dapat DNAA. Siasatan ICIJ Pandora Papers mengaitkan Daim dengan amanah luar pesisir $52 juta — jadi mungkin ada substansi dalam siasatan. Tetapi aplikasi keadilan yang terpilih memastikan siasatan yang sah pun dicemari syak politik.

🎯 Panggilan Kepada PH

Korang nak kredit kejar Daim? Boleh. Tapi jelaskan kenapa korang gugurkan 47 pertuduhan Zahid tiga bulan sebelum tu. Jelaskan kenapa Azam Baki — orang yang ada skandal aset sendiri — yang ketuai siasatan ini. Korang tak boleh dakwa lawan dan lindungi kawan dan panggil ia Reformasi. Rakyat nampak coraknya. Sama ada semua orang tertakluk kepada undang-undang, atau tiada siapa. Sekarang ni, nampak macam undang-undang hanya terpakai kepada orang yang menyusahkan politik Anwar Ibrahim.

MACC Seizes Daim's Ilham Tower in Anti-Money Laundering Probe — Selective Prosecution or Genuine Accountability?

Said by: Azam Baki, MACC Chief Commissioner / Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2023-12-21

📌 What Happened

On December 21, 2023, MACC seized the 60-storey Ilham Tower in Kuala Lumpur — worth an estimated RM1 to RM1.5 billion — under Section 51 of the Anti-Money Laundering Act (AMLA). The seizure was part of a widening probe into former finance minister Tun Daim Zainuddin over a 1997 corporate transaction worth RM2.3 billion involving Renong Bhd and United Engineers Malaysia (UEM), both linked to UMNO. MACC Chief Azam Baki confirmed the seizure. Daim denied any wrongdoing and maintained that the investigations were politically motivated. MACC also charged Daim and his wife Naimah Abdul Khalid for failing to declare assets, and issued notices to locate four of Daim’s children.

🔥 Why It Matters

On paper, investigating a former finance minister’s hidden wealth is exactly what an anti-corruption government should do. But the context matters: Daim was a key ally of Mahathir, who is now politically opposed to Anwar. Mahathir’s allies — Daim, various opposition figures — faced MACC scrutiny under Anwar’s watch, while Anwar’s own coalition partner Zahid had 47 charges dropped months earlier. The pattern is unmistakable: MACC goes hard on political opponents and soft on political allies. This isn’t anti-corruption — it’s the weaponization of prosecution.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

The same MACC chief, Azam Baki, who aggressively pursued Daim is the same man who faced his own shareholding scandal and was protected by Anwar’s government. The same AG’s office that signed off on the Daim probe also signed off on dropping Zahid’s 47 charges. If MACC were genuinely independent, the public might celebrate the Daim investigation as a win. But when the anti-corruption agency’s chief is himself mired in controversy, and the government picks and chooses which corruption to prosecute based on political alignment, the whole exercise looks like state power dressed up as accountability.

📜 Previous Cases

Selective prosecution has a long pedigree in Malaysia. Under Najib, MACC was accused of targeting opposition figures while ignoring BN-linked corruption. Under Mahathir 2.0, UMNO leaders faced charges that conveniently served political goals. Under Anwar, the target list shifted again: Mahathir’s allies get raided, Anwar’s allies get DNAA. The ICIJ’s Pandora Papers investigation linked Daim to $52 million in offshore trusts — so there may be genuine substance to the probe. But the selective application of justice ensures that even legitimate investigations are tainted by political suspicion.

🎯 PH Callout

You want credit for going after Daim? Fine. Then explain why you dropped Zahid’s 47 charges three months earlier. Explain why Azam Baki — a man with his own asset scandals — is leading this probe. You cannot prosecute your opponents and protect your allies and call it Reformasi. The rakyat sees the pattern. Either everyone is subject to the law, or nobody is. Right now, it looks like the law only applies to people who are politically inconvenient to Anwar Ibrahim.

[BM] AG Gugurkan Semua 47 Pertuduhan Terhadap Zahid Hamidi Tengah Perbicaraan — Anwar Kata 'Tidak Campur Tangan,' Tiada Siapa Percaya

Said by: Ahmad Zahid Hamidi, Deputy Prime Minister / Attorney General’s Chambers

Category: Politics

Date: 2023-09-04

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Pada 4 September 2023, Jabatan Peguam Negara memohon untuk menghentikan semua 47 pertuduhan rasuah terhadap Timbalan Perdana Menteri Ahmad Zahid Hamidi — 12 pertuduhan pecah amanah jenayah, 27 pengubahan wang haram, dan 8 rasuah melibatkan puluhan juta ringgit daripada Yayasan Akalbudi. Mahkamah Tinggi memberikan Pelepasan Tanpa Pembebasan (DNAA). Ini berlaku selepas Hakim Collin Lawrence Sequerah sudah memutuskan bahawa pihak pendakwaan berjaya membuktikan kes prima facie terhadap semua pertuduhan, dan perbicaraan sudah di peringkat pembelaan. Pendakwaan memetik 11 alasan termasuk keperluan ‘siasatan lebih menyeluruh.’ Perdana Menteri Anwar Ibrahim menafikan sebarang campur tangan, berkata ‘AG mempunyai kuasa untuk mengendalikan kes tersebut.’

🔥 Kenapa Ini Penting

Ini adalah pukulan paling merosakkan terhadap kredibiliti antirasuah PH sejak berkuasa. Zahid adalah presiden UMNO dan pengerusi BN — rakan gabungan paling penting Anwar. Tanpa kerusi parlimen UMNO, kerajaan perpaduan runtuh. Masa — tengah perbicaraan, selepas keputusan prima facie — tidak pernah berlaku dan luar biasa dari segi undang-undang. Majlis Peguam Malaysia mengambil langkah luar biasa memfailkan semakan kehakiman terhadap keputusan AG. Seluruh jenama politik Anwar dibina atas perjuangan menentang rasuah. Membiarkan rakan gabungannya terlepas dari 47 pertuduhan memusnahkan jenama itu dalam sehari.

💩 Kenapa Ini Karut

Pertahanan Anwar ‘saya tidak campur tangan’ adalah helah paling lama dalam buku panduan naungan: anda tidak perlu angkat telefon apabila seluruh sistem faham bahawa TPM terlepas adalah syarat kelangsungan kerajaan anda. AG melapor kepada PM. PM perlukan Zahid. Pertuduhan digugurkan. Matematinya bercakap sendiri. PH menghabiskan sedekad menjerit tentang campur tangan AG di bawah Najib dan Muhyiddin — kini mereka mahu kita percaya pejabat yang sama bertindak secara bebas untuk membebaskan sekutu terpenting PM pada masa paling sesuai dari segi politik. Majlis Peguam tidak percaya. Rakyat tidak percaya. Satu-satunya yang percaya adalah mereka yang kelangsungan politik bergantung pada berpura-pura.

📜 Kes-Kes Sebelum Ini

Ini mengikut corak tepat tradisi ‘ahli politik tidak boleh disentuh’ Malaysia. Pertuduhan 1MDB Najib difailkan selepas BN hilang kuasa; pertuduhan Zahid digugurkan selepas UMNO sertai PH. Di bawah Mahathir 2.0, tiada pemimpin UMNO didakwa dengan serius sehingga angin politik berubah. Peranan dwi AG — penasihat undang-undang kerajaan DAN pendakwa raya — telah menjadi mekanisme campur tangan politik dalam pendakwaan sejak merdeka. PH janji untuk memisahkan peranan ini. Pada 2023, mereka belum buat. Menjelang 2026, masih belum.

🎯 Panggilan Kepada PH

Korang kata korang berbeza. Korang kata Reformasi bermakna tiada lagi urus niaga politik, tiada lagi pemimpin kebal, tiada lagi pendakwaan terpilih. Lepas tu kerajaan korang bebaskan seorang lelaki yang hadapi 47 pertuduhan rasuah sebab korang perlukan kerusi partinya. Setiap ceramah, setiap sepanduk, setiap slogan tentang tadbir urus bersih — inilah hari ia mati. Korang tak boleh berkempen atas platform antirasuah dan kemudian berdagang pendakwaan untuk aritmetik parlimen. Rakyat ingat.

AG Drops All 47 Charges Against Zahid Hamidi Mid-Trial — Anwar Says He 'Did Not Interfere,' Nobody Believes Him

Said by: Ahmad Zahid Hamidi, Deputy Prime Minister / Attorney General’s Chambers

Category: Politics

Date: 2023-09-04

📌 What Happened

On September 4, 2023, the Attorney General’s Chambers applied to discontinue all 47 corruption charges against Deputy Prime Minister Ahmad Zahid Hamidi — 12 counts of criminal breach of trust, 27 of money laundering, and 8 of bribery involving tens of millions from his Yayasan Akalbudi charitable foundation. The High Court granted a Discharge Not Amounting to Acquittal (DNAA). This was after presiding judge Justice Collin Lawrence Sequerah had already ruled that the prosecution had established a prima facie case on all charges, and the trial was already at the defence stage. The prosecution cited 11 reasons including the need for ‘more comprehensive investigations.’ Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim denied any involvement, saying ’the AG had the powers to handle the case.’

🔥 Why It Matters

This was the single most damaging blow to PH’s anti-corruption credibility since taking power. Zahid is the president of UMNO and chairman of Barisan Nasional — Anwar’s essential coalition partner. Without UMNO’s parliamentary seats, the unity government collapses. The timing — mid-trial, after a prima facie ruling — was unprecedented and legally extraordinary. The Malaysian Bar took the extraordinary step of filing a judicial review against the AG’s decision, calling it unjustifiable. Anwar’s entire political brand was built on fighting corruption. Letting his coalition partner walk free from 47 charges shattered that brand in a single day.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

MCMC Blocks Opposition Media Sites Before State Elections — Fahmi Says 'I Gave No Instruction,' RSF Calls It Censorship

Said by: Fahmi Fadzil, Communications Minister / MCMC

Category: Politics

Date: 2023-08-07

📌 What Happened

In August 2023, in the lead-up to six state elections, Malaysia’s MCMC blocked access to at least four pro-opposition news sites — MalaysiaNow, UtusanTV, Malaysia Today, and TV Pertiwi — as well as blogger Wee Choo Keong’s site. Users were redirected to an MCMC IP address. None of the outlets received any warning or explanation of which articles triggered the blocks. Communications Minister Fahmi Fadzil denied giving any instruction to MCMC, saying ‘I have given no instruction to MCMC to block anyone.’ Reporters Without Borders (RSF) issued a statement slamming the crackdowns and urging Fahmi to end such censorship. While blocks on MalaysiaNow and UtusanTV were lifted within days after the elections, Malaysia Today and other sites remained blocked long after.

🔥 Why It Matters

PH came to power in GE14 in 2018 partly on a promise to end media repression. The Economist ranked Malaysia the most democratic country in Southeast Asia that year. Five years later, PH is blocking opposition media right before elections — the exact playbook of the BN regimes they replaced. The timing was not subtle: block critical voices before voting day, unblock some after results are in. This signalled that PH’s commitment to press freedom was conditional — free when it suits them, restricted when the polls are tight.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

Fahmi’s ‘I gave no instruction’ defence is the plausible deniability playbook. MCMC falls under his ministry. The blocks happened during the most politically sensitive period of 2023. If the Communications Minister doesn’t know who ordered his own agency to block news sites days before an election, he is either incompetent or lying — and neither answer is acceptable. PH in opposition screamed when BN blocked media. PH in government says ’not my fault, MCMC acted independently.’ The hypocrisy is breathtaking. RSF saw right through it. The international community saw right through it. Only Fahmi pretends there’s nothing to see.

📜 Previous Cases

Media suppression is a bipartisan Malaysian tradition. BN blocked The Malaysian Insider and Sarawak Report. Muhyiddin’s government pressured Al Jazeera and detained journalists. PH promised to repeal the Printing Presses and Publications Act, the Sedition Act, and Section 233 of the CMA. None have been repealed. Instead, PH has been using the same laws — sometimes more aggressively — than their predecessors, as documented by the US State Department’s 2023 human rights report on Malaysia.

🎯 PH Callout

You promised media freedom. You promised to end MCMC censorship. You promised the rakyat that a PH government would never block news sites for political convenience. Then you blocked four outlets right before an election and told us it wasn’t you. If your ‘reform’ government cannot even guarantee that journalists can publish without being blocked by the state, you are not reformers — you are the same authoritarian impulse wearing a different jersey.
