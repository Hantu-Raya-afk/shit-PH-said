18,000 March to Oust Anwar — 'Turun Anwar' Rally Fills Dataran Merdeka Over Broken Promises

Said by: Malaysian Public / Opposition Coalition

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-07-26

📌 WHAT HAPPENED

On 26 July 2025, an estimated 18,000 people filled Dataran Merdeka (Independence Square) in Kuala Lumpur, wearing black t-shirts and bandannas with the slogan “Turun Anwar” (Step Down Anwar). It was the first major anti-government protest since Anwar came to power in 2022.

Reuters reported: “Thousands of people rallied in the Malaysian capital to demand the resignation of Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim, as public discontent grows over rising living costs and allegations of failing to deliver promised reforms.”

Former PM Mahathir Mohamad, aged 100, was the keynote speaker. He told the crowd: “He gave everyone RM100 but raised taxes instead. He doesn’t understand how badly the lower-income groups are suffering.”

Former PM Muhyiddin Yassin said people had been complaining about price hikes from the expanded sales and service tax.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS

18,000 people don’t show up in the KL heat to protest for fun. This rally was a referendum on broken promises. The protest’s demands read like a scorecard of PH failures:

  • Cost of living not reduced
  • GE15 manifesto promises broken
  • Prices rising, salaries stagnant
  • Cronyism and nepotism worsening
  • Madani policy is “played on paper” only
  • Education and health neglected
  • The spirit of reformasi betrayed
  • Young people losing hope on housing and jobs Bloomberg’s headline said it all: “Anwar’s Broken Promises Spark Protests Demanding His Ouster.”

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT

When Anwar was in opposition, he celebrated street protests as the voice of the rakyat. He marched with Bersih. He called for the right to peaceful assembly. Now that the streets are against him, the government’s response was to frame it as opposition-organised troublemaking.

The protest organisers listed 16 specific grievances. Not one was about race or religion. They were all about governance, economics, and accountability. This was a multiracial crowd united by disappointment in a government that promised everything and delivered excuses.

Al Jazeera noted it was “the first major protest since Anwar was propelled to power.” The significance is clear: the honeymoon is over.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES

Malaysia has a proud tradition of street protest driving political change. Bersih rallies from 2007-2016 were instrumental in building the momentum that eventually toppled BN. Anwar was a beneficiary of that movement. Now the movement has turned on him. The irony is poetic.

🎯 PH CALLOUT

18,000 Malaysians stood in the sun to tell you they’re disappointed. Not angry at the opposition. Not manipulated by foreign agents. Disappointed in you. They marched because you promised reform and delivered status quo. You promised lower costs and delivered SST expansion. You promised clean governance and delivered Zahid’s DNAA. The streets that carried you to power are now asking you to leave. Are you listening?

Said by: MACC (Malaysian Anti-Corruption Commission)

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-07-11

Merampas RM3 Bilion Daripada Keluarga Si Mati

Pada Julai 2025, SPRM mengumumkan ia sedang berusaha merampas dan melucuthakkan lebih daripada RM3 bilion aset merentasi 11 negara yang dikaitkan dengan mendiang Daim Zainuddin, keluarganya dan rakan-rakannya. Daim telah meninggal dunia lapan bulan sebelumnya, mengekalkan ketidasalahannya.

Fakta-Fakta

Fakta: Daim berkhidmat dua kali sebagai Menteri Kewangan dan dikreditkan membantu Malaysia pulih daripada Krisis Kewangan Asia 1997.

Fakta: Beliau didakwa pada Januari 2024 kerana gagal mengisytihar aset, berikutan pendedahan Pandora Papers. Pertuduhan digugurkan selepas kematiannya pada November 2024.

Fakta: Walaupun beliau meninggal dunia, SPRM meneruskan siasatan dan mengeluarkan notis pelucuthakan baharu pada Disember 2025.

Fakta: Daim menafikan semua kesalahan, berkata siasatan itu “bermotif politik.”

Fakta: Bloomberg bertanya terus: “Bolehkah Malaysia merampas $700 juta daripada tokoh perniagaan yang sudah meninggal?”

Mengapa Ia Penting

  • Jika kekayaan Daim diperolehi secara haram, pelucuthakan adalah wajar. Tetapi mengejar keluarga si mati sementara 47 pertuduhan Zahid digugurkan menimbulkan persoalan tidak selesa tentang keadilan terpilih.

MACC Seeks to Seize RM3 Billion in Daim Family Assets — Critics Ask If It's Justice or Vendetta

Said by: MACC (Malaysian Anti-Corruption Commission)

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-07-11

Seizing RM3 Billion From a Dead Man’s Family

In July 2025, MACC announced it was seeking to seize and forfeit more than RM3 billion in assets across 11 countries linked to the late Daim Zainuddin, his family, and associates. Daim had died eight months earlier, maintaining his innocence.

The Facts

Fact: Daim served twice as Finance Minister and was credited with helping Malaysia recover from the 1997 Asian Financial Crisis.

Fact: He was charged in January 2024 for failing to declare assets, following Pandora Papers revelations. The charge was dropped after his death in November 2024.

Fact: Despite his death, MACC continued investigations and issued fresh forfeiture notices in December 2025.

Fact: Daim had denied all wrongdoing, saying the investigations were “politically motivated.”

Fact: Bloomberg asked directly: “Could Malaysia seize $700 million from a dead tycoon?”

Why It Matters

  • If Daim’s wealth was illegally obtained, forfeiture is justified. But pursuing a dead man’s family while Zahid’s 47 charges were dropped raises uncomfortable questions about selective justice.
  • MACC’s credibility depends on consistency. Going after Daim’s billions while defending Azam Baki’s share scandal looks like political targeting. *Sources: Bloomberg · Bloomberg · *Malay Mail

[BM] Perbicaraan Rasuah Lim Guan Eng — Pegawai SPRM Beri Keterangan Mengenai Wang Diberi Kepada Bekas KM Pulau Pinang

Said by: Lim Guan Eng, former Penang CM / MACC

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-07-10

📌 APA YANG BERLAKU: Pada 10 Julai 2025, seorang pegawai penyiasat SPRM memberitahu Mahkamah Sesyen bahawa semasa siasatan penipuan pada Disember 2019, Datuk Zarul Ahmad Mohd Zulkifli — ketika itu pengarah Consortium Zenith Construction (CZCSB) — mendedahkan beliau telah memberikan wang kepada kedua-dua G. Gnanaraja dan Lim Guan Eng berkaitan projek terowong dasar laut Pulau Pinang. Penguasa Kanan SPRM Ng Heng Jun mengesahkan beliau telah memaklumkan pegawai atasan mengenai kenyataan Zarul. Projek terowong itu, bernilai RM6.3 bilion, telah digambarkan oleh saksi SPRM sebagai ‘serupa penipuan tanah.’

🔥 MENGAPA IA PENTING: Lim Guan Eng adalah pemimpin paling terkenal DAP dan Ketua Menteri Pulau Pinang selama 10 tahun. Beliau adalah wajah model tadbir urus DAP. Jika dakwaan rasuah terowong dibuktikan, ia bukan sahaja membabitkan seorang ahli politik — ia membabitkan keseluruhan legasi DAP di Pulau Pinang. Projek RM6.3 bilion yang saksi SPRM gambarkan sebagai ‘penipuan tanah’ akan menjadi kes rasuah terbesar dikaitkan dengan pemimpin DAP dalam sejarah parti.

💩 MENGAPA IA MENGARUT: DAP telah memposisikan diri sebagai parti antirasuah selama berdekad. Keseluruhan jenamanya adalah tadbir urus bersih dan meritokrasi. Perbicaraan terowong Pulau Pinang mengancam meruntuhkan jenama itu. Dan respons DAP? Senyap daripada kepimpinan kebangsaan. Tiada tuntutan terbuka untuk akauntabiliti penuh. Tiada semakan bebas proses perolehan Pulau Pinang di bawah Guan Eng. Parti yang menuntut akauntabiliti daripada semua orang lain tidak mampu menuntutnya daripada diri sendiri.

📜 KES-KES TERDAHULU: Projek terowong diluluskan semasa pemerintahan Guan Eng, menggunakan mekanisme ‘permintaan cadangan’ dan bukannya tender terbuka — kaedah perolehan yang pengkritik kata memihak syarikat berkait. Kekaburan perolehan yang sama dikritik dalam Pelan Induk Pengangkutan Pulau Pinang (PTMP). Di bawah DAP, projek mega Pulau Pinang secara konsisten mengelak proses tender terbuka.

🎯 SERUAN PH: Bekas KM Pulau Pinang anda sedang dibicarakan atas rasuah berkaitan projek RM6.3 bilion yang SPRM panggil ‘penipuan tanah.’ KM Pulau Pinang semasa anda kata projek terowong itu ‘masih aktif.’ Parti anda tidak berkata apa-apa bermakna tentang akauntabiliti. Sama ada tuntut ketelusan penuh daripada pemimpin sendiri atau berhenti berpura-pura DAP adalah parti antirasuah. Anda tidak boleh dua-dua.

Lim Guan Eng Corruption Trial — MACC Officer Testifies About Money Given to Former Penang CM

Said by: Lim Guan Eng, former Penang CM / MACC

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-07-10

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: On 10 July 2025, a MACC investigating officer told the Sessions Court that during a fraud investigation in December 2019, Datuk Zarul Ahmad Mohd Zulkifli — then director of Consortium Zenith Construction (CZCSB) — revealed he had given money to both G. Gnanaraja and Lim Guan Eng in connection with the Penang undersea tunnel project. MACC Senior Superintendent Ng Heng Jun confirmed he had informed his superiors about Zarul’s statement. The tunnel project, worth RM6.3 billion, has been described by MACC witnesses as ‘akin to a land scam.’

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: Lim Guan Eng was DAP’s most prominent leader and Penang’s Chief Minister for 10 years. He’s the face of DAP’s governance model. If the tunnel corruption allegations are proven, it doesn’t just implicate one politician — it implicates DAP’s entire Penang legacy. A RM6.3 billion project that MACC witnesses describe as a ’land scam’ would be the biggest corruption case linked to a DAP leader in the party’s history.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: DAP has positioned itself as the anti-corruption party for decades. Its entire brand is clean governance and meritocracy. The Penang tunnel trial threatens to demolish that brand. And DAP’s response? Silence from the national leadership. No public demand for full accountability. No independent review of Penang’s procurement processes under Guan Eng. The party that demands accountability from everyone else cannot bring itself to demand it from its own.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: The tunnel project was approved during Guan Eng’s tenure, using a ‘request for proposal’ mechanism rather than open tender — a procurement method that critics say favours connected companies. The same procurement opacity was criticised in the Penang Transport Master Plan (PTMP). Under DAP, Penang’s mega-projects consistently avoided open tender processes.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: Your former Penang CM is on trial for corruption related to a RM6.3 billion project that MACC calls a ’land scam.’ Your current Penang CM says the tunnel project is ‘still active.’ Your party has said nothing meaningful about accountability. Either demand full transparency from your own leaders or stop pretending DAP is the anti-corruption party. You can’t have it both ways.

[BM] GLC Sebagai Tempat Buang Politik — 47.5 Lantikan Setahun, Lebih Banyak Daripada Mahathir 2.0

Said by: IDEAS Think Tank Research

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-07-04

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Penyelidikan badan pemikir IDEAS mendapati kerajaan Anwar melantik purata 47.5 lantikan politik setahun ke Syarikat Berkaitan Kerajaan (GLC) dan badan berkanun — melebihi kadar Mahathir 2.0 sebanyak 43 setahun dan jauh melebihi matlamat lantikan berasaskan merit. Sebanyak 238 lantikan politik didokumenkan sepanjang tahun-tahun pertama Anwar.

🔥 Mengapa Ia Penting

GLC mengawal bahagian besar ekonomi Malaysia — Petronas, Khazanah, TNB, Telekom, MAHB, dan berpuluh-puluh lagi. Apabila jawatan lembaga pengarah diisi berdasarkan kesetiaan politik dan bukannya kecekapan, akibatnya besar: keputusan strategik yang lemah, rasuah, dan salah urus. Setiap lantikan politik yang kurang kemahiran untuk peranan mereka merugikan ekonomi dari segi kecekapan, inovasi, dan nilai pemegang saham.

💩 Mengapa Ia Mengarut

PH bertanding atas platform JELAS menamatkan naungan politik dalam GLC. Anwar sendiri mengkritik amalan BN. Beliau menjanjikan lantikan berasaskan merit dan akauntabiliti prestasi. Sebaliknya, beliau melantik sekutu politik pada kadar LEBIH PANTAS daripada pendahulunya. Reformis yang berjanji menamatkan naungan telah mengindustrikannya.

📜 Kes-Kes Terdahulu

Di bawah Najib, lantikan GLC adalah tunjang naungan UMNO. Skandal 1MDB sendiri sebahagiannya dimungkinkan oleh lembaga yang dilantik secara politik yang gagal dalam pengawasan. Di bawah Mahathir 2.0, 43/tahun. Di bawah Anwar, 47.5/tahun. Setiap kerajaan menjanjikan reformasi; setiap kerajaan mengukuhkan naungan lebih dalam.

🎯 Seruan PH

47.5 lantikan politik setahun. LEBIH daripada kerajaan Mahathir. Anda tidak mereformasi tadbir urus GLC — anda mempercepatkan mesin naungan. Setiap lantikan politik tidak layak di lembaga GLC mewakili seorang profesional berkelayakan yang dilepaskan. Ini adalah rasuah dengan langkah tambahan.

[BM] Reformasi Perkhidmatan Awam: Tiga Tahun Bercakap, Sifar Perubahan Struktur — 1.6 Juta Birokrat Tidak Disentuh

Said by: IDEAS / Public Policy Analysts

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-07-04

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Anwar Ibrahim memasuki pejabat menjanjikan reformasi perkhidmatan awam menyeluruh — pemodenan, meritokrasi, mengecilkan birokrasi yang gemuk yang memakan bahagian besar belanjawan negara. Tiga tahun kemudian, perkhidmatan awam kekal pada dasarnya tidak berubah: 1.3-1.6 juta pekerja (tidak termasuk polis dan tentera), 90% Bumiputera, memakan kira-kira 40% daripada jumlah perbelanjaan mengurus kerajaan dalam gaji dan pencen.

🔥 Mengapa Ia Penting

Badan pemikir IDEAS mendapati kerajaan Anwar sebenarnya meningkatkan lantikan politik ke GLC dan badan berkanun, purata 47.5 setahun — melebihi kadar Mahathir 2.0 sebanyak 43 setahun. Birokrasi yang gemuk bermakna Malaysia membelanjakan lebih banyak untuk gaji penjawat awam berbanding perbelanjaan pembangunan. Kewajipan pencen terus membengkak tanpa reformasi struktur.

💩 Mengapa Ia Mengarut

Cadangan untuk penilaian berasaskan prestasi, pengecilan saiz, dan kenaikan pangkat berasaskan merit tidak ke mana kerana undi perkhidmatan awam adalah penting secara politik — tiada kerajaan mahu menggusarkan 1.6 juta pengundi dan keluarga mereka. Reformasi sentiasa pembohongan; perkhidmatan awam adalah tabung undi PH sama seperti BN dahulu.

🎯 Seruan PH

Anda janji meritokrasi. Anda sampaikan lebih banyak lantikan politik daripada Mahathir. Anda janji pengecilan saiz. Birokrasi membesar. Anda janji pemodenan. Ketidakcekapan yang sama berterusan. Pada satu ketika, ‘reformasi’ berhenti menjadi janji dan menjadi bahan jenaka. Anda sudah melepasi titik itu dua tahun lalu.

Civil Service Reform: Three Years of Talk, Zero Structural Change — 1.6 Million Bureaucrats Untouched

Said by: IDEAS / Public Policy Analysts

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-07-04

📌 What Happened

Anwar Ibrahim entered office promising sweeping civil service reform — modernisation, meritocracy, right-sizing a bloated bureaucracy that consumes a massive share of the national budget. Three years later, the civil service remains essentially unchanged: 1.3-1.6 million employees (excluding police and military), 90% Bumiputera, consuming approximately 40% of total government operating expenditure in salaries and pensions.

🔥 Why It Matters

IDEAS think tank found that the Anwar government actually increased political appointments to GLCs and statutory bodies, averaging 47.5 per year — exceeding even Mahathir 2.0’s rate of 43 per year. The bloated bureaucracy means Malaysia spends more on civil servant wages than on development spending. Pension obligations continue to balloon with no structural reform.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

Proposals for performance-based assessments, right-sizing, and merit-based promotions have gone nowhere because the civil service vote is politically crucial — no government wants to antagonise 1.6 million voters and their families. Reform was always a lie; the civil service is PH’s vote bank just as it was BN’s.

🎯 PH Callout

You promised meritocracy. You delivered more political appointments than Mahathir. You promised right-sizing. The bureaucracy grew. You promised modernisation. The same inefficiencies persist. At some point, ‘reform’ stops being a promise and becomes a punchline. You passed that point two years ago.
