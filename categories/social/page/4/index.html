[BM] Anthony Loke Akui Masyarakat Malaysia 'Terpolarisasi' — Tapi Salahkan Pembangkang Mainkan Isu Kaum

Said by: Anthony Loke, Transport Minister

Category: Social

Date: 2025-09-11

Terpolarisasi, Tapi Jangan Salahkan Kami

Pada September 2025, Menteri Pengangkutan Anthony Loke memberitahu rakyat Malaysia di Hong Kong bahawa masyarakat telah menjadi “lebih terpolarisasi” dengan retorik kaum yang semakin sengit. Diagnosisnya tepat. Preskripsinya — salahkan pembangkang — adalah ketidakjujuran intelektual.

Fakta-Fakta

Fakta: Di sebuah acara di Hong Kong, Loke mengakui bahawa Malaysia semakin berpecah mengikut garisan kaum, kelas, dan pendidikan, dengan media sosial menguatkan ucapan kebencian dan maklumat palsu.

Fakta: Beliau mengaitkan polarisasi terutamanya kepada “pembangkang yang memainkan isu-isu sedemikian untuk modal politik” — seolah-olah kerajaan tidak menanggung sebarang tanggungjawab terhadap keretakan tersebut.

Fakta: Ini datang daripada kerajaan yang merombak kabinetnya mengikut garisan kaum pada Disember 2025, memberikan UMNO lebih banyak jawatan untuk mengukuhkan sokongan Melayu sambil mengurangkan kehadiran DAP.

Fakta: Garis panduan antara agama yang dicadangkan JAKIM pada 2024 — yang menyekat cara bukan Islam boleh terlibat dalam dialog agama — memecahbelahkan komuniti dan dikritik meluas oleh kumpulan masyarakat sivil sebagai diskriminasi.

Fakta: Kerajaan tidak membuat sebarang langkah untuk mereformasi dasar tindakan afirmatif berasaskan kaum (kuota era DEB dalam pendidikan, perolehan, dan perumahan), walaupun manifesto PH menjanjikan pendekatan “berasaskan keperluan”.

Fakta: Kerajaan Anwar sangat bergantung kepada retorik kaum dan agama apabila sesuai — termasuk ucapan Melayu-dahulu Anwar sendiri di perhimpunan agung UMNO, pembelaannya terhadap “hak istimewa” Melayu-Islam, dan penggunaan Akta Hasutan oleh gabungannya terhadap mereka yang mempersoalkan dasar kaum.

Fakta: Laporan SCMP menyatakan bahawa walaupun optimisme Loke tentang “mengatasi cabaran,” metrik polarisasi kaum Malaysia — diukur melalui data tinjauan tentang kepercayaan antara etnik — telah bertambah buruk sejak 2023.

Mengapa Ia Penting

  • Mengakui negara terpolarisasi sambil menyalahkan hanya pembangkang adalah setara politik dengan mendiagnosis penyakit dan menetapkan ubat orang lain. Kerajaan mengawal dasar, pelantikan, pengawalseliaan media, dan jentera negara. Jika polarisasi semakin buruk semasa pemerintahan anda, anda yang bertanggungjawab.
  • Tindakan kerajaan sendiri telah menyumbang secara aktif kepada perpecahan: rombakan kabinet yang mengutamakan pengurusan gabungan kaum berbanding kecekapan, garis panduan antara agama yang mengasingkan minoriti, dasar berasaskan kaum berterusan yang PH janji untuk direformasi, dan PM yang bertukar kod antara retorik reformasi untuk penonton antarabangsa dan retorik etno-agama untuk khalayak domestik.
  • Loke membuat kenyataan ini di Hong Kong — bukan di Malaysia. Ahli politik Malaysia sangat terus terang tentang kegagalan domestik apabila berucap kepada penonton asing. Di tanah air, masalah yang sama dinafikan atau dialihkan. Jurang antara apa yang mereka katakan di luar negara dan apa yang mereka lakukan di dalam negara adalah ukuran sebenar tadbir urus.
  • Pembangkang memang memainkan kad kaum. Populisme etno-agama PN memang nyata dan berbahaya. Tetapi menyalahkan PN atas polarisasi sementara kerajaan anda sendiri mengekalkan dasar berasaskan kaum, mengambil hati konservatif agama, dan menggunakan undang-undang hasutan terhadap mereka yang mencabar ortodoksi kaum bukanlah analisis — ia adalah pengalihan.
  • Jika seorang menteri DAP — dari parti yang sepatutnya mewakili reformasi pelbagai kaum — tidak dapat mendiagnosis dengan jujur mengapa Malaysia terpolarisasi, maka PH telah sepenuhnya menyerap buku panduan BN: akui gejalanya, salahkan musuh, ubah tiada apa-apa. Sumber: SCMP

Anthony Loke Admits Malaysian Society Is 'Polarised' — But Blames Opposition for Playing Up Race

Said by: Anthony Loke, Transport Minister

Category: Social

Date: 2025-09-11

Polarised, But Don’t Blame Us

In September 2025, Transport Minister Anthony Loke told Malaysians in Hong Kong that society had become “more polarised” with intensifying race rhetoric. His diagnosis was accurate. His prescription — blame the opposition — was intellectually dishonest.

The Facts

Fact: At an event in Hong Kong, Loke acknowledged that Malaysia was increasingly divided along racial, class, and education lines, with social media amplifying hate speech and misinformation.

Fact: He attributed the polarisation primarily to “the opposition playing up such issues for political capital” — as though the government bore no responsibility for the fractures.

Fact: This came from a government that reshuffled its cabinet along racial lines in December 2025, giving UMNO more positions to shore up Malay support while reducing DAP’s presence.

Fact: JAKIM’s proposed interfaith guidelines in 2024 — which restricted how non-Muslims could engage in religious dialogue — divided communities and were widely criticised by civil society groups as discriminatory.

Fact: The government has made no move to reform race-based affirmative action policies (NEP-era quotas in education, procurement, and housing), despite PH’s manifesto promising a “needs-based” approach.

Fact: Anwar’s government has relied heavily on racial and religious rhetoric when convenient — including Anwar’s own Malay-first speeches at UMNO general assemblies, his defence of Malay-Muslim “special rights,” and his coalition’s use of the Sedition Act against those who question racial policies.

Fact: The SCMP report noted that despite Loke’s optimism about “overcoming challenges,” Malaysia’s racial polarisation metrics — measured by survey data on inter-ethnic trust — have worsened since 2023.

Why It Matters

  • Admitting the country is polarised while blaming only the opposition is the political equivalent of diagnosing the disease and prescribing someone else’s medicine. The government controls policy, appointments, media regulation, and the machinery of state. If polarisation is worsening on your watch, you own it.
  • The government’s own actions have actively contributed to division: a cabinet reshuffle that prioritised racial coalition management over competence, interfaith guidelines that alienated minorities, continued race-based policies that PH promised to reform, and a PM who code-switches between reform rhetoric for international audiences and ethno-religious rhetoric for domestic ones.
  • Loke made these remarks in Hong Kong — not in Malaysia. Malaysian politicians are remarkably candid about domestic failures when speaking to foreign audiences. At home, the same problems are denied or deflected. The gap between what they say abroad and what they do domestically is the real measure of governance.
  • The opposition absolutely plays the race card. PN’s ethno-religious populism is real and dangerous. But blaming PN for polarisation while your own government maintains race-based policies, panders to religious conservatives, and uses sedition laws against those who challenge racial orthodoxy is not analysis — it is deflection.
  • If even a DAP minister — from the party that was supposed to represent multiracial reform — cannot honestly diagnose why Malaysia is polarised, then PH has fully absorbed the BN playbook: acknowledge the symptom, blame the enemy, change nothing. *Source: *SCMP

[BM] Tebus Guna Pulau Selatan Pulau Pinang — Musnahkan Mata Pencarian 4,000 Nelayan Demi Impian Pemaju

Said by: Chow Kon Yeow, Penang Chief Minister / DAP

Category: Social

Date: 2025-08-20

📌 APA YANG BERLAKU: Projek tebus guna Pulau Selatan Pulau Pinang (PSI) — kini dijenamakan semula sebagai ‘Silicon Island’ — adalah pelan pembangunan mega kerajaan negeri pimpinan DAP untuk mencipta 930 hektar tanah buatan di luar pantai selatan Pulau Pinang. Sehingga Ogos 2025, 80 hektar telah ditebus guna. Projek ini asalnya tiga pulau, kemudian dikecilkan kepada satu selepas bantahan awam. Lebih 4,000 nelayan pesisir terjejas. Hanya RM11.7 juta telah dibayar kepada 791 nelayan di bawah Pelan Pengurusan Kesan Sosial, dan 118 daripada 295 nelayan aktif menerima bot dan enjin baru bernilai RM10 juta. Nelayan kalah dalam cabaran mahkamah terhadap kelulusan Penilaian Kesan Alam Sekitar.

🔥 MENGAPA IA PENTING: Ini adalah projek tebus guna tanah terbesar dalam sejarah Pulau Pinang, dan ia ditolak oleh DAP — parti yang meletakkan dirinya sebagai pro-rakyat dan pro-alam sekitar. Penyelidikan akademik mengesahkan projek ini menyebabkan ‘kehilangan kekal dan tidak boleh dipulihkan terhadap ekosistem.’ Nelayan yang telah bekerja di perairan ini selama bergenerasi sedang disingkirkan dengan pampasan minimum. Projek ini direka untuk memperluaskan Zon Perindustrian Bebas Bayan Lepas untuk pembuatan semikonduktor — objektif korporat yang dibiayai dengan memusnahkan mata pencarian komuniti.

💩 MENGAPA IA KARUT: DAP dalam pembangkang pasti akan memimpin protes menentang ini. Kerajaan negeri BN yang menebus guna 930 hektar laut, menyingkirkan ribuan nelayan, dan kalah kes mahkamah atas kerosakan alam sekitar pasti akan menjadi isu kempen impian DAP. Tetapi apabila DAP melakukannya? Ia ‘pembangunan untuk rakyat.’ Nelayan yang kehilangan mata pencarian bukan ‘rakyat’ dalam pengiraan DAP — mereka adalah kerosakan sampingan untuk angka FDI.

📜 KES TERDAHULU: Pulau Pinang mempunyai sejarah panjang tebus guna — Gurney Drive, Tanjung Tokong — tetapi tiada yang sebesar ini. Kumpulan masyarakat sivil termasuk SAM dan Penang Forum telah mendokumentasikan kemusnahan alam sekitar dan penyingkiran komuniti. Nelayan dan NGO ‘kecewa’ apabila cabaran mahkamah mereka gagal. Polanya jelas: DAP mentadbir Pulau Pinang seperti negeri pemaju, mengutamakan pelaburan berbanding komuniti.

🎯 TEGURAN PH: 4,000 nelayan disingkirkan. RM11.7 juta pampasan untuk 791 daripada mereka — itu RM14,800 setiap seorang untuk kehilangan mata pencarian. 930 hektar dasar laut dimusnahkan. Dan anda panggil ini pembangunan? Ini pengekstrakan — mengambil daripada laut, mengambil daripada nelayan, dan memberi kepada syarikat semikonduktor. Jika ini model tadbir urus PH, nelayan Pulau Pinang kini tahu tepat berapa nilai nyawa mereka bagi DAP.

Penang South Islands Reclamation — Destroying 4,000 Fishermen's Livelihoods for a Developer's Dream

Said by: Chow Kon Yeow, Penang Chief Minister / DAP

Category: Social

Date: 2025-08-20

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: The Penang South Islands (PSI) reclamation project — now rebranded as ‘Silicon Island’ — is the DAP-led state government’s mega-development plan to create 930 hectares of artificial land off Penang’s southern coast. As of August 2025, 80 hectares had been reclaimed. The project was originally three islands, later downsized to one after public outcry. More than 4,000 coastal fishermen are affected. Only RM11.7 million has been paid to 791 fishermen under the Social Impact Management Plan, and 118 of 295 active fishermen received new boats and engines worth RM10 million. Fishermen lost their court challenge to the Environmental Impact Assessment approval.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: This is the largest land reclamation project in Penang’s history, and it’s being pushed through by DAP — the party that positions itself as pro-people and pro-environment. Academic research confirms the project causes ‘permanent and irreversible loss to the ecosystem.’ Fishermen who have worked these waters for generations are being displaced with minimal compensation. The project is designed to extend Bayan Lepas Free Industrial Zone for semiconductor manufacturing — a corporate objective funded by destroying a community’s livelihood.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: DAP in opposition would have led protests against this. A BN state government reclaiming 930 hectares of sea, displacing thousands of fishermen, and losing court cases over environmental damage would have been DAP’s dream campaign issue. But when DAP does it? It’s ‘development for the people.’ The fishermen who lost their livelihoods are not ’the people’ in DAP’s calculation — they’re collateral damage for FDI numbers.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: Penang has a long history of reclamation — Gurney Drive, Tanjung Tokong — but nothing on this scale. Civil society groups including SAM and Penang Forum have documented environmental destruction and community displacement. Fishermen and NGOs were ‘dismayed’ when their court challenge failed. The pattern is clear: DAP governs Penang like a developer-state, prioritising investment over communities.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: 4,000 fishermen displaced. RM11.7 million in compensation for 791 of them — that’s RM14,800 each for losing a livelihood. 930 hectares of seabed destroyed. And you call this development? This is extraction — taking from the sea, taking from the fishermen, and giving to semiconductor companies. If this is PH’s model of governance, the fishermen of Penang now know exactly how much their lives are worth to DAP.

[BM] Institut Keamanan Toda: Anwar 'Jadikan Dasar Berasaskan Kaum LEBIH BURUK, Bukan Lebih Baik' — Cuba Mengatasi Islamis Dalam Pengislaman

Category: Social

🔥 Mengapa Ia Penting

Ini adalah institut penyelidikan keamanan antarabangsa yang menyatakan bahawa PM Malaysia yang kononnya reformis sedang MEMBURUKKAN diskriminasi kaum, bukan mengurangkannya. Sistem dasar berasaskan kaum yang telah dilembagakan — yang digambarkan sebagai ‘membunuh merit, menyekat persaingan dan inovasi, membiakkan rasa tidak puas hati minoriti, dan melahirkan kronisme dengan mencipta elit Melayu kaya tanpa berbuat banyak untuk Melayu miskin’ — sedang diperkukuh bukannya direformasi di bawah Anwar.

💩 Mengapa Ia Karut

Gerakan Reformasi Anwar secara jelas ‘menuntut pengakhiran sistem diskriminasi kaum yang dilembagakan ini.’ Itu adalah JANJI TERAS. Bukan komitmen sampingan — sebab asas mengapa minoriti menyokongnya. Kenaikannya ‘secara wajar mencetuskan harapan keadilan kaum.’ Sebaliknya, beliau bersaing dengan PAS tentang siapa boleh lebih Melayu-Islam, sementara minoriti yang mengundi PH secara beramai-ramai mendapat retorik kosong dan peluang bergambar dengan pengumuman peruntukan sekolah Cina sekali-sekala.

📜 Kes Terdahulu

Pada April 2025, apabila seorang profesor UiTM mencadangkan membuka latihan pembedahan kardiotoraks kepada pelajar bukan Bumiputera (kerana rakyat mati akibat kekurangan pakar bedah), kerajaan terpaku. Keperluan wajib Bahasa Melayu untuk pekerja asing dikenakan. Dasar Bumiputera diisytiharkan ’tidak boleh dirunding’ dalam rundingan perdagangan AS. ICERD kekal tidak diratifikasi. Perkhidmatan awam kekal 90% Bumiputera.

🎯 Teguran PH

Anda meminta rakyat Malaysia bukan Melayu mempercayai anda. Mereka melakukannya — mereka mengundi PH dalam jumlah besar. Sebagai balasan, anda bukan sahaja mengekalkan setiap dasar diskriminasi tetapi, menurut analisis bebas, menjadikan keadaan LEBIH BURUK. Anda telah menukar hak minoriti untuk undi Melayu-Islam. Setiap rakyat Malaysia minoriti yang mengundi PH layak mendapat permohonan maaf — dan penjelasan mengapa kepercayaan mereka dijadikan senjata terhadap kepentingan mereka sendiri.

Toda Peace Institute: Anwar 'Making Race-Based Policies WORSE, Not Better' — Trying to Out-Islamise the Islamists

Said by: Toda Peace Institute Analysis

Category: Social

Date: 2025-08-01

📌 What Happened

The Toda Peace Institute published a comprehensive analysis titled ‘The Many Wars of Malaysia’s Anwar Ibrahim,’ concluding that Anwar ‘isn’t just reluctant to disturb the status quo; he is in fact making it worse by trying to out-Islamize the Islamists with gestures, policies and statements that the minorities find deeply worrying.’

🔥 Why It Matters

This is an international peace research institute saying that Malaysia’s supposedly reformist PM is WORSENING racial discrimination, not reducing it. The institutionalised system of race-based policies — described as ‘killing off merit, stifling competition and innovation, breeding minority resentments, and spawning cronyism by creating a rich Malay elite without doing much for poor Malays’ — is being strengthened rather than reformed under Anwar.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

Anwar’s Reformasi movement explicitly ‘sought an end to this system of institutionalized racial discrimination.’ That was the CORE PROMISE. Not a peripheral commitment — the fundamental reason minorities supported him. His ascent ‘understandably triggered hopes of racial justice.’ Instead, he’s competing with PAS on who can be more Malay-Muslim, while minorities who voted PH in droves get empty rhetoric and photo ops with the occasional Chinese school allocation announcement.

📜 Previous Cases

In April 2025, when a UiTM professor suggested opening cardiothoracic surgery training to non-Bumiputera students (because people were dying from surgeon shortages), the government was paralysed. The mandatory Malay language requirement for foreign workers was imposed. Bumiputera policies were declared ’non-negotiable’ in US trade talks. ICERD remains unratified. The civil service remains 90% Bumiputera.

🎯 PH Callout

You asked non-Malay Malaysians to trust you. They did — they voted for PH in overwhelming numbers. In return, you’ve not only maintained every discriminatory policy but, according to independent analysis, made things WORSE. You’ve traded minority rights for Malay-Muslim votes. Every minority Malaysian who voted PH deserves an apology — and an explanation for why their trust was weaponised against their own interests.
