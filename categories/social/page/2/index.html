Penang's Planning Failures Exposed — Traffic, Floods, Vanishing Green Spaces After 16 Years of DAP

Said by: Chow Kon Yeow / Penang Forum / Aliran

Category: Social

Date: 2025-12-01

Amirudin Rolls Out Flood Recovery Plan — After Selangor's Worst Floods Under His Watch

Said by: Amirudin Shari, Selangor Menteri Besar

Category: Social

Date: 2025-11-29

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: On 29 November 2025, Amirudin rolled out a ‘major mobilisation’ for post-flood recovery as evacuee numbers surged across Selangor. Water from northern Selangor flooded into Kuala Langat and Sepang. JPS installed four pumps in Kuala Selangor with six more planned. A police officer went missing after a car skidded into a storm drain near KLIA during the floods. The Met Department withdrew severe rain warnings, but damage was extensive.

Social Media Ban for Under-16s — Enforcement Plan: TBD

Said by: Fahmi Fadzil, Communications Minister

Category: Social

Date: 2025-11-24

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: Communications Minister Fahmi Fadzil announced in November 2025 that Malaysia would ban social media access for children under 16, following Australia’s lead. The ban was set for implementation in 2026. However, the NYT noted the announcement was “light on details” — no age verification infrastructure exists, no enforcement mechanism was outlined, and no timeline for implementation was provided. The government framed it as protecting children from online harms, but offered no technical plan for making it work.
