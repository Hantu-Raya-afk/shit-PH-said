DAP Internal War: Chow vs Guan Eng Over Penang Councillor Appointments

Hantu Raya

February 14, 2026

Said by: Chow Kon Yeow / Lim Guan Eng / Penang DAP

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-02-14

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: Penang DAP was forced to publicly clarify an ‘alleged misunderstanding’ between CM Chow Kon Yeow and his predecessor Lim Guan Eng over the appointment of councillors for both city councils. DAP publicity secretary Joshua Woo insisted the appointments followed ‘required conditions and processes’ and were consistent with ‘institutional precedent.’ The statement was damage control after reports suggested a factional power struggle between Chow’s camp and Guan Eng’s allies over who controls appointments in Penang.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: When a party has to issue public statements clarifying that its CM and former CM are not fighting over councillor appointments, they are definitely fighting over councillor appointments. Penang councillors control local planning approvals, licensing, and land use decisions worth billions. This isn’t a personality clash — it’s a fight over patronage distribution. Who gets appointed to councils determines who controls development in Penang.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: DAP’s internal appointment process is described as following ’established method long practised by Penang DAP.’ Translation: the same opaque, personality-driven selection process that the party has always used. There is no open application, no independent vetting committee, no published criteria. Councillors are appointed by party leaders based on factional loyalty. CNA’s analysis described DAP’s Penang politics as ‘acrimonious’ and driven by ‘personality politics’ rather than policy.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: The Chow-Guan Eng tension has been building since DAP’s party elections in September 2024, when Chow lost the state DAP chairmanship — effectively making him a CM without control of the state party machinery. The Straits Times noted Chow ‘vowed work continues’ even though he’s ’not state’s DAP chief’ — a ’not a lame duck’ declaration that only lame ducks need to make.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: Your party can’t even agree on who appoints councillors without a public spat. Your CM and former CM are in a factional war. Your councillor appointments follow an ’established method’ that nobody outside the party understands. Implement transparent, merit-based appointment criteria. Publish them. And resolve your internal power struggle before it starts affecting planning approvals and development decisions that impact Penangites.
