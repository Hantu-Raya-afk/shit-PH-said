Rafizi Warns: Farhash Allegations Undermine PM's Anti-Corruption Credentials

Hantu Raya

October 15, 2025

Said by: Rafizi Ramli, Economy Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-10-15

📌 What Happened

Rafizi publicly urged Anwar to confront allegations against Farhash Wafa, warning that Malaysia’s CPI flatlined at 50 points — the same score as 2015 during the peak 1MDB scandal. Said it’s ’extremely dangerous if the administration is seen as inconsistent in combating corruption.'

🔥 Why It Matters

When your own Economy Minister publicly warns that your anti-corruption credentials are collapsing, the problem is undeniable. Rafizi effectively told Anwar: your refusal to act against Farhash is measurably damaging the country’s corruption score. A CPI of 50 under Anwar is the same as under Najib during 1MDB — that comparison alone is devastating.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

Rafizi’s warning was ignored; he was later sidelined from cabinet. The minister who told the truth was punished, while the aide accused of corruption was protected. This is not reform — it is the old playbook in new packaging.

🎯 PH Callout

Your most competent minister warned you publicly that protecting Farhash was destroying your anti-corruption credentials. Your response was to sideline the minister and protect the aide. In any reform government, that’s backwards. In yours, it’s standard operating procedure.
