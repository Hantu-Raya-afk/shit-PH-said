MACC Seizes RM6M and Freezes Assets Linked to Selangor Menteri Besar Inc in Sand Mining Corruption Probe

Hantu Raya

October 18, 2024

Said by: Amirudin Shari / MACC

Category: Politics

Date: 2024-10-18

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: In October 2024, the Malaysian Anti-Corruption Commission (MACC) seized RM6 million and froze assets linked to Selangor Menteri Besar Incorporated (MBI) in connection with a sand mining corruption probe. MACC Chief Commissioner Azam Baki confirmed that five firms linked to a Selangor politician were under investigation for alleged corruption involving a sand mining concession. The probe uncovered a network of contracts and payments that raised questions about the integrity of Selangor’s sand mining approvals.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: Selangor MBI is the state’s investment arm — directly under the Menteri Besar’s purview. When MACC seizes millions linked to the MB’s own corporate vehicle, it implicates the governance of the state’s highest office. Sand mining is one of Selangor’s most corruption-prone sectors, involving environmental destruction, river pollution, and billions in revenue. The fact that MACC had to intervene means internal checks failed.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: Amirudin has been MB since 2018. Sand mining corruption in Selangor has been documented for years. Environmental groups have warned about illegal mining destroying riverbanks and poisoning water sources. Under Amirudin’s watch, the problem grew until MACC had to step in. That’s not governance — that’s negligence. The MB either didn’t know (incompetence) or didn’t act (complicity). Neither is acceptable.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: In September 2025, MACC confirmed new developments in the MBI corruption probe. A former Selangor GLC officer was charged over RM1.7 million in graft. Earlier, activist Chegubard lodged a MACC report alleging RM10 billion in irregularities linked to the Selangor Maritime Gateway project. The pattern is consistent: Selangor under PH is a corruption hotspot that the government refuses to acknowledge.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: MACC is investigating your state investment arm for corruption. Your own GLC officers have been charged. Your sand mining sector is a mess. And your response is silence. If this happened under a BN menteri besar, PKR would be holding nightly ceramah demanding resignation. Under Amirudin? Crickets.
