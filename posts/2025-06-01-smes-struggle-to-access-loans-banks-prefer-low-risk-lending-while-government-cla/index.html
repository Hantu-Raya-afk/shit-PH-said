SMEs Struggle to Access Loans — Banks Prefer Low-Risk Lending While Government Claims 'Business-Friendly'

Hantu Raya

June 1, 2025

Said by: SME Association / Bank Negara Data

Category: Economy

Date: 2025-06-01

SMEs employ 7M+ workers and contribute 40% of GDP but face significant barriers accessing financing. Banks remain risk-averse, loan rejection rates high. SMEs face a perfect storm: rising costs from SST, labour shortages, and slow-paying government agencies.
