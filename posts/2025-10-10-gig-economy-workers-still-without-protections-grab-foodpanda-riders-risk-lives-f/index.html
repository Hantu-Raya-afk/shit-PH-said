Gig Economy Workers Still Without Protections — Grab, Foodpanda Riders Risk Lives for Poverty Wages

Hantu Raya

October 10, 2025

Said by: Labour Advocates / i-Saraan Programme

Category: Economy

Date: 2025-10-10

Gig workers remain without basic labour protections — no minimum wage, no mandatory accident insurance, no collective bargaining. Government’s i-Saraan Plus offers voluntary EPF matching of up to RM600/year, a band-aid on a gaping wound.
