Anwar Hits Back at Critics Who Say He Travels Too Much — 'They Don't Understand Hard Work'

Hantu Raya

February 27, 2025

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-02-27

The Jet-Setting PM Who Can’t Take Criticism

When Malaysians questioned why their PM was constantly abroad while domestic crises mounted, Anwar’s response was not empathy — it was indignation. He hit out at critics, saying they were “clearly unaware of the hard work” that goes into overseas trips.

The Facts

Fact: Anwar defended his extensive overseas travel schedule, dismissing critics as uninformed.

Fact: In November 2024, his own disclosure that private companies funded 75% of travel costs raised further questions.

Fact: While the PM travels, Malaysians face: rising costs, healthcare crisis, education decline, housing unaffordability, and democratic backsliding.

Why It Matters

  • Every PM travels. The question is whether the results justify the frequency.
  • Dismissing legitimate questions about travel priorities as ignorance is the same arrogance PH promised to end.
  • Malaysians don’t begrudge diplomatic work. They begrudge a PM who lectures them about hard work while they can’t afford groceries. Sources: Multiple news reports
