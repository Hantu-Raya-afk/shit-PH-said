Lim Guan Eng Corruption Trial — MACC Officer Testifies About Money Given to Former Penang CM

Hantu Raya

July 10, 2025

Said by: Lim Guan Eng, former Penang CM / MACC

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-07-10

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: On 10 July 2025, a MACC investigating officer told the Sessions Court that during a fraud investigation in December 2019, Datuk Zarul Ahmad Mohd Zulkifli — then director of Consortium Zenith Construction (CZCSB) — revealed he had given money to both G. Gnanaraja and Lim Guan Eng in connection with the Penang undersea tunnel project. MACC Senior Superintendent Ng Heng Jun confirmed he had informed his superiors about Zarul’s statement. The tunnel project, worth RM6.3 billion, has been described by MACC witnesses as ‘akin to a land scam.’

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: Lim Guan Eng was DAP’s most prominent leader and Penang’s Chief Minister for 10 years. He’s the face of DAP’s governance model. If the tunnel corruption allegations are proven, it doesn’t just implicate one politician — it implicates DAP’s entire Penang legacy. A RM6.3 billion project that MACC witnesses describe as a ’land scam’ would be the biggest corruption case linked to a DAP leader in the party’s history.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: DAP has positioned itself as the anti-corruption party for decades. Its entire brand is clean governance and meritocracy. The Penang tunnel trial threatens to demolish that brand. And DAP’s response? Silence from the national leadership. No public demand for full accountability. No independent review of Penang’s procurement processes under Guan Eng. The party that demands accountability from everyone else cannot bring itself to demand it from its own.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: The tunnel project was approved during Guan Eng’s tenure, using a ‘request for proposal’ mechanism rather than open tender — a procurement method that critics say favours connected companies. The same procurement opacity was criticised in the Penang Transport Master Plan (PTMP). Under DAP, Penang’s mega-projects consistently avoided open tender processes.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: Your former Penang CM is on trial for corruption related to a RM6.3 billion project that MACC calls a ’land scam.’ Your current Penang CM says the tunnel project is ‘still active.’ Your party has said nothing meaningful about accountability. Either demand full transparency from your own leaders or stop pretending DAP is the anti-corruption party. You can’t have it both ways.
