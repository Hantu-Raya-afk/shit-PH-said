River Water Quality Declining — More Polluted Rivers in 2025 Than 2024

Said by: Natural Resources Ministry

Category: Other

Date: 2026-01-28

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: According to data released by the Natural Resources Ministry in late January 2026, Malaysia’s river water quality deteriorated year-over-year. The numbers are moving in the wrong direction across every category. Said by: Royal Malaysia Police

Category: Social

Date: 2026-01-17
