Ini bukan kali pertama keretakan dalaman PH menjadi awam. Pada Disember 2025, DAP mengancam “mempertimbangkan semula” kedudukannya dalam kerajaan jika Anwar gagal menyampaikan reformasi dalam enam bulan. Nurul Izzah sendiri telah menyeru jawatankuasa pengawasan SPRM bebas seawal Februari 2026. Coraknya jelas: tangguh sebagai strategi, kesetiaan sebagai kesunyian.

🎯 Teguran PH

Anda berjanji reformasi. Anda berjanji tiada siapa yang kebal daripada penelitian. Kini anak perempuan anda sendiri menyeru pengawasan SPRM, ahli parlimen belakang anda memberi amaran kepada anda dalam akhbar, dan tindak balas anda adalah menyuruh semua orang bertenang dan menunggu jawatankuasa. Inilah realitinya, Perdana Menteri: apabila ahli parti anda terpaksa menggunakan Malaysiakini untuk mendapatkan perhatian anda mengenai rasuah, anda sudah kehilangan kuasa moral yang anda pernah tuntut.

Tiada pengumuman rasmi bahawa kemiskinan tegar telah dihapuskan. Tiada sidang akhbar. Tiada berita kilat Bernama. Tiada kenyataan PMO yang penuh kemenangan.

🔥 KENAPA IA PENTING: Ini bukan sekadar kenyataan sambil lewa. Anwar menjadikan pembasmian kemiskinan tegar sebagai teras naratif ekonomi Madani. Dakwaan pembasmian kemiskinan turut membawa risiko metodologi. Ahli ekonomi sebelum ini mempersoalkan sama ada garis kemiskinan tegar Malaysia (RM1,169/bulan seisi rumah) ditetapkan terlalu rendah, menjadikan “pembasmian” satu latihan statistik dan bukannya realiti kehidupan.

Tiada kemas kini. Tiada garis masa baharu. Tiada pengakuan bahawa sasaran itu wujud. Ini pentadbiran yang sama yang dengan kuat menuntut penghargaan untuk setiap ringgit pertumbuhan KDNK dan setiap titik perpuluhan penurunan inflasi. Tetapi apabila melibatkan janji konkrit dengan tarikh akhir konkrit? Senyap. Jika kemiskinan tegar sudah dihapuskan, katakanlah. Terbitkan data. Tunjukkan metodologi. Biar ahli ekonomi bebas mengesahkannya. Jika belum dihapuskan, katakan juga. Terangkan apa yang salah. Semak semula garis masa. Jujurlah. Apa yang anda tidak boleh lakukan ialah membuat janji besar, menunggang gelombang PR, dan kemudian berpura-pura tarikh akhir tidak wujud.

📜 KES TERDAHULU: Ini bukan tarikh akhir PH yang pertama lenyap:

Bernama menyiarkannya. Setiap media utama melaporkannya. Sekarang 9 Mac. S1 hampir tamat. Mana pengumuman? Mana data? Mana sidang akhbar yang anda buka champagne dan tunjukkan angka sifar? Anwar Brings Forward RM15 Billion STR Cash Handout — Record Spending, Hari Raya Timing, Snap Election Vibes

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister and Finance Minister

Category: Economy

Date: 2026-03-09

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: On March 9, 2026, Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim announced that Phase 2 of the Sumbangan Tunai Rahmah (STR) cash transfer programme would be brought forward to March 10 — weeks ahead of its originally scheduled date. The early disbursement will benefit 5.2 million recipients.

Anwar framed the move as helping Malaysians prepare for Hari Raya Aidilfitri. The total 2026 allocation for STR and SARA (Sumbangan Asas Rahmah) stands at RM15 billion — the highest in the history of the Federal Government’s direct cash assistance programmes.

This comes on the same day Anwar also announced a separate RM500 special financial aid for civil servants in Grade 15 and below (to be paid March 13) and mosque staff.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS

Three things make this significant:

  1. Record spending on cash transfers. RM15 billion is not pocket change. This is the largest direct cash handout allocation any Malaysian government has ever made — and it comes from a PM who campaigned on fiscal discipline, subsidy rationalisation, and ending the culture of handouts.
  2. The timing is suspicious. Reuters reported on March 6 that government sources say Anwar could call snap elections as early as July 2026. Bringing forward cash payments right before Hari Raya — the single most politically sensitive festive period in Malaysia — is textbook pre-election cash politics.
  3. Structural reform is nowhere. Cash transfers are not economic policy. They are a painkiller. The RON95 fuel subsidy rationalisation has been delayed repeatedly. The promised targeted subsidy mechanism is still not operational. Instead of fixing the system, the government is writing bigger cheques.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT

Anwar spent years — decades — attacking BN’s BR1M cash handout programme as vote-buying, populist bribery, and fiscal irresponsibility. He called it a “crutch” that kept the rakyat dependent on government generosity instead of building real economic resilience.

Now he’s running the exact same playbook, except he’s spending more money and bringing payments forward to land right before Hari Raya. STR is BR1M with a rebrand. The only difference is the name on the cheque.

The “highest allocation in history” line is not a flex — it’s a confession. If the economy is performing as well as the government claims (4-4.5% GDP growth, record approved investments), why does the government need to spend a record RM15 billion on cash handouts? 🎯 PH CALLOUT

You promised “reformasi, bukan derma” — reform, not charity. You told Malaysians that PH would build an economy where people didn’t need handouts. You mocked Najib for BR1M. You called it a plaster on a broken bone.

If you actually believed in reform, you’d be fixing wages, fixing subsidies, and fixing the structural reasons people need these handouts in the first place. Instead, you’re writing record cheques and hoping the rakyat forgets you promised something better.
