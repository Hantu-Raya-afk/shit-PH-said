📜 KES TERDAHULU: Skandal pemilikan saham Azam Baki pertama kali muncul pada awal 2022, apabila Malaysiakini melaporkan dia memegang saham dalam dua syarikat tersenarai melebihi had pemilikan perkhidmatan awam. Jawatankuasa parlimen khas membersihkannya — mencetuskan kemarahan meluas. Jika dia bersalah, infrastruktur antirasuah negara perlu dibina semula dari asas. Pasukan petugas kamu adalah mekanisme penangguhan, bukan mekanisme akauntabiliti.

Azam Baki's RM100M Defamation Suit Against Bloomberg — MACC Chief Fights Media Instead of Corruption

Said by: Bloomberg / Azam Baki, MACC Chief Commissioner

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-02-27

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: On 27 February 2026, it was reported that MACC Chief Commissioner Azam Baki filed a RM100 million defamation suit against Bloomberg. The suit came after Bloomberg published an investigation alleging that Azam owned millions of shares in publicly listed companies — potentially breaching civil servant ownership limits. Bloomberg further alleged that MACC officials had colluded with businessmen to orchestrate hostile takeovers of listed firms, using raids and intimidation to force executives into selling shares to groups dubbed the “corporate mafia.” Rather than resigning or submitting to an independent investigation, Azam chose to sue the messenger. Anwar’s response was to announce a task force — not an independent inquiry, not a Royal Commission. Even DAP, his own coalition partner, said a task force “does not go far enough.”

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: The head of Malaysia’s anti-corruption agency is suing the world’s most prominent financial news outlet for reporting on his alleged financial misconduct. This is not a case of a private citizen defending their reputation — this is the chief enforcer of anti-corruption law using the legal system to intimidate a media organisation that investigated him. If Azam’s role is to fight corruption, and a credible international outlet alleges he is compromised, the correct institutional response is a transparent, independent investigation — not a defamation suit designed to chill future reporting. The RM100M figure is not about compensation; it is about deterrence. Bloomberg has stood by its reporting. The anti-corruption agency fighting corruption journalism instead of actual corruption is peak Madani irony.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: Anwar’s response — a task force — is the bureaucratic equivalent of thoughts and prayers. A task force reports to the government. An independent inquiry reports to the public. Anwar chose the one that protects him. DAP publicly said a task force “does not go far enough,” meaning even Anwar’s own coalition partners don’t trust his anti-corruption mechanisms. CNA reported that Anwar faces a “graft crisis” with “PKR party fractures” and “PH coalition jitters.” The man who built Reformasi on the promise of clean governance now leads a government where the anti-corruption chief sues journalists, the PM’s aide gets charged with bribery, and the DPM’s 47 charges get dropped. At some point, the pattern is the policy.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: Azam Baki’s share ownership scandal first surfaced in early 2022, when Malaysiakini reported he held shares in two public-listed companies exceeding the civil service ownership cap. A special parliamentary committee cleared him — prompting widespread outrage. Bloomberg’s 2025-2026 investigation went further, alleging systemic collusion between MACC officials and corporate raiders. The MACC-media adversarial pattern is not new either: the commission has previously threatened legal action against outlets that reported on its internal affairs. Under Azam’s leadership, MACC has become less an anti-corruption body and more a political instrument — selectively prosecuting opposition figures while shielding coalition allies.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: You promised MACC independence. Instead, the MACC chief is suing Bloomberg for doing MACC’s job. An independent inquiry — not a task force — is the minimum acceptable response. If Azam is innocent, an inquiry clears him. If he is not, the country’s anti-corruption infrastructure needs to be rebuilt from scratch. Your task force is a stalling mechanism, not an accountability mechanism. Even your own coalition partner sees through it. Do better, or stop pretending Reformasi means anything.

Jika kamu tak mahu dengar DAP, dengar prinsip yang pernah memenjarakanmu: bahawa institusi antirasuah mesti bebas daripada kawalan politik. [BM] Fahmi Sahkan SKMM Sekat Grindr Dan Blued — 'Kerajaan Reformasi' PH Kini Jalankan Penapisan Digital Terhadap Minoriti

Said by: Fahmi Fadzil, Communications Minister

Category: Social

Date: 2026-02-25

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Pada 25 Februari 2026, Menteri Komunikasi Fahmi Fadzil mengesahkan dalam jawapan bertulis parlimen bahawa Suruhanjaya Komunikasi dan Multimedia Malaysia (SKMM) telah menyekat laman web Grindr dan Blued — dua platform temu janji dan rangkaian terbesar di dunia yang digunakan oleh lelaki gay dan biseksual. Fahmi berkata SKMM kini “mengkaji beberapa langkah perundangan” untuk mengekang aplikasi tersebut dari Google Play dan Apple App Store. Menjelang awal Mac, kedua-dua aplikasi telah dikeluarkan dari kedai aplikasi Malaysia sepenuhnya. Timbalan Perdana Menteri Ahmad Zahid Hamidi secara berasingan mengumumkan cadangan Rang Undang-Undang Jenayah Siber untuk memperluaskan kuasa penguatkuasaan digital kerajaan.

🔥 Kenapa Ini Penting

Ini adalah kerajaan Madani menggunakan infrastruktur negara untuk memadamkan secara sistematik ruang selamat digital bagi komuniti minoriti yang dianiaya — dan melakukannya secara senyap melalui jawapan bertulis parlimen dan tindakan kawal selia dan bukannya perbahasan dasar terbuka. Fahmi merangkanya di bawah payung “kandungan lucah atau tidak bermoral, eksploitasi, penyalahgunaan, penipuan, mengeksploitasi kanak-kanak atau ancaman terhadap keselamatan awam” — kemahiran tinggi mencampurkan orang dewasa yang saling bersetuju dengan penjenayah.

💩 Kenapa Ini Karut

Pakatan Harapan berkempen atas kebebasan sivil, reformasi institusi dan janji bahawa Malaysia akan berhenti menggunakan negara untuk menganiaya rakyatnya sendiri. Fahmi Fadzil — bekas tokoh masyarakat sivil dan pernah memperjuangkan kebebasan akhbar — kini adalah menteri yang meluluskan penapisan internet menyasarkan minoriti. Kerajaan yang sama yang menjerit tentang “kebebasan bersuara” apabila pengkritiknya dipanggil di bawah Seksyen 124B selesa mengarahkan SKMM menyekat laman web dan menekan Apple dan Google untuk menyahsenarai aplikasi.

📜 Kes Terdahulu

Ini mengikuti corak peningkatan yang didokumentasi dengan baik di bawah pentadbiran Anwar. Pada Mei 2023, Kementerian Dalam Negeri menyerbu kedai Swatch dan merampas lebih 160 jam tangan berwarna pelangi. Pada November 2025, 202 orang ditahan dalam serbuan di tempat khusus lelaki di Kuala Lumpur. Justice for Sisters mendokumentasikan pelanggaran prosedur sistematik. Setiap kali, kerajaan sama ada menyokong tindakan itu, berdiam diri atau memperluaskan alat perundangan yang tersedia untuk tindakan keras seterusnya.

🎯 Seruan Kepada PH

Kamu menjanjikan rakyat Malaysia kerajaan yang akan mempertahankan kebebasan sivil, memansuhkan undang-undang represif dan melindungi golongan lemah daripada tindakan berlebihan negara. Sebaliknya, kamu membina infrastruktur penapisan baharu, mencadangkan perundangan siber yang lebih luas dan menjalankan program pemulihan melalui jabatan agama negeri — semuanya sementara Fahmi Fadzil, yang pernah memperjuangkan kebebasan media, menandatangani arahan sekatan. Jika kamu tak boleh pun meninggalkan telefon orang, berhenti berlakon kamu kerajaan reformasi.

Dia tidak mendedahkan apa KPI tersebut, bagaimana ia diukur atau siapa yang menilainya. 🔥 KENAPA INI PENTING: Mendakwa pematuhan KPI 100% tanpa mendedahkan KPI adalah setara korporat memberi diri sendiri ulasan prestasi yang sempurna dan enggan tunjukkan borangnya. Ia bukan akauntabiliti — ia pensijilan sendiri. Jika setiap menteri memenuhi KPI mereka, maka sama ada KPI begitu rendah sehingga kegagalan mustahil, atau penilaian itu tidak jujur. Korea Selatan menjalankan penilaian prestasi kerajaan tahunan dengan keputusan yang diterbitkan. Pendekatan Malaysia di bawah PM “reformis”: percaya katanya. Tinjauan Pusat Merdeka Februari 2026 menunjukkan kepuasan orang ramai terhadap pengurusan ekonomi pada 38% — mencadangkan bahawa apa sahaja KPI yang wujud, orang ramai tidak bersetuju ia dipenuhi.

📜 KES TERDAHULU: Setiap PM Malaysia pernah mendakwa kabinetnya berprestasi baik. Najib mendakwa menterinya menyampaikan keputusan — sementara 1MDB berdarah berbilion. Muhyiddin mendakwa pengurusan pandemik berkesan — sementara ribuan mati. Coraknya sejagat: penilaian sendiri tanpa pengesahan luaran sentiasa menghasilkan skor sempurna.

🎯 SERUAN KEPADA PH: Terbitkan KPI. Teliti setiap menteri, setiap metrik, setiap sasaran dan setiap keputusan. Letakkan dalam talian. Biar rakyat lihat sama ada KPI Menteri Pendidikan termasuk skor PISA yang merosot, sama ada KPI Menteri Kesihatan termasuk 33,000 doktor kontrak masih menunggu jawatan tetap, sama ada KPI Menteri Ekonomi termasuk 77% rakyat Malaysia bergelut dengan kos. Kamu takkan — kerana kad skor itu akan menghancurkan. Kerajaan yang menilai diri sendiri secara rahsia tiada kredibiliti mendakwa akauntabiliti.

Every Cabinet Member Meeting KPIs, Says Anwar — Critics Ask: What KPIs?

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-02-25

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: On 25 February 2026, Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim told Parliament that every cabinet member is meeting their KPIs and performing with integrity. He did not disclose what those KPIs were, how they were measured, or who evaluated them. Anwar was responding to an opposition MP who asked if ministers would be forced to resign for poor performance. His reply: “Accountability is not only about punishment but also correction, continuous improvement, and the moral responsibility ministers held for the people.” No KPIs have ever been publicly disclosed for any minister in Anwar’s cabinet. No minister has been penalised, demoted, or publicly reprimanded for underperformance. Two ministers were removed in the December 2025 reshuffle — including Rafizi Ramli — but Anwar framed it as “portfolio realignment,” not performance accountability.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: Claiming 100% KPI compliance without disclosing the KPIs is the corporate equivalent of giving yourself a perfect performance review and refusing to show the form. It is not accountability — it is self-certification. If every minister is meeting their KPIs, then either the KPIs are so low that failure is impossible, or the assessment is dishonest. Given that 77% of Malaysians report struggling with cost of living, university rankings are dropping, the CPI is stuck at 1MDB-era levels, and reform promises remain unmet, the claim is laughable on its face. Transparency means publishing the KPIs, the methodology, and the results — and letting the public judge. “Trust me, everyone is great” is not reform governance.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: A PM who holds both the PM and Finance Minister portfolios is essentially grading himself on fiscal management. There is no independent check on the most powerful office in the country. The Najib government also claimed ministers were performing. The Mahathir government also claimed results were being delivered. Without published metrics, every government is equally excellent — which means the exercise is meaningless. Singapore publishes ministerial performance metrics and links them to public service delivery targets. South Korea conducts annual government performance evaluations with published results. Malaysia’s approach under the “reformist” PM: trust his word.
