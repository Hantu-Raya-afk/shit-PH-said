Nearly RM1 Billion Paid in Court Damages for Government Negligence — Anwar's Fix? Letters of Reprimand

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister and Finance Minister

Category: Economy

Date: 2026-03-04

📌 What Happened

Prime Minister and Finance Minister Anwar Ibrahim disclosed in a written parliamentary reply — responding to PH’s own William Leong (Selayang) — that the Malaysian government paid out RM933,360,633 in court-awarded damages between 2020 and 2025. The payouts were the result of negligence, misconduct, and other wrongdoing by government officials and agencies. Anwar outlined measures including instructing ministries to conduct investigations into the cases and “recommend disciplinary action” against those found negligent — and, spectacularly, issuing letters of reprimand.

🔥 Why It Matters

Nearly RM1 billion of public money was handed over because government officials were negligent, irresponsible, or outright corrupt in carrying out their duties — and the government’s proposed accountability mechanism is a sternly worded letter. This figure spans the administrations of Muhyiddin, Ismail Sabri, and now Anwar himself. The fact that this disclosure came buried in a written parliamentary reply rather than proactively announced tells you everything about how seriously this government takes fiscal accountability. PH promised to end the culture of impunity. Instead, it’s inherited it and furnished it with a fresh coat of bureaucratic language.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

Letters of reprimand. For nearly a billion ringgit in losses caused by government negligence. Let that sink in. In the private sector, a single case of professional negligence resulting in significant financial loss would trigger terminations, lawsuits, and regulatory action. In Anwar’s Malaysia, it triggers a stern memo. This isn’t accountability — it’s theatre. If PH were serious about reform, it would name the ministries involved, disclose the individual cases, surcharge the officers responsible, and refer criminal negligence to the relevant authorities. Instead, the Prime Minister filed a quiet written reply and moved on. The rakyat pays the bill; nobody pays the price.

📜 Previous Cases

Malaysia has a long, inglorious history of government negligence costing taxpayers billions — from the PKFZ scandal (RM12.5 billion in cost overruns and soft loans) to the endless saga of abandoned government projects and botched procurement exercises under successive administrations. PH itself campaigned on ending this cycle in both 2018 and 2022. During PH 1.0 under Mahathir, the government conducted an audit of 1MDB-era spending but quietly shelved follow-up enforcement. Now under PH 2.0, Anwar’s government discloses the losses but proposes remedies so weak they amount to an invitation to keep doing it.

🎯 PH Callout

You promised the rakyat you would be different. You said there would be consequences. You said clean governance wasn’t a slogan — it was a commitment. So where are the consequences for nearly RM1 billion in court-ordered damages caused by your own government machinery? Letters of reprimand are what you give someone who parks in the wrong spot, not officials whose negligence cost the nation hundreds of millions. Name the ministries. Publish the cases. Surcharge the officers. Show the country that “Madani” means something beyond a logo on a government letterhead.

Negri Sembilan MB Admits His Driver Runs Red Lights — 'I Have No Choice, Look at My Facebook Schedule'

Said by: Aminuddin Harun, Menteri Besar of Negri Sembilan (PH-Lenggeng)

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-04

📌 What Happened

On March 4, 2026, Negri Sembilan Menteri Besar Datuk Seri Aminuddin Harun publicly admitted that his official car routinely runs red lights, after a viral video showed a vehicle believed to be his using blue strobe lights and a siren to blast through a red light on Jalan Rasah. Speaking to reporters after a state executive council meeting, Aminuddin said: “I admit that we have to beat the light at times, but I have no choice, as there are sometimes several official events lined up in a day.” He pointed to his Facebook page as proof of his hectic schedule. He added that a police officer is always present in the car, and that his office has sought “necessary approvals from the relevant authorities.”

🔥 Why It Matters

This is not about a traffic violation. This is about a PH state leader publicly declaring that the rules of the road do not apply to him — and framing it as a reasonable accommodation for public service. In a country where ordinary Malaysians receive summonses, demerit points, and fines for the same offence, a Menteri Besar is casually telling reporters he does it regularly and expects understanding. The message to the public is unmistakable: there is one set of rules for leaders and another for everyone else. This is the exact culture of elite entitlement PH promised to dismantle.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

Aminuddin’s defence is remarkable in its audacity. He didn’t deny it. He didn’t promise to stop. He told Malaysians to check his Facebook schedule to understand why he needs to break the law. The ‘I had a cop in the car’ justification is not exoneration — it’s an admission that the state apparatus is being used to facilitate rule-breaking by the powerful. If the MB’s schedule is so packed that traffic laws become optional, the answer is better scheduling, not blue strobes and sirens. An apology that ends with ‘but state leaders need some leeway’ is not an apology. It is a declaration of privilege.

📜 Previous Cases

Malaysia has a long history of political elites treating traffic laws as suggestions. VIP motorcades, outriders, and sirens have been normalised to the point where ordinary motorists are expected to yield or face consequences. In 2022, a viral video of a minister’s convoy forcing vehicles off the road drew widespread criticism. PH, in opposition, was vocal about such abuses of power. Now in government, their own MB is doing the same thing and asking for sympathy. The entitlement transcends party lines — it’s baked into how Malaysian political elites understand their own importance.

🎯 PH Callout

You campaigned on humility. You told Malaysians that the era of feudal politics and VIP culture was over. Your own Prime Minister once made a show of flying economy class and driving himself. Now your Negri Sembilan MB is running red lights with police cover and telling the rakyat to look at his Facebook to understand why he’s too important to stop at a traffic light. If you can’t even hold your state leaders to basic traffic laws, what hope is there for institutional reform? Follow the rules or change them through proper process. You don’t get to break them and call it public service.

Anwar mempunyai sejarah panjang menggunakan konspirasi apabila tertekan. Ini adalah taktik yang sama Najib gunakan semasa skandal 1MDB, apabila dia menyalahkan media asing, George Soros dan sabotaj pembangkang atas pendedahan. 📜 KES TERDAHULU: Lynas telah menjadi beracun secara politik sejak loji Kuantan dibuka pada 2012. PH 1.0 mengenakan syarat ketat terhadap pemindahan sisa yang tidak pernah dikuatkuasakan. Coraknya: kempen menentang Lynas dalam pembangkang, memuaskan Lynas dalam kerajaan, harap pengundi lupa.

🎯 SERUAN KEPADA PH: Kamu berkempen menentang loji ini. Ahli kamu sendiri membina kerjaya menentangnya. Pangkalan alam sekitar kamu mengundi untuk kamu sebahagiannya kerana Lynas. Dan kini kamu beri mereka pembaharuan 10 tahun sementara hampir sejuta tan sisa radioaktif duduk di Pahang tanpa pelan pelupusan. Jika sisa boleh diproses, buktikan. Jika tidak boleh, jelaskan kenapa kamu memperbaharui. Pengundi kamu di Pahang berhak mendapat jawapan yang jujur.

Malaysia’s dual 5G network strategy — introduced after Anwar’s government reversed the original single-network plan — has been plagued by policy flip-flops, RM2 billion in DNB debt, and the controversial awarding of the second 5G network to U Mobile.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: You inherited a flawed 5G plan and made it worse. The dual network was your decision. The contractual chaos is your responsibility. While DNB and TM fight over termination clauses, Malaysians are stuck with incomplete 5G infrastructure and a RM2 billion bill. Fix the policy, resolve the contracts, and stop letting political interests dictate telecoms strategy. Or admit that 5G in Malaysia is just another patronage project with better branding.

BN extended the licence before GE15. PH 2.0 has now gone further with a 10-year renewal. Every government promises to resolve the waste issue; none have. The pattern: campaign against Lynas in opposition, accommodate Lynas in government, hope voters forget.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: You campaigned against this plant. Your own members built careers opposing it. Your environmental base voted for you partly because of Lynas. And now you’ve given them a 10-year renewal while nearly a million tonnes of radioactive waste sits in Pahang with no disposal plan. If the waste can be processed, prove it. Ketiga, beralih ke arah Iran adalah tidak bertanggungjawab dari segi diplomatik. Mazhab Syiah Iran secara rasmi dianggap “menyimpang” di Malaysia yang majoriti Sunni. Anwar memberi isyarat solidariti dengan negara teokratik yang identiti agamanya dijenayahkan oleh kerajaannya sendiri.

💩 KENAPA INI KARUT

Mari kira percanggahan:

Soal kemunafikan hak asasi: Anwar mengatakan dia “tidak boleh menelan lagi” ceramah hak asasi Barat. Ini orang yang sama yang menghabiskan bertahun-tahun melobi kerajaan Barat, NGO dan media untuk hak asasinya sendiri — semasa dipenjarakan di bawah Mahathir dan Najib. Kini penglibatan asing adalah “komplot Zionis” dan piawai hak asasi Barat adalah “kemunafikan.” Satu-satunya yang berubah ialah sebelah mana meja PM dia duduk.

Soal boikot kedutaan AS: Syahredzan Johan menyeru boikot diplomatik sementara kerajaannya sendiri mengekalkan hubungan diplomatik, perdagangan dan ketenteraan penuh dengan AS. Malaysia baru menandatangani Perkongsian Strategik Komprehensif dengan Washington pada Oktober 2025.

Soal solidariti Iran: Malaysia menjenayahkan amalan Syiah. Anwar menyatakan solidariti dengan Iran sambil menganiaya penganut semazhab di tanah air — ini bukan berprinsip, ia oportunistik.

🎯 SERUAN KEPADA PH

Anwar, kamu beritahu Parlimen: “Kita tidak boleh menelan hujah-hujah mereka lagi.”

Adil. Tetapi rakyat juga tidak boleh menelan hujah kamu lagi.

Kamu ceramah Barat tentang kemunafikan sementara ketua SPRM kamu duduk atas RM800,000 saham yang tidak didedahkan. Kamu panggil advokasi asing “komplot Zionis” selepas menghabiskan berdekad melakukan perkara yang sama. Ini bukan dasar luar yang berprinsip. Ini populisme musim Ramadan.

[BM] PKR Upah Perunding AS Untuk Lobi Washington Terhadap Najib — Kini Menjerit Salah Bila Orang Lain Buat Sama

Said by: Sivarasa Rasiah, Former PKR Vice-President (confirmed); PKR leadership

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-02

📌 APA YANG BERLAKU

Pada Mac 2026, MalaysiaNow menerbitkan emel bocor yang mendedahkan PKR telah mengupah sebuah firma perundingan politik berpusat di Washington pada 2016 untuk melobi Amerika Syarikat menekan Perdana Menteri ketika itu Najib Razak. Emel tersebut termasuk dokumen 84 muka surat yang membuat tuduhan serius terhadap Najib, dengan matlamat mendapatkan Washington untuk secara terbuka mengecam kerajaan Malaysia.

Bekas naib presiden PKR Sivarasa Rasiah mengesahkan komunikasi tersebut, mengatakan pemimpin parti termasuk beliau sendiri terlibat. Beliau merangkanya sebagai advokasi yang sah untuk membebaskan Anwar Ibrahim, yang ketika itu seorang tahanan politik.

Pendedahan ini datang ketika kerajaan Anwar secara serentak menuduh keluarga bekas menteri kewangan Daim Zainuddin menggunakan ejen asing untuk menggugat kestabilan pentadbiran semasa — memanggilnya ancaman keselamatan negara.

🔥 KENAPA INI PENTING

Ini mendedahkan piawai berganda yang besar di teras strategi politik PH semasa. Anwar merangka dakwaan lobi asing keluarga Daim sebagai pengkhianatan — komplot untuk menjatuhkan kerajaan menggunakan pengaruh asing. Tetapi partinya sendiri melakukan perkara yang sama apabila mereka dalam pembangkang.

💩 KENAPA INI KARUT

Pembelaan Sivarasa — “kami cuba membebaskan tahanan politik” — mudah tetapi tidak tahan penelitian. Dokumen 84 muka surat itu bukan sahaja meminta pembebasan Anwar. Ia membuat tuduhan menyeluruh terhadap Najib dan secara jelas meminta campur tangan AS dalam politik domestik Malaysia. Itu lobi asing. Noktah.

Apabila PKR melakukannya, ia “advokasi.” Apabila keluarga Daim didakwa melakukannya, ia “konspirasi Zionis” dan ancaman keselamatan negara. Piawai berganda begitu terang-terangan sehingga pemerhati biasa pun boleh nampak. Anwar pada dasarnya mengatakan: lobi asing adalah patriotik apabila kami melakukannya, dan pengkhianatan apabila lawan kami melakukannya. Itu bukan prinsip — ia kemudahan.

📜 KES TERDAHULU

Parti politik Malaysia mempunyai sejarah panjang lobi antarabangsa. UMNO mengekalkan pelobi Washington selama berdekad. Anwar sendiri memupuk hubungan mendalam dengan pembuat dasar AS, badan pemikir dan media semasa tahun-tahun pembangkangan. Kemarahan terpilih mengikuti corak yang sama seperti layanan PH terhadap kebebasan akhbar (Bloomberg bagus apabila mendedahkan Najib, Bloomberg buruk apabila mendedahkan Azam Baki).

🎯 SERUAN KEPADA PH

Kamu mengupah firma AS untuk melobi Washington terhadap PM Malaysia yang memerintah, menghasilkan dokumen 84 muka surat tuduhan, dan meminta campur tangan Amerika dalam politik Malaysia. Bekas naib presiden kamu sendiri mengesahkannya. Kini kamu beritahu rakyat Malaysia apabila orang lain melakukan perkara yang sama, ia konspirasi Zionis untuk menjatuhkan kerajaan? Pilih satu laluan. Sama ada lobi asing adalah bahagian sah advokasi politik — maka berhenti menjenayahkan lawan kamu — atau ia ancaman keselamatan negara, maka pemimpin PKR sendiri perlu disiasat juga. Kamu tak boleh ambil kedua-duanya.
