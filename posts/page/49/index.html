[BM] Krisis K-Pod Dan Vape Dalam Kalangan Belia — Wabak Dadah Baru Sementara Kerajaan Lambat Bertindak

Said by: DPM Ahmad Zahid / AADK

Category: Health

Date: 2025-08-10

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Krisis dadah baru yang dahsyat sedang melanda sekolah dan komuniti Malaysia: K-pod dan vape berlapis dadah sintetik menjadi mekanisme penghantaran dadah pilihan belia Malaysia. Timbalan PM Ahmad Zahid mengakui keadaan berada pada ’tahap kritikal,’ dengan kes penyalahgunaan dadah melonjak 32.5% dari 2023 ke 2024 dan perangsang jenis amfetamina terlibat dalam lebih 70% kes.

🔥 Mengapa Ia Penting

Fenomena K-pod — kartrij vape berlapis dadah sintetik — amat berbahaya kerana ia menyamarkan penggunaan dadah sebagai vape biasa, menjadikan pengesanan oleh ibu bapa dan guru hampir mustahil. Platform media sosial telah menjadi saluran pengedaran, dengan pengedar mengiklan melalui Telegram dan TikTok.

💩 Mengapa Ia Karut

Respons kerajaan ialah Pelan Komunikasi Anti-Dadah 2025-2027 — pada dasarnya kempen perhubungan awam sedangkan yang diperlukan ialah penguatkuasaan dan pintasan. Malaysia perlu ‘mengubah strateginya’ menurut Zahid, tetapi perubahan strategik tidak terwujud melebihi sidang akhbar. Sementara itu, satu generasi belia Malaysia sedang diubah secara kimia.

🎯 Teguran PH

TPM anda memanggilnya ’tahap kritikal’ dan respons anda ialah pelan komunikasi. Kanak-kanak menghisap vape dadah sintetik di tandas sekolah dan anda menulis risalah. Tutup saluran Telegram. Kuatkuasakan di sempadan. Biayai intervensi peringkat sekolah. Atau akui bahawa ’tahap kritikal’ hanyalah satu lagi frasa yang anda gunakan untuk sidang akhbar sebelum bergerak.

K-Pods and Vape Crisis Among Youth — New Drug Epidemic While Government Plays Catch-Up

Said by: DPM Ahmad Zahid / AADK

Category: Health

Date: 2025-08-10

📌 What Happened

A devastating new drug crisis is sweeping through Malaysian schools and communities: K-pods and synthetic drug-laced vapes are becoming the drug delivery mechanism of choice for young Malaysians. Deputy PM Ahmad Zahid acknowledged the situation was at a ‘critical level,’ with drug abuse cases surging 32.5% from 2023 to 2024 and amphetamine-type stimulants involved in over 70% of cases.

🔥 Why It Matters

The K-pod phenomenon — vape cartridges laced with synthetic drugs — is particularly insidious because it disguises drug use as ordinary vaping, making detection by parents and teachers nearly impossible. Social media platforms have become distribution channels, with dealers advertising through Telegram and TikTok.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

The government’s response has been the Anti-Drug Communication Plan 2025-2027 — essentially a PR campaign when what’s needed is enforcement and interdiction. Malaysia needs to ‘change its strategy’ according to Zahid, but the strategic change hasn’t materialised beyond press conferences. Meanwhile, a generation of young Malaysians is being chemically rewired.

🎯 PH Callout

Your DPM calls it ‘critical level’ and your response is a communication plan. Kids are vaping synthetic drugs in school bathrooms and you’re writing brochures. Shut down the Telegram channels. Enforce at the border. Fund school-level intervention. Or admit that ‘critical level’ is just another phrase you use for press conferences before moving on.

[BM] Institut Keamanan Toda: Anwar 'Jadikan Dasar Berasaskan Kaum LEBIH BURUK, Bukan Lebih Baik' — Cuba Mengatasi Islamis Dalam Pengislaman

Said by: Toda Peace Institute Analysis

Category: Social

Date: 2025-08-01

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Institut Keamanan Toda menerbitkan analisis menyeluruh bertajuk ‘Pelbagai Peperangan Anwar Ibrahim di Malaysia,’ menyimpulkan bahawa Anwar ‘bukan sekadar enggan mengganggu status quo; beliau sebenarnya menjadikannya lebih buruk dengan cuba mengatasi Islamis dalam pengislaman melalui isyarat, dasar dan kenyataan yang dianggap sangat membimbangkan oleh minoriti.’

🔥 Mengapa Ia Penting

Ini adalah institut penyelidikan keamanan antarabangsa yang menyatakan bahawa PM Malaysia yang kononnya reformis sedang MEMBURUKKAN diskriminasi kaum, bukan mengurangkannya. Sistem dasar berasaskan kaum yang telah dilembagakan — yang digambarkan sebagai ‘membunuh merit, menyekat persaingan dan inovasi, membiakkan rasa tidak puas hati minoriti, dan melahirkan kronisme dengan mencipta elit Melayu kaya tanpa berbuat banyak untuk Melayu miskin’ — sedang diperkukuh bukannya direformasi di bawah Anwar.

💩 Mengapa Ia Karut

Gerakan Reformasi Anwar secara jelas ‘menuntut pengakhiran sistem diskriminasi kaum yang dilembagakan ini.’ Itu adalah JANJI TERAS. Bukan komitmen sampingan — sebab asas mengapa minoriti menyokongnya. Kenaikannya ‘secara wajar mencetuskan harapan keadilan kaum.’ Sebaliknya, beliau bersaing dengan PAS tentang siapa boleh lebih Melayu-Islam, sementara minoriti yang mengundi PH secara beramai-ramai mendapat retorik kosong dan peluang bergambar dengan pengumuman peruntukan sekolah Cina sekali-sekala.

📜 Kes Terdahulu

Pada April 2025, apabila seorang profesor UiTM mencadangkan membuka latihan pembedahan kardiotoraks kepada pelajar bukan Bumiputera (kerana rakyat mati akibat kekurangan pakar bedah), kerajaan terpaku. Keperluan wajib Bahasa Melayu untuk pekerja asing dikenakan. Dasar Bumiputera diisytiharkan ’tidak boleh dirunding’ dalam rundingan perdagangan AS. ICERD kekal tidak diratifikasi. Perkhidmatan awam kekal 90% Bumiputera.

🎯 Teguran PH

Anda meminta rakyat Malaysia bukan Melayu mempercayai anda. Mereka melakukannya — mereka mengundi PH dalam jumlah besar. Sebagai balasan, anda bukan sahaja mengekalkan setiap dasar diskriminasi tetapi, menurut analisis bebas, menjadikan keadaan LEBIH BURUK. Anda telah menukar hak minoriti untuk undi Melayu-Islam. Setiap rakyat Malaysia minoriti yang mengundi PH layak mendapat permohonan maaf — dan penjelasan mengapa kepercayaan mereka dijadikan senjata terhadap kepentingan mereka sendiri.

[BM] MITI Dituduh Curi Konsep Pavilion Ekspo Dunia Osaka — Agensi Kreatif Hantar Surat Tuntutan

Said by: Tengku Zafrul Abdul Aziz, MITI Minister / Current Media Group

Category: Economy

Date: 2025-08-01

📌 APA YANG BERLAKU: Pada Ogos 2025, MITI di bawah Tengku Zafrul dihantar surat tuntutan oleh Current Media Group (CMG), sebuah konsultansi kreatif yang mendakwa kementerian mencuri konsepnya untuk pavilion Ekspo Dunia 2025 Osaka Malaysia tanpa memberikan pengiktirafan atau bayaran. Dakwaan itu menyatakan pitching asal CMG digunakan sebagai asas reka bentuk pavilion tanpa kontrak atau kredit. Zafrul menjawab perkara itu dalam jawapan parlimen kepada AP Kepong Lim Lip Eng.

🔥 MENGAPA IA PENTING: Jika sebuah kementerian kerajaan menggunakan harta intelek syarikat swasta tanpa bayaran atau kredit, itu adalah kecurian — tanpa mengira sama ada ia dipanggil ‘inspirasi’ atau ‘pembangunan selari.’ Pavilion Ekspo Dunia adalah pameran nasional berprofil tinggi dengan bajet yang besar. Dakwaan bahawa MITI mengambil konsep tanpa membayarnya menimbulkan persoalan serius tentang integriti perolehan dan penghormatan harta intelek dalam kementerian.

💩 MENGAPA IA KARUT: Kerajaan ‘reformis’ yang menceramah dunia tentang tadbir urus baik dan urusan adil didakwa mencuri konsep kreatif daripada syarikat Malaysia. PH naik berkuasa menjanjikan perolehan telus dan penghormatan terhadap proses wajar. Jika MITI tidak mampu menugaskan reka bentuk pavilion tanpa menerima surat tuntutan daripada pencipta asal, apa yang dikatakan tentang bagaimana kontrak lain yang lebih besar diuruskan di sebalik tabir?

📜 KES TERDAHULU: Kecurian harta intelek kerajaan daripada kontraktor swasta adalah aduan berulang di Malaysia. Agensi kreatif, arkitek, dan perunding telah lama melaporkan bahawa pitching kerajaan dilayan sebagai kerja percuma — idea diambil, kontrak pergi ke tempat lain, dan pencipta asal ditinggalkan. Di bawah BN, ini adalah standard. Di bawah PH, ia sepatutnya berubah.

🎯 TEGURAN PH: Anda mewakili Malaysia di pentas dunia di Osaka dan anda tidak mampu membayar orang yang mereka bentuknya? Jika dakwaan itu benar, pampaskan CMG dan siasat bagaimana ini berlaku. Jika palsu, keluarkan dokumen perolehan. Ketelusan berfungsi dua hala — anda tidak boleh menceramah dunia tentang tadbir urus sementara kementerian anda sendiri dihantar surat tuntutan atas kecurian harta intelek.

MITI Accused of Stealing World Expo Osaka Pavilion Concept — Creative Agency Serves Letter of Demand

Said by: Tengku Zafrul Abdul Aziz, MITI Minister / Current Media Group

Category: Economy

Date: 2025-08-01

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: In August 2025, MITI under Tengku Zafrul was served a letter of demand by Current Media Group (CMG), a creative consultancy that claimed the ministry stole its concept for Malaysia’s World Expo 2025 Osaka pavilion without providing recognition or payment. The allegation was that CMG’s original pitch was used as the basis for the pavilion design without a contract or credit. Zafrul addressed the matter in a parliamentary reply to Kepong MP Lim Lip Eng.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: If a government ministry used a private company’s intellectual property without payment or credit, that is theft — regardless of whether it’s called ‘inspiration’ or ‘parallel development.’ World Expo pavilions are high-profile national showcases with significant budgets. The allegation that MITI took a concept without paying for it raises serious questions about procurement integrity and intellectual property respect within the ministry.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: The ‘reformist’ government that lectures the world about good governance and fair dealing allegedly stole a creative concept from a Malaysian company. PH came to power promising transparent procurement and respect for due process. If MITI can’t even commission a pavilion design without a letter of demand from the original creator, what does that say about how other, larger contracts are handled behind closed doors?

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: Government IP theft from private contractors is a recurring complaint in Malaysia. Creative agencies, architects, and consultants have long reported that government pitches are treated as free work — ideas are taken, contracts go elsewhere, and the original creators are ghosted. Under BN, this was standard. Under PH, it was supposed to change.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: You represent Malaysia on the world stage at Osaka and you can’t even pay the people who designed it? If the allegation is true, compensate CMG and investigate how this happened. If it’s false, release the procurement documents. Transparency works both ways — you don’t get to lecture the world about governance while your own ministry is served letters of demand for IP theft.

Toda Peace Institute: Anwar 'Making Race-Based Policies WORSE, Not Better' — Trying to Out-Islamise the Islamists

Said by: Toda Peace Institute Analysis

Category: Social

Date: 2025-08-01

📌 What Happened

The Toda Peace Institute published a comprehensive analysis titled ‘The Many Wars of Malaysia’s Anwar Ibrahim,’ concluding that Anwar ‘isn’t just reluctant to disturb the status quo; he is in fact making it worse by trying to out-Islamize the Islamists with gestures, policies and statements that the minorities find deeply worrying.’

🔥 Why It Matters

This is an international peace research institute saying that Malaysia’s supposedly reformist PM is WORSENING racial discrimination, not reducing it. The institutionalised system of race-based policies — described as ‘killing off merit, stifling competition and innovation, breeding minority resentments, and spawning cronyism by creating a rich Malay elite without doing much for poor Malays’ — is being strengthened rather than reformed under Anwar.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

Anwar’s Reformasi movement explicitly ‘sought an end to this system of institutionalized racial discrimination.’ That was the CORE PROMISE. Not a peripheral commitment — the fundamental reason minorities supported him. His ascent ‘understandably triggered hopes of racial justice.’ Instead, he’s competing with PAS on who can be more Malay-Muslim, while minorities who voted PH in droves get empty rhetoric and photo ops with the occasional Chinese school allocation announcement.

📜 Previous Cases

In April 2025, when a UiTM professor suggested opening cardiothoracic surgery training to non-Bumiputera students (because people were dying from surgeon shortages), the government was paralysed. The mandatory Malay language requirement for foreign workers was imposed. Bumiputera policies were declared ’non-negotiable’ in US trade talks. ICERD remains unratified. The civil service remains 90% Bumiputera.

🎯 PH Callout

You asked non-Malay Malaysians to trust you. They did — they voted for PH in overwhelming numbers. In return, you’ve not only maintained every discriminatory policy but, according to independent analysis, made things WORSE. You’ve traded minority rights for Malay-Muslim votes. Every minority Malaysian who voted PH deserves an apology — and an explanation for why their trust was weaponised against their own interests.
