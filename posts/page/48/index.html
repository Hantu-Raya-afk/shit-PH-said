[BM] Nik Nazmi Letak Jawatan Menteri Alam Sekitar Selepas Kalah Pilihan Raya Parti PKR — Dedahkan Politik Feudal PH

Said by: Nik Nazmi Nik Ahmad, Former Natural Resources and Environmental Sustainability Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-05-28

📌 APA YANG BERLAKU

Pada 28 Mei 2025, Menteri Sumber Asli dan Kelestarian Alam Sekitar Nik Nazmi Nik Ahmad menyerahkan perletakan jawatan daripada Kabinet, berkuat kuasa 4 Julai. Perletakan jawatannya datang beberapa jam selepas Menteri Ekonomi Rafizi Ramli juga meletak jawatan — kedua-duanya berikutan kekalahan mereka dalam pilihan raya dalaman PKR lima hari sebelumnya.

Nik Nazmi kehilangan jawatan Naib Presiden PKR dan berkata beliau tidak boleh lagi berkhidmat sebagai menteri tanpa mandat parti sepenuhnya. “Menjadi menteri boleh memerangkap anda dalam ‘gelembung’ — dengan keistimewaan, pengiring keselamatan, dan protokol di setiap acara,” katanya. “Saya percaya terlalu melekat dengan gaya hidup sedemikian boleh berbahaya.”

Anwar secara terbuka meminta Nik Nazmi mempertimbangkan semula. Nik Nazmi enggan, berkata keputusannya muktamad dan beliau akan menumpukan kepada berkhidmat kepada pengundi Setiawangsanya.

🔥 MENGAPA IA PENTING

Dua menteri Kabinet meletak jawatan dalam beberapa jam antara satu sama lain atas politik parti dalaman bukanlah tadbir urus biasa. Ia mendedahkan hakikat bahawa pelantikan menteri PH pada asasnya dipacu oleh dinamik puak parti, bukan kecekapan atau kepentingan nasional.

Pilihan raya parti PKR yang mencetuskan perletakan jawatan ini sendiri dicemari dakwaan penipuan undi dan nepotisme — anak perempuan Anwar, Nurul Izzah, menewaskan Rafizi untuk jawatan timbalan presiden. Apabila anak perempuan PM menang pertandingan dalaman dan yang kalah dipaksa keluar daripada Kabinet, itu bukan pembaharuan demokratik. Itu politik feudal berselindung di sebalik retorik reformasi.

Pemergian serentak Menteri Ekonomi dan Menteri Alam Sekitar mewujudkan ketidaktentuan dasar berbulan-bulan dalam dua portfolio kritikal, dengan kekosongan tidak diisi sehingga Disember 2025.

💩 MENGAPA IA KARUT

Nik Nazmi layak menerima sedikit pujian atas pendiriannya yang berprinsip — beliau antara sedikit ahli politik Malaysia yang rela melepaskan kuasa. Tetapi sistem yang menghasilkan keputusan ini busuk.

Pilihan raya parti PKR sepatutnya menjadi latihan demokratik dalaman. Sebaliknya, ia menjadi perang proksi untuk penyatuan kuasa keluarga Anwar. Kemenangan Nurul Izzah ke atas Rafizi bukan sekadar pertandingan parti — ia adalah anak perempuan PM menewaskan teknokrat ekonomi paling cekap beliau, membawa kepada kekosongan dasar berbulan-bulan di Kementerian Ekonomi.

Dan respons Anwar? Beliau memohon Nik Nazmi untuk kekal — bukan kerana kesinambungan dasar, tetapi kerana dua perletakan jawatan menteri nampak buruk. PM tidak mampu mengawal kesannya daripada pilihan raya partinya sendiri. Ini parti ‘reformis’ yang kononnya berbeza daripada politik feudal UMNO.

📜 KES TERDAHULU

Sejarah UMNO dipenuhi pergolakan kuasa dalaman yang melimpah ke dalam tadbir urus — pergaduhan puak antara Mahathir dan Ku Li, antara Mahathir dan Anwar, antara Najib dan Muhyiddin. PKR sepatutnya berbeza. Ia diasaskan atas prinsip reformasi. Sebaliknya, ia mereplikakan politik dalaman berasaskan naungan yang sama, lengkap dengan pertempuran penggantian dinasti.

Pilihan raya PKR 2025 juga menggemakan pilihan raya parti 2018, yang turut dicemari dakwaan penyelewengan serupa. Parti ini tidak pernah berjaya mengadakan pilihan raya dalaman yang bersih dalam seluruh sejarahnya.

🎯 Seruan Kepada PH

Anda membina PKR atas janji reformasi demokratik. Kemudian pilihan raya parti anda dicemari dakwaan penipuan undi, anak perempuan anda menewaskan reformis ekonomi paling terkemuka anda, dan dua menteri meletak jawatan sebagai protes. Hasilnya? Kekosongan dasar berbulan-bulan di kementerian Ekonomi dan Alam Sekitar. Ini bukan reformasi — ini UMNO dengan jenama yang lebih baik. Jika PKR tidak boleh menjalankan pilihan raya dalaman yang bersih, mengapa rakyat Malaysia harus mempercayai anda untuk mereformasi negara?

[BM] Rafizi Ramli Letak Jawatan Menteri Ekonomi Selepas Dimalukan PKR — Reformis Yang Disingkirkan

Said by: Rafizi Ramli, former Economy Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-05-28

📌 APA YANG BERLAKU

Pada 28 Mei 2025, Menteri Ekonomi Rafizi Ramli menyerahkan perletakan jawatannya daripada Kabinet, berkuat kuasa 17 Jun. Sekutunya, Menteri Sumber Asli Nik Nazmi Nik Ahmad, turut mengumumkan pemergiannya, berkuat kuasa 4 Julai.

Rafizi berkata: “Saya memasuki politik untuk memupuk budaya politik baru yang berasaskan akauntabiliti dan mandat rakyat. Kekalahan saya baru-baru ini dalam pilihan raya PKR menandakan saya tidak lagi memiliki mandat parti.”

Beliau menyatakan bahawa di negara yang berprinsipkan demokrasi, “pemimpin yang kalah dalam pilihan raya parti harus memberi laluan kepada pemenang.” Tindakan terakhir beliau sebagai menteri adalah menyiapkan Rancangan Malaysia Ke-13.

Anwar dilaporkan menggesa Rafizi mempertimbangkan semula. Beliau enggan.

🔥 MENGAPA IA PENTING

Rafizi adalah reformis paling berkredibiliti dalam Kabinet Anwar. Beliau mereka bentuk PADU, mendorong rasionalisasi subsidi, memperjuangkan tadbir urus berasaskan data, dan menjadi enjin intelektual di sebalik agenda reformasi ekonomi PH. Pemergiannya meninggalkan kekosongan pada saat Malaysia memerlukan pengawasan ekonomi yang mantap.

Bloomberg menyebutnya “tamparan kepada kerajaan Anwar.” Straits Times menyatakan pemergiannya “akan meninggalkan jurang pada masa reformasi ekonomi Malaysia terbantut.” CNA melaporkan pilihan raya parti “dicemari dakwaan penipuan undi dan nepotisme.”

Dua menteri meletak jawatan atas pilihan raya parti bukanlah biasa. Ia isyarat bahawa sesuatu amat tidak kena di dalam PKR.

💩 MENGAPA IA KARUT

Anwar kehilangan menteri terbaiknya kerana beliau membenarkan — atau membolehkan — anak perempuannya mencabar dan menghancurkan penyandang. Rafizi tidak kalah kerana tidak cekap. Beliau kalah kerana jentera parti digerakkan untuk anak perempuan bos.

Hakikat bahawa Anwar kemudian meminta Rafizi untuk kekal adalah bahagian paling sinis. Anda mendalangi penghinaan, kemudian meminta yang dihina untuk terus bekerja untuk anda. Itu bukan kepimpinan — itu manipulasi.

Sejak pemergian Rafizi, beliau menjadi pengkritik yang semakin lantang. Beliau memberi amaran kepada Anwar jangan “memandang rendah sentimen rakyat.” Beliau menggesa penggantungan ketua SPRM Azam Baki. Beliau kini sendiri di bawah siasatan SPRM — pemasaan yang pengkritik panggil sebagai tindakan balas.

📜 KES TERDAHULU

Politik Malaysia mempunyai tradisi lama menyingkirkan saingan dalaman. Mahathir meminggirkan Anwar pada 1998. Kini Anwar secara efektif meminggirkan Rafizi pada 2025. Kaedahnya berbeza — pilihan raya parti dan bukannya tuduhan liwat — tetapi hasilnya sama: saingan paling berkebolehan pemimpin disingkirkan.

🎯 Seruan Kepada PH

Anda kehilangan minda ekonomi terbaik anda kerana politik keluarga lebih penting daripada kepentingan nasional. Rafizi membina kredibiliti dasar anda. Beliau sebab pelabur memandang serius PH. Dan anda biarkan beliau pergi — atau menolaknya keluar. Rancangan Malaysia Ke-13 adalah hadiah terakhirnya kepada anda. Apa yang anda lakukan dengannya sejak itu?

Nik Nazmi Resigns as Environment Minister After Losing PKR Party Election — Exposes PH's Feudal Politics

Said by: Nik Nazmi Nik Ahmad, Former Natural Resources and Environmental Sustainability Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-05-28

📌 WHAT HAPPENED

On May 28, 2025, Natural Resources and Environmental Sustainability Minister Nik Nazmi Nik Ahmad tendered his resignation from Cabinet, effective July 4. His resignation came hours after Economy Minister Rafizi Ramli also resigned — both following their defeats in PKR’s internal party elections five days earlier.

Nik Nazmi lost his PKR vice-president position and said he could no longer serve as minister without a full party mandate. “Being a minister can trap you in a ‘bubble’ — with privileges, security escorts, and protocols at every event,” he said. “I believe that becoming too attached to such a lifestyle can be dangerous.”

Anwar publicly asked Nik Nazmi to reconsider. Nik Nazmi refused, saying his decision was firm and he would focus on serving his Setiawangsa constituents.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS

Two Cabinet ministers resigning within hours of each other over internal party politics is not normal governance. It exposes the fact that PH ministerial appointments are fundamentally driven by party faction dynamics, not competence or national interest.

The PKR party election that triggered these resignations was itself marred by allegations of vote-rigging and nepotism — Anwar’s daughter Nurul Izzah defeated Rafizi for deputy president. When the PM’s daughter wins an internal contest and the losers are forced out of Cabinet, that’s not democratic renewal. That’s feudal politics dressed up in reform rhetoric.

The simultaneous departure of the Economy Minister and Environment Minister created months of policy uncertainty in two critical portfolios, with vacancies unfilled until December 2025.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT

Nik Nazmi deserves some credit for his principled stance — he’s one of the few Malaysian politicians who voluntarily gave up power. But the system that produced this outcome is rotten.

PKR’s party elections are supposed to be internal democratic exercises. Instead, they became proxy wars for the Anwar family’s consolidation of power. Nurul Izzah’s victory over Rafizi wasn’t just a party contest — it was the PM’s daughter defeating his most capable economic technocrat, leading to months of policy vacuum in the Economy Ministry.

And Anwar’s response? He begged Nik Nazmi to stay — not because of policy continuity, but because two ministerial resignations looked bad. The PM couldn’t even control the fallout from his own party’s elections. This is the “reformist” party that’s supposed to be different from UMNO’s feudal politics.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES

UMNO’s history is littered with internal power struggles that spilled into governance — factional fights between Mahathir and Ku Li, between Mahathir and Anwar, between Najib and Muhyiddin. PKR was supposed to be different. It was founded on reformasi principles. Instead, it’s replicated the exact same patronage-driven internal politics, complete with a dynastic succession battle.

The PKR 2025 election also echoed the 2018 party polls, which were marred by similar allegations of irregularities. The party has never managed a clean internal election in its entire history.

🎯 PH CALLOUT

You built PKR on the promise of democratic reform. Then your party election was marred by vote-rigging allegations, your daughter defeated your most prominent economic reformer, and two ministers resigned in protest. The result? Months of policy vacuum in the Economy and Environment ministries. This isn’t reform — it’s UMNO with better branding. If PKR can’t even run a clean internal election, why should Malaysians trust you to reform the country?

Rafizi Ramli Resigns as Economy Minister After PKR Humiliation — The Reformist Who Got Purged

Said by: Rafizi Ramli, former Economy Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-05-28

📌 WHAT HAPPENED

On 28 May 2025, Economy Minister Rafizi Ramli submitted his resignation from Cabinet, effective 17 June. His ally, Natural Resources Minister Nik Nazmi Nik Ahmad, also announced his exit, effective 4 July.

Rafizi said: “I entered politics to cultivate a new political culture grounded in accountability and the people’s mandate. My recent defeat in the PKR elections signifies that I no longer possess the party’s mandate.”

He noted that in countries with democratic principles, “leaders who lose in party elections should make way for the victors.” His final act as minister was completing the 13th Malaysia Plan.

Anwar reportedly urged Rafizi to reconsider. He refused.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS

Rafizi was the most credible reformist in Anwar’s Cabinet. He designed PADU, pushed subsidy rationalisation, championed data-driven governance, and was the intellectual engine behind PH’s economic reform agenda. His departure left a vacuum at exactly the moment Malaysia needed steady economic stewardship.

Bloomberg called it “a blow to Anwar’s government.” The Straits Times noted his exit “will leave a gap at a time when Malaysia’s economic reforms are stalling.” CNA reported that the party election was “marred by allegations of vote-rigging and nepotism.”

Two ministers quitting over a party election isn’t normal. It’s a signal that something is deeply wrong inside PKR.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT

Anwar lost his best minister because he allowed — or enabled — his daughter to challenge and destroy the incumbent. Rafizi didn’t lose because he was incompetent. He lost because the party machinery was mobilised for the boss’s daughter.

The fact that Anwar then asked Rafizi to stay is the most cynical part. You orchestrate a humiliation, then ask the humiliated to keep working for you. That’s not leadership — that’s gaslighting.

Since Rafizi’s departure, he has become an increasingly vocal critic. He warned Anwar not to “underestimate public sentiment.” He called for MACC chief Azam Baki’s suspension. He’s now under MACC investigation himself — a timing that critics call retaliatory.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES

Malaysian politics has a long tradition of purging internal rivals. Mahathir sidelined Anwar in 1998. Now Anwar has effectively sidelined Rafizi in 2025. The method is different — party elections instead of sodomy charges — but the outcome is the same: the leader’s most capable rival is removed.

🎯 PH CALLOUT

You lost your best economic mind because family politics mattered more than national interest. Rafizi built your policy credibility. He was the reason investors took PH seriously. And you let him walk — or pushed him out. The 13th Malaysia Plan was his last gift to you. What have you done with it since?

[BM] Nurul Izzah Menang Timbalan Presiden PKR — Anwar Kata Demokrasi, Bukan Nepotisme

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister & PKR President

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-05-24

Daripada Penasihat Kepada Timbalan Presiden: Dinasti Anwar Terbentuk

Pada Mei 2025, Nurul Izzah Anwar dipilih sebagai Timbalan Presiden PKR — jawatan nombor dua dalam parti yang diketuai bapanya, Perdana Menteri yang sedang berkhidmat. Anwar Ibrahim menolak semua kritikan dengan lambaian tangan: dia dipilih oleh ahli. Kes ditutup. Kecuali ia tidak.

Apa Yang Berlaku

Di Kongres Nasional PKR di Johor Bahru pada 24 Mei 2025, Nurul Izzah memenangi pertandingan Timbalan Presiden. Jawatan presiden — yang dipegang Anwar — tidak dipertandingkan. Ini bermakna dua jawatan teratas PKR kini dipegang oleh ayah dan anak perempuan.

Respons Anwar adalah terus:

Orang akan kata isteri dan anak saya dalam parti. Itu mungkin benar, tetapi semasa kita berjuang pada 1998, kongres pun tak ada.

Masalah Dengan “Dipilih Oleh Ahli”

Ya, ahli parti mengundi. Tetapi konteks penting:

  • Anwar adalah presiden parti. Sokongannya — walaupun tidak dinyatakan — membawa pengaruh besar dalam pilihan raya dalaman. Ahli PKR tahu bahawa mengundi menentang anak perempuan presiden membawa risiko politik.
  • Naungan mengalir ke bawah. Dalam sistem di mana PM mengawal pelantikan kerajaan, kontrak, dan jawatan GLC, ahli parti yang bersekutu dengan keluarga kepimpinan berada dalam kedudukan lebih baik. Ini tekanan struktur, bukan pilihan bebas.
  • Bekas pembantu Ezam Nor menjadi tular menjelaskan bahawa ini kurang tentang demokrasi dan lebih tentang Anwar menyatukan kuasa menjelang cabaran politik dan perundangan yang dijangka.

Penjejak Dinasti

  • Dr Wan Azizah Wan Ismail (isteri Anwar): bekas Presiden PKR, bekas Timbalan PM
  • Nurul Izzah (anak perempuan): bekas AP, Penasihat Kanan Ekonomi kepada PM, kini Timbalan Presiden PKR
  • Keluarga Anwar telah memegang atau kini memegang dua jawatan parti teratas dan peranan penasihat kanan kerajaan Fakta: Tiada keluarga politik lain dalam gerakan reformis Malaysia memegang penumpuan kuasa ini dalam satu parti.

Hujah Terbaik (Dan Mengapa Ia Gagal)

Nurul Izzah telah berkecimpung dalam politik sejak usia dua puluhan, bertahan melawat penjara, berkempen semasa penahanan bapanya, dan mempunyai kelayakan dasar. Beliau bukan tidak layak. Tetapi persoalannya bukan tentang kelayakan — ia tentang sama ada parti reformis patut mempunyai struktur kepimpinan ayah-anak perempuan sementara bapanya juga Perdana Menteri. Di mana-mana negara demokrasi matang, ini akan dianggap bendera merah tadbir urus.

Apa Yang Patut Berlaku

  1. PKR patut menerima pakai peraturan menghalang ahli keluarga terdekat presiden daripada memegang jawatan timbalan presiden secara serentak.
  2. Anwar patut secara terbuka kekal berkecuali dalam pertandingan tersebut.
  3. Masyarakat sivil Malaysia patut berhenti menerima “ahli yang memilih” sebagai jawapan lengkap apabila bapa yang memilih mengawal parti dan kerajaan.

Keputusan: Gerakan reformasi lahir daripada penentangan terhadap penumpuan kuasa Mahathir. Tiga dekad kemudian, penumpuan baru sedang terbentuk — kali ini dengan lambang keluarga padanya.

Nurul Izzah Wins PKR Deputy President — Anwar Says It's Democracy, Not Nepotism

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister & PKR President

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-05-24

From Adviser to Deputy President: The Anwar Dynasty Takes Shape

In May 2025, Nurul Izzah Anwar was elected PKR Deputy President — the number two position in the party led by her father, the sitting Prime Minister. Anwar Ibrahim dismissed all criticism with a wave of the hand: she was chosen by members. Case closed. Except it isn’t.

What Happened

At the PKR National Congress in Johor Bahru on 24 May 2025, Nurul Izzah won the contest for Deputy President. The presidency — held by Anwar — was not up for contest. This means the top two positions in PKR are now held by father and daughter.

Anwar’s response was direct:

People will say that my wife and daughter are in the party. That may be true, but when we were fighting in 1998, there wasn’t even a congress.

The Problem With “Chosen by Members”

Yes, party members voted. But context matters:

  • Anwar is the party president. His endorsement — even if unstated — carries enormous weight in internal elections. PKR members know that voting against the president’s daughter carries political risk.
  • Patronage flows downward. In a system where the PM controls government appointments, contracts, and GLC positions, party members who align with the leadership family are better positioned. This is structural pressure, not free choice.
  • Former aide Ezam Nor went viral explaining that this was less about democracy and more about Anwar consolidating power ahead of anticipated political and legal challenges.

The Dynasty Tracker

  • Dr Wan Azizah Wan Ismail (Anwar’s wife): former PKR President, former Deputy PM
  • Nurul Izzah (daughter): former MP, Senior Economic Adviser to PM, now PKR Deputy President
  • The Anwar family has held or currently holds the top two party positions and a senior government advisory role Fact: No other political family in Malaysia’s reformist movement holds this concentration of power within a single party.

The Steelman (and Why It Fails)

Nurul Izzah has been in politics since her twenties, survived prison visits, campaigned during her father’s incarceration, and has policy credentials. She is not unqualified. But the question is not about qualifications — it is about whether a reformist party should have a father-daughter leadership structure while the father is also Prime Minister. In any mature democracy, this would be considered a governance red flag.

What Should Happen

  1. PKR should adopt rules preventing immediate family members of the president from holding the deputy presidency simultaneously.
  2. Anwar should have publicly stayed neutral in the contest.
  3. Malaysian civil society should stop accepting “the members chose” as a complete answer when the chooser’s father controls the party and the government.

Verdict: The reformasi movement was born out of opposition to Mahathir’s concentration of power. Three decades later, a new concentration is forming — this time with a family crest on it.

[BM] Nurul Izzah Tewaskan Rafizi Dengan 6,000 Undi Untuk Timbalan Presiden PKR — Parti Ayah, Peraturan Ayah

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-05-23

📌 APA YANG BERLAKU

Pada 23 Mei 2025, Nurul Izzah Anwar — anak sulung Perdana Menteri Anwar Ibrahim — menewaskan timbalan presiden penyandang Rafizi Ramli dalam pilihan raya dalaman PKR dengan margin yang meyakinkan: 9,803 undi berbanding 3,866.

Keputusannya tidak pernah diragui. Lebih 200 daripada 222 bahagian PKR telah mengisytiharkan sokongan untuk Nurul Izzah sebelum undian dijalankan. Pilihan raya diadakan di Johor Bahru, dengan presiden parti Anwar Ibrahim mengekalkan jawatan teratas tanpa dicabar.

Beberapa hari kemudian, Rafizi meletak jawatan sebagai Menteri Ekonomi, berkata beliau “tidak lagi memiliki mandat parti.” Sekutunya, Menteri Sumber Asli Nik Nazmi, turut meletak jawatan.

🔥 MENGAPA IA PENTING

Anak perempuan PM kini orang kedua paling berkuasa dalam parti yang mentadbir Malaysia. Ini bukan sekadar pilihan raya parti — ia adalah penyatuan dinasti.

Anwar Ibrahim menghabiskan 25 tahun berkhutbah tentang reformasi. Beliau berjanji untuk memutuskan kitaran naungan, nepotisme, dan tangkapan elit yang mendefinisikan politik Malaysia. Kini anak perempuannya memegang jawatan No. 2 parti, isterinya presiden PKR Wanita, dan penentang politiknya di dalam parti telah dipinggirkan secara sistematik.

SCMP melaporkannya sebagai “kontroversi nepotisme.” Bloomberg menyebutnya “tamparan kepada kerajaan Anwar.” Hanya di PKR ada yang berpura-pura ini pertandingan demokratik biasa.

💩 MENGAPA IA KARUT

Mari kita jelaskan apa yang berlaku: anak perempuan PM bertanding menentang Menteri Ekonominya sendiri untuk kawalan parti, dan jentera parti menyampaikan kemenangan besar untuk anak bos. Lebih 200 bahagian mengisytiharkan untuk Nurul Izzah sebelum sehelai undi dibuang. Itu bukan demokrasi — itu pertabalan.

Rafizi — orang yang membina kredibiliti dasar PKR, yang mereka bentuk PADU, yang mendorong rasionalisasi subsidi — diganjar dengan penghinaan. Mesej kepada setiap reformis dalam PKR tidak boleh disalah erti: kesetiaan kepada keluarga mengatasi kecekapan.

Nurul Izzah bahkan ditawarkan peluang untuk mewujudkan jawatan “timbalan presiden kedua” untuk Rafizi. Beliau tidak menafikannya. Hadiah saguhati kerana kalah kepada anak perempuan bos adalah gelaran yang dicipta. Itu bukan parti politik — itu firma keluarga.

📜 KES TERDAHULU

Anwar sudah melantik Nurul Izzah sebagai Penasihat Kanan Ekonomi pada Januari 2023, walaupun beliau kehilangan kerusi parlimen. Beliau menolak dakwaan nepotisme dengan mendefinisikan semula perkataan itu. Kini corak itu meningkat: daripada penasihat tidak dipilih kepada timbalan presiden parti. Perhentian seterusnya: Timbalan PM?

Nurul Izzah Defeats Rafizi by 6,000 Votes for PKR Deputy President — Daddy's Party, Daddy's Rules

Said by: PKR Party Elections / Nurul Izzah Anwar

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-05-23

📌 WHAT HAPPENED

On 23 May 2025, Nurul Izzah Anwar — the eldest daughter of Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim — defeated incumbent deputy president Rafizi Ramli in PKR’s internal elections by a commanding margin: 9,803 votes to 3,866.

The result was never in doubt. Over 200 of PKR’s 222 divisions had declared support for Nurul Izzah before the vote even took place. The election was held in Johor Bahru, with party president Anwar Ibrahim retaining the top post unchallenged.

Days later, Rafizi resigned as Economy Minister, saying he “no longer possesses the party’s mandate.” His ally, Natural Resources Minister Nik Nazmi, also quit.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS

The PM’s daughter is now the second-most powerful person in the party that runs Malaysia. This isn’t just a party election — it’s a dynastic consolidation.

Anwar Ibrahim spent 25 years preaching reformasi. He promised to break the cycle of patronage, nepotism, and elite capture that defined Malaysian politics. Now his daughter holds the No. 2 party position, his wife is the president of PKR Wanita, and his political opponents within the party have been systematically sidelined.

The SCMP reported it as a “nepotism row.” Bloomberg called it a “blow to Anwar’s government.” Only in PKR would anyone pretend this was a normal democratic contest.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT

Let’s be clear about what happened: the PM’s daughter ran against his own Economy Minister for control of the party, and the party apparatus delivered a landslide for the boss’s kid. Over 200 divisions declared for Nurul Izzah before a single ballot was cast. That’s not democracy — that’s a coronation.

Rafizi — the man who built PKR’s policy credibility, who designed PADU, who pushed subsidy rationalisation — was rewarded with humiliation. The message to every reformist in PKR is unmistakable: loyalty to the family trumps competence.

Nurul Izzah was even offered the chance to create a “second deputy president” post for Rafizi. She didn’t deny it. The consolation prize for losing to the boss’s daughter is a made-up title. That’s not a political party — it’s a family firm.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES

Anwar already appointed Nurul Izzah as Senior Economic Adviser in January 2023, despite her losing her parliamentary seat. He dismissed nepotism accusations by redefining the word. Now the pattern escalates: from unelected adviser to party deputy president. Next stop: Deputy PM?

Malaysia has a long history of political dynasties — Mahathir groomed his son Mukhriz, Najib inherited his father’s legacy. Anwar promised to be different. He isn’t.

🎯 PH CALLOUT

You told us reformasi was about breaking the old ways. About meritocracy. About ending the politics of patronage and family. Your daughter now controls your party’s No. 2 slot with a 6,000-vote margin that was engineered before the ballots were printed. Your Economy Minister quit in protest. Your reform agenda lost its most credible champion. And you called it democracy. The only thing reformed in PKR is the family’s grip on power.
