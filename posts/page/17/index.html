[BM] Anwar Megah Pelaburan 'Rekod' RM426.7 Bilion Diluluskan — Tapi 'Diluluskan' Bukan Bermakna 'Tiba'

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister and Finance Minister

Category: Economy

Date: 2026-03-06

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Pada 6 Mac 2026, Perdana Menteri Anwar Ibrahim mengumumkan bahawa Malaysia mencatat pelaburan diluluskan tertinggi sebanyak RM426.7 bilion pada 2025 — peningkatan 11% daripada RM384.4 bilion pada 2024. Menurut MIDA, 8,390 projek diluluskan, dengan potensi mewujudkan 244,902 pekerjaan. Anwar membingkainya sebagai pembuktian agenda reformasinya dan bukti keyakinan global terhadap hala tuju ekonomi Malaysia.

Pengumuman dibuat semasa pelancaran Pelan Induk Pasaran Modal 2026-2030, dengan Anwar juga berkhidmat sebagai Menteri Kewangan — peranan dwi yang dipegangnya sejak berkuasa pada 2022.

🔥 Mengapa Ia Penting

Kerana pelaburan diluluskan tidak sama dengan pelaburan direalisasikan, dan rakyat Malaysia telah diberi tajuk utama tanpa pembezaan ini selama berdekad-dekad.

MIDA menjejaki kelulusan — pada dasarnya, surat niat dan sijil pelaburan. Ini adalah komitmen di atas kertas. Wang sebenar yang mengalir ke dalam ekonomi — pelaburan langsung asing (FDI) direalisasikan — secara sejarahnya 50-60% daripada angka diluluskan di Malaysia. Ini bermakna sehingga separuh daripada angka tajuk utama ini mungkin tidak pernah diterjemahkan kepada kilang, pekerjaan, atau aktiviti ekonomi sebenar.

Anwar menggunakan angka pelaburan diluluskan sama seperti setiap PM Malaysia sebelum beliau: sebagai tajuk utama politik, bukan semakan realiti ekonomi. 244,902 pekerjaan itu adalah pekerjaan berpotensi — bukan disahkan, bukan diwujudkan, bukan dijamin.

💩 Mengapa Ia Karut

Mari kita tepat tentang apa yang berlaku: Perdana Menteri berdiri di podium dan meraikan nombor yang beliau tahu tidak mencerminkan pelaburan sebenar.

Setiap laporan tahunan MIDA membezakan antara pelaburan diluluskan dan direalisasikan. Setiap ahli ekonomi serius di Malaysia tahu jurang realisasi. Anwar — seorang yang terlibat dalam penggubalan dasar ekonomi Malaysia selama tiga dekad — tahu ini juga.

Namun: tiada kadar realisasi didedahkan. Tiada pecahan berapa banyak pelaburan diluluskan 2023 atau 2024 benar-benar terwujud. Tiada metrik akauntabiliti. Hanya nombor terbesar, tajuk utama terbaik, dan dakwaan “keyakinan global.”

Ini adalah setara ekonomi mengira RSVP sebagai kehadiran. Anda tidak boleh mendakwa 244,902 pekerjaan apabila pekerjaan itu hanya wujud dalam surat kelulusan. Anda tidak boleh mendakwa RM426.7 bilion apabila sebahagian besar daripadanya mungkin tidak pernah tiba.

📜 Kes Terdahulu

Ini bukan baru. Setiap kerajaan Malaysia telah bermain permainan diluluskan-lawan-direalisasikan:

  • Di bawah Najib, pelaburan diluluskan secara rutin melebihi RM200B+ setiap tahun, tetapi FDI direalisasikan jauh ketinggalan.
  • Di bawah Mahathir 2.0, corak yang sama berterusan — kelulusan besar, prestasi rendah secara senyap.
  • Kerajaan Anwar sendiri tidak menerbitkan audit realisasi komprehensif dan telus sejak berkuasa. Coraknya sentiasa sama: umumkan nombor sebesar mungkin di sidang akhbar, biarkan media jalankan tajuk utama, dan jangan sekali-kali tindak balas dengan apa yang sebenarnya berlaku.

🎯 Seruan PH

Anda menjanjikan ketelusan dan akauntabiliti sebagai jenama anda. Anda berkata anda berbeza daripada BN. Anda berkata anda tidak akan memutar nombor.

Jadi di mana laporan realisasi? Di mana pecahan berapa banyak pelaburan diluluskan 2023 dan 2024 benar-benar muncul sebagai wang sebenar, kilang sebenar, dan pekerjaan sebenar?

Jika anda mahu kredit untuk RM426.7 bilion, tunjukkan resit — bukan jemputan. Sehingga itu, ini adalah teater ekonomi sama yang ditonton rakyat Malaysia sejak 1990-an, cuma dengan cat reformis.

[BM] Anwar Nafi Pilihan Raya Kilat Pada Disember — Kini Kerajaannya Senyap-Senyap Bersedia Untuk Pilihan Raya 2026

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister / Unity Government sources

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-06

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Pada Disember 2025, Perdana Menteri Anwar Ibrahim memberitahu wartawan bahawa pilihan raya kilat tidak dalam pertimbangan — keutamaannya, katanya, ialah melaksanakan reformasi dan menangani kos sara hidup. Mara pantas ke Mac 2026: pelbagai orang dalam kerajaan, pemimpin akar umbi parti, dan penjawat awam memberitahu CNA bahawa persediaan sedang berjalan secara senyap untuk kemungkinan pilihan raya umum pada separuh kedua 2026. Ahli politik Johor dari kerajaan perpaduan berkata akar umbi telah diberitahu untuk meningkatkan persediaan selepas Hari Raya Aidilfitri. Penjawat awam kanan mengesahkan pegawai pentadbiran kerajaan telah diletakkan dalam keadaan berwaspada dan sumber ditetapkan “dalam keadaan bersedia” dari Jun. Timbalan Pengerusi SPR Azmi Sharom berkata suruhanjaya bersedia, termasuk untuk pilihan raya negeri dan parlimen serentak.

🔥 Kenapa Ia Penting

Ini isyarat paling jelas lagi bahawa kerajaan Anwar bergerak ke arah pilihan raya kilat meskipun menafikannya secara terbuka. Jurang antara apa yang PH kata dan apa yang PH lakukan terus melebar. Jika pilihan raya diadakan pada lewat 2026, bermakna kerajaan Madani akan memerintah selama kira-kira empat tahun tanpa menyampaikan janji reformasi utama.

💩 Kenapa Ia Karut

Anwar kata reformasi dahulu, pilihan raya kemudian. Kini pilihan raya dahulu, reformasi tidak pernah. Sebabnya telus: pembangkang dalam keadaan kucar-kacir. Bersatu berpecah antara puak Muhyiddin dan Hamzah, Rafizi mengapung haluan berasingan, dan PAS belum mengukuhkan strateginya selepas Sabah. Ini bukan kerajaan yang pergi kepada rakyat dengan rekod penyampaian — ia kerajaan yang bergegas ke peti undi sebelum tingkap kelemahan pembangkang tertutup.

📜 Kes Terdahulu

PM-PM Malaysia mempunyai tradisi panjang memanggil pilihan raya apabila pembangkang lemah dan bukannya apabila kerajaan kuat. Mahathir melakukannya. Najib mencubanya pada 2018 dan gagal. Rang undang-undang had tempoh PM — yang baru gagal — sepatutnya menjadi penawar struktur. Sebaliknya, kerajaan dilaporkan mempertimbangkan jenis manuver pilihan raya kilat yang tepat yang rang undang-undang itu direka untuk menghalang.

🎯 Panggilan PH

Anda menjanjikan Malaysia reformasi institusi. Anda berjanji kuasa tidak akan dilaksanakan mengikut kehendak kalkulus politik seorang lelaki. Kini jentera anda dalam keadaan bersedia, akar umbi anda sedang bergerak, dan SPR anda secara terbuka mengesahkan kesediaan — semuanya sementara anda beritahu kamera pilihan raya kilat tidak berlaku. Sama ada beritahu kebenaran dan panggil pilihan raya, atau berhenti bersiap sedia untuk satu. Rakyat bukan prop dalam garis masa pemilihan semula anda.

[BM] Anwar Suruh Bersih Berhenti Kritik SPRM 'Semasa Ramadan' — Guna Bulan Suci Sebagai Perisai Terhadap Akauntabiliti

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-06

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Pada 6 Mac 2026, Perdana Menteri Anwar Ibrahim secara terbuka menolak tuduhan Gabungan Pilihan Raya Bersih dan Adil (Bersih) bahawa Suruhanjaya Pencegahan Rasuah Malaysia (SPRM) telah menjadi alat politik kerajaan. Berucap selepas program Ihya’ Ramadan Jabatan Perkhidmatan Awam di Putrajaya, Anwar memanggil kenyataan Bersih “tidak adil” dan berkata kumpulan itu “tidak membuat kenyataan bersih.” Beliau kemudian menambah: “Jangan serang orang tanpa terlebih dahulu mendapatkan maklumat, terutama semasa Ramadan.”

🔥 Kenapa Ia Penting

Kritikan Bersih bukan secara rawak — ia respons langsung kepada bukti yang semakin meningkat bahawa SPRM di bawah Azam Baki telah digunakan untuk menakut-nakutkan lawan politik dan wartawan. Apabila NGO reformasi pilihan raya paling terkemuka Malaysia berkata agensi antirasuah anda telah menyeleweng, respons yang betul ialah mengambilnya secara serius, bukan memohon Ramadan sebagai alasan untuk berhenti bertanya soalan.

💩 Kenapa Ia Karut

Menggunakan Ramadan untuk mengelak kritikan akauntabiliti adalah langkah retorik yang sinis. Bulan suci adalah tentang refleksi, keadilan, dan disiplin moral — bukan tentang melindungi agensi kerajaan daripada penelitian. Barisan Anwar — “Jangan serang orang tanpa maklumat, terutama semasa Ramadan” — mencampuradukkan kritikan institusi yang sah dengan serangan peribadi, dan kemudian membalut pengelakan itu dengan sentimen agama. Bersih bukan menyerang seseorang; ia mempersoalkan sama ada agensi antirasuah negara layak berkhidmat.

📜 Kes Terdahulu

Anwar mempunyai corak yang didokumenkan dengan baik menggunakan bingkai moral untuk membungkam kritikan. Apabila Bloomberg melaporkan mengenai pegangan saham Azam Baki, naluri pertama kerajaan adalah membingkaikannya sebagai komplot asing dan bukannya menyiasat kandungannya. Ini buku panduan yang sama yang digunakan setiap PM Malaysia sebelum Anwar — mengelak, menangguh, dan menuduh pengkritik berniat buruk.

🎯 Panggilan PH

Anda berjanji menjadi kerajaan yang mendengar masyarakat sivil. Bersih berarak untuk anda. Bersih berjuang untuk pilihan raya bersih yang meletakkan anda berkuasa. Dan kini, apabila mereka membangkitkan kebimbangan sah tentang persenjataan SPRM, respons anda ialah menyuruh mereka berdiam diri semasa Ramadan? Ramadan menyeru keadilan, bukan kesunyian. Berhenti bersembunyi di sebalik jawatankuasa dan bulan suci. Jawab soalannya: Adakah SPRM bebas, atau tidak?

238 Political Appointments to FSBs and GLCs Under Anwar — IDEAS Report Exposes 'Connections Over Competence'

Said by: IDEAS Report / Anwar Ibrahim Administration

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-06

📌 What Happened

The Institute for Democracy and Economic Affairs (IDEAS) released a report titled Who Sits at the Table: From Political to Public Appointees in Malaysia’s Federal Statutory Bodies on March 6, 2026, revealing that 238 political appointments were made to federal statutory bodies (FSBs) and government-linked companies (GLCs) under Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim’s administration between November 2022 and October 2025. Of these, 222 were to FSBs and 16 to GLCs. The report reviewed 135 agencies and found that 78% of chairperson positions were filled based on political connections rather than competence. Most lacked clearly defined qualification criteria. The appointments were concentrated in key ministries, and the governance provisions around them were described as inconsistent and fragmented.

🔥 Why It Matters

This report is a direct, data-backed indictment of PH’s reform credentials. Anwar came to power promising meritocracy, transparency, and an end to the patronage politics that defined BN-era governance. Three years in, an independent, respected think tank has documented that political appointments haven’t just continued — they dominate. When 78% of the people running your statutory bodies got the job through connections rather than qualifications, you are not reforming governance. You are changing the names on the patronage list. This is not a fringe accusation — IDEAS is one of Malaysia’s most credible policy institutes, and its Pantau Kuasa initiative has tracked appointments across multiple administrations.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

PH sold voters the idea that they were fundamentally different from UMNO and BN. That the era of politically connected chairs presiding over government agencies was over. IDEAS’ numbers say otherwise: 238 appointments in under three years, with the overwhelming majority driven by political affiliation rather than professional merit. This is not reform — it is rotation. Different coalition, same playbook. The only thing that changed is the party logo on the appointment letters. Anwar has gone from demanding accountability for political appointments in opposition to treating them as a natural entitlement of power in government. If competence mattered, these numbers would look different. They do not.

📜 Previous Cases

Political appointments to statutory bodies and GLCs have been a recurring feature of Malaysian governance for decades. Under Najib Razak, the practice was widely criticised — particularly at 1MDB, Tabung Haji, and FELDA, where politically connected chairs presided over mismanagement and outright corruption. PH’s 2018 manifesto explicitly promised to end this, and the short-lived PH 1.0 government made some initial moves toward meritocratic appointments before collapsing in the Sheraton Move. Under PH 2.0, the pattern has returned with a vengeance. IDEAS’ Pantau Kuasa data shows that Anwar’s government is not the outlier it promised to be — it is the continuation of a structural problem that PH once made its name fighting.

🎯 PH Callout

You promised Malaysians a new Malaysia. You said the era of political cronies was over. You told voters that under Madani, competence would matter more than connections. IDEAS just put a number on that promise: 238 political appointments, 78% driven by politics, not merit. That is not reform — that is business as usual with better branding. If PH cannot even clean up the appointment process for statutory bodies — a basic, achievable governance reform with no legal barriers — why should anyone believe the bigger promises about institutional independence, anti-corruption, or meritocracy? The data speaks louder than the slogans.

Anwar Admits He Can Only Hold RON95 Subsidy for 'One or Two Months' — While Fuel Prices Spike Across the Board

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister and Finance Minister

Category: Economy

Date: 2026-03-06

📌 What Happened

On March 6, 2026, Prime Minister and Finance Minister Anwar Ibrahim told reporters that Malaysia can maintain the subsidised RON95 petrol price at RM1.99 per litre under the Budi95 scheme — but only for ‘one or two months.’ His exact words: ‘We will try to control the effects of the Iranian conflict, including for RON95, which is RM1.99 per litre. We will monitor this situation because so far, we can still hold off for one or two months.’ This came the same week that RON97 petrol rose 10 sen to RM3.25 per litre, unsubsidised RON95 climbed 8 sen to RM2.67, and diesel in Peninsular Malaysia jumped 8 sen to RM3.12 — all driven by the escalating Middle East conflict and 200 vessels stuck in the Strait of Hormuz.

🔥 Why It Matters

Anwar Boasts RM426.7B 'Record' Approved Investments — But 'Approved' Doesn't Mean 'Arrived'

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister and Finance Minister

Category: Economy

Date: 2026-03-06

📌 WHAT HAPPENED

On March 6, 2026, Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim announced that Malaysia recorded its highest-ever approved investments of RM426.7 billion in 2025 — an 11% increase from RM384.4 billion in 2024. According to MIDA, 8,390 projects were approved, with the potential to create 244,902 jobs. Anwar framed this as a vindication of his reform agenda and evidence of global confidence in Malaysia’s economic direction.

The announcement was made at the launch of the Capital Market Masterplan 2026-2030, with Anwar also serving as Finance Minister — a dual role he has held since taking power in 2022.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS

Because approved investments are not the same as realized investments, and the Malaysian public has been fed this distinction-free headline for decades.

MIDA tracks approvals — essentially, letters of intent and investment certificates. These are commitments on paper. The actual money flowing into the economy — foreign direct investment (FDI) realized — has historically been 50-60% of approved figures in Malaysia. That means up to half of these headline numbers may never translate into actual factories, jobs, or economic activity.

Anwar is using approved investment figures the same way every Malaysian PM before him has: as a political headline, not an economic reality check. The 244,902 jobs are potential jobs — not confirmed, not created, not guaranteed.

This matters because Malaysians are living through cost-of-living pressures, stagnant real wages, and subsidy rationalization. A headline number that may be half-real does not pay anyone’s bills.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT

Let’s be precise about what happened here: the Prime Minister stood at a podium and celebrated a number that he knows does not reflect actual investment.

Every MIDA annual report distinguishes between approved and realized investments. Every serious economist in Malaysia knows the realization gap. Anwar — a man who has been in and around Malaysian economic policymaking for three decades — knows this too.

And yet: no realization rate was disclosed. No breakdown of how much of 2023 or 2024’s approved investments actually materialized. No accountability metric. Just the biggest number, the best headline, and a claim of “global confidence.”

This is the economic equivalent of counting RSVPs as attendance. You don’t get to claim 244,902 jobs when those jobs exist only in approval letters. You don’t get to claim RM426.7 billion when a significant chunk of it may never arrive.

The UNCTAD Global Investment Trends Monitor (January 2026) itself noted that much of global FDI growth was concentrated in financial centres rather than productive assets. MIDA’s own press release acknowledged this — then quietly moved on to the headline number.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES

This is not new. Every Malaysian government has played the approved-vs-realized game:

  • Under Najib, approved investments regularly topped RM200B+ annually, but realized FDI lagged significantly behind.
  • Under Mahathir 2.0, the same pattern continued — big approvals, quiet underperformance.
  • Anwar’s own government has not published a comprehensive, transparent realization audit since taking office. The pattern is always the same: announce the biggest possible number at a press conference, let the media run the headline, and never follow up with what actually happened. No PM has ever been held accountable for the gap between approved and realized investments.

🎯 PH CALLOUT

You promised transparency and accountability as your brand. You said you were different from BN. You said you wouldn’t spin numbers.

So where is the realization report? Where is the breakdown of how much of 2023’s and 2024’s approved investments actually showed up as real money, real factories, and real jobs?

If you want credit for RM426.7 billion, show the receipts — not the invitations. Until then, this is the same economic theatre Malaysians have been watching since the 1990s, just with a reformist coat of paint.
