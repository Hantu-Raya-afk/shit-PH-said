DAP Finally Demands RCI on MACC — Weeks Late, and PAS Correctly Calls It Political Theatre

Said by: DAP Leadership / Ahmad Fadhli Shaari, PAS Information Chief

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-10

📌 What Happened

On March 10, DAP — the largest party in the Pakatan Harapan coalition with 40 parliamentary seats — formally called for a Royal Commission of Inquiry (RCI) to investigate the Malaysian Anti-Corruption Commission over allegations first reported by Bloomberg, including that MACC chief Azam Baki breached civil servant shareholding limits and that the agency was involved in corporate intimidation. PAS information chief Ahmad Fadhli Shaari responded by calling the demand “political theatre,” noting that DAP had remained silent when the same Bloomberg reports alleged that Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim personally interfered in MACC investigations.

🔥 Why It Matters

This is the clearest example yet of DAP’s credibility gap on institutional reform. The party spent weeks after the Bloomberg exposé refusing to directly challenge Anwar’s handling of Azam Baki, even as eight PKR MPs led by Rafizi Ramli publicly demanded Azam’s suspension in mid-February. DAP’s belated RCI call comes only after its catastrophic Sabah election loss and growing voter fury — not from genuine conviction. When even PAS can accurately identify your reform posture as performative, something has gone deeply wrong with your party’s moral compass.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

DAP had the numbers to force the issue from day one. Forty seats. The largest bloc in government. Instead, they sat on their hands for weeks while Bloomberg’s reporting exposed potential corruption at the highest levels of the anti-graft agency — and Anwar’s alleged interference in it. Now they want credit for demanding an RCI? That’s not accountability. That’s damage control dressed up as principle. And the most damning part: DAP still hasn’t addressed the Bloomberg allegation that their own Prime Minister interfered in MACC affairs. They’re demanding an investigation into everyone except the man they answer to.

📜 Previous Cases

This pattern is pure DAP under the unity government. After the Sabah election wipeout in November 2025, DAP pledged to “accelerate the reform agenda over the next six months.” Months later, the PM tenure limit bill failed partly because government MPs were absent, and DAP’s response was to blame the opposition rather than hold their own coalition accountable. On the Azam Baki shareholding scandal — which first surfaced in 2021 when DAP was in opposition and demanded action — DAP is now the party in government protecting the same man they once wanted removed. The pattern is clear: loud in opposition, silent in power.

🎯 PH Callout

DAP, you promised reformasi. You promised institutional accountability. You promised you were different. But when Bloomberg dropped the biggest anti-corruption story in Malaysian politics since 1MDB, your first instinct was to protect the Prime Minister, not the public. An RCI demand that comes weeks late and deliberately avoids the PM’s role isn’t reform — it’s theatre. If you want to prove PAS wrong, start by addressing the Bloomberg allegations about Anwar’s interference in MACC. Until then, Ahmad Fadhli is right: you are not qualified to champion this.

Dzulkefly Promises — Again — to Crack Down on Workplace Bullying in Government Hospitals

Said by: Dzulkefly Ahmad, Health Minister

Category: Health

Date: 2026-03-10

📌 What Happened

Health Minister Dzulkefly Ahmad announced on March 10, 2026 that the Health Ministry will “bolster enforcement” against workplace bullying in government health facilities. He said public hospitals should be “professional and ethical workplace environments” and pointed to a Public Services Department (PSD) circular that bans coarse language and verbal abuse in the workplace. He pledged to “enhance implementation” of the circular to ensure “no ambiguity” in handling misconduct — language that strongly implies the existing rules are not being enforced.

🔥 Why It Matters

This is not a new announcement. Dzulkefly has been making near-identical promises since at least August 2025, when the Health Ministry recorded 430 bullying complaints with only four under investigation. He launched “zero tolerance” pledges, announced new guidelines classifying verbal threats and mockery as bullying (due in October 2025), and even threatened to fire senior officials who conceal cases. Months later, he is back at the podium saying the same things — which tells you everything about whether the previous rounds of promises produced results. Housemen and junior doctors continue to report systemic harassment, and the ministry’s response remains performative announcements rather than structural accountability.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

When a minister announces the same policy three times in under a year, the announcement is the policy — not the enforcement. Dzulkefly’s playbook is textbook PH governance: identify a real problem, hold a press conference, issue a circular, then do nothing until the next round of bad press forces another press conference. The circular he cited already exists. The guidelines were already launched. The zero-tolerance stance was already declared. If any of those measures had worked, he would not be standing up in March 2026 promising to “enhance implementation.” The problem is not ambiguity in the rules — it is impunity for senior medical staff who bully juniors, and a ministry that treats announcements as action.

📜 Previous Cases

In August 2025, the same minister made headlines for declaring zero tolerance on bullying after the case of “Dr S,” a houseman in Sabah allegedly bullied so severely she was confined to her room for years while the perpetrator moved abroad to pursue specialist training. In July 2025, the ministry announced new guidelines classifying insults, threats, and mockery as workplace bullying — guidelines supposedly launching in October 2025. Pressure group Hartal Doktor Kontrak has repeatedly called for systemic reform, not just guidelines, pointing to research showing high prevalence of bullying among Malaysian junior doctors across multiple government hospitals.

🎯 PH Callout

Anwar’s Madani government promised institutional reform and a government that works for the people. Dzulkefly is a senior PH minister in a critical portfolio, and the best he can offer after three years in power is re-announcing the same circular and calling it progress. Junior doctors — the backbone of Malaysia’s public health system — deserve more than recycled press releases. Either enforce the rules you already have, fire the people responsible, and publish the outcomes — or stop pretending the announcements mean anything.

Rafizi Quietly Drops NFCorp Bankruptcy Bid — The Anti-Corruption Crusader Makes Nice With the People He Once Exposed

Said by: Rafizi Ramli, Pandan MP / Former Economy Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-10

📌 What Happened

Rafizi Ramli, the Pandan MP and former Economy Minister who built his political career on exposing the National Feedlot Corporation (NFCorp) scandal, has quietly dropped his bankruptcy and winding-up proceedings against NFCorp director Datuk Seri Dr Mohamad Salleh Ismail and the company itself. A Notice of Discontinuance was filed on March 3, and the High Court struck out the case on March 10 without an order as to costs. Rafizi’s lawyer Razlan Hadri Zulkifli confirmed the matter was “amicably resolved.” The case stemmed from court-ordered costs owed to Rafizi after the Federal Court upheld his appeal in April 2022 — NFCorp and Salleh had paid RM300,000 in December 2022 but stopped paying the outstanding interest balance.

🔥 Why It Matters

Rafizi’s entire political brand was built on the NFCorp exposé. In 2012, he blew the lid off a RM250 million government soft loan meant for a national cattle-rearing project that instead went toward luxury condominiums and personal expenses — a scandal that brought down UMNO Wanita chief Shahrizat Abdul Jalil and became a symbol of BN-era corruption. He was charged under the Official Secrets Act for it. He went to jail for it. The courts vindicated him all the way to the Federal Court. And now, in March 2026, with his own MACC investigation hanging over his head over the RM1.11 billion ARM Holdings chip deal, Rafizi quietly makes nice with the very people he once dragged through the courts. The timing is impossible to ignore.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

This is the man who told Malaysians he would never compromise on accountability. The man who said corruption must be pursued relentlessly, regardless of political cost. And now he’s walking away from money the courts said he was owed — not because he’s being magnanimous, but because the political calculus has changed. Rafizi is under MACC investigation. His former aide has fled to the UK. His political position within PKR is weakened after being dropped from Cabinet. When you’re no longer in a position of strength, you don’t pick fights — you settle. That’s not principle. That’s survival.

📜 Previous Cases

Rafizi has a pattern of selective aggression. He was relentless against BN-era scandals — NFCorp, 1MDB, Tabung Haji — when PH was in opposition. But once PH came to power, the fire dimmed. As Economy Minister, he championed the Central Database Hub (PADU) that drew criticism for data privacy concerns, and oversaw investment policies that favoured headline numbers over structural reform. His recent MACC trouble over the ARM Holdings chip deal, where he allegedly facilitated a RM1.11 billion investment in a company with questionable fundamentals, shows a man who has drifted far from his whistleblower roots. The NFCorp settlement is just the latest chapter in a long arc from crusader to career politician.

🎯 PH Callout

PH, you told Malaysians that Reformasi meant accountability without exception. Rafizi was your poster boy — the brave whistleblower who went to prison for exposing corruption. Now he’s settling with the people he exposed, dropping court-ordered debts, and making amicable deals while his own MACC probe gathers steam. If your best anti-corruption champion can’t even follow through on a case he already won, what exactly does PH stand for anymore? You don’t get to claim the moral high ground when your own people are cutting quiet deals in courtrooms.

Ramanan Claims 'Paid Agents' Are Behind Racial Tensions — Promises Cabinet Crackdown Instead of Addressing Root Causes

Said by: R. Ramanan, Human Resources Minister / Aaron Ago Dagang, National Unity Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-10

📌 What Happened

Human Resources Minister and PKR Vice-President Datuk Seri R. Ramanan held a special press conference in Putrajaya on March 9 flanked by PKR officials, claiming that “agents of provocation” are being paid to incite racial and religious tensions in Malaysia. He said the matter would be raised at Cabinet on March 11, and that arrests have already been made. National Unity Minister Aaron Ago Dagang echoed the call on March 10, urging police to “take stern action” against anyone disrupting harmony. Neither minister named the alleged provocateurs or their financiers — Ramanan specifically said he would not name them to avoid “making them famous.”

🔥 Why It Matters

This is the latest escalation in a pattern where PH ministers frame legitimate racial grievances and social media criticism as the work of paid saboteurs, rather than symptoms of real policy failures. Coming barely a week after Anwar’s “Zionist plot to destabilise the government” claims in Parliament, Ramanan’s press conference — complete with a PKR entourage — signals that the Unity Government is building a coordinated narrative to justify crackdowns on dissent. The vague language about “agents” and “provocateurs” without naming anyone creates a chilling effect: anyone who raises racial issues can now be framed as a paid agent. For a coalition that came to power promising civil liberties reform, this is a deeply authoritarian move.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

Ramanan’s argument boils down to: the economy is doing well, the ringgit is strong, unemployment is low — so anyone complaining about racial issues must be a paid agent. This is textbook deflection. Racial tensions in Malaysia are not manufactured by shadowy provocateurs; they are the product of decades of structural inequality, uneven affirmative action, and a political class that has never seriously addressed inter-ethnic grievances. PH promised institutional reform. Instead, it is adopting the exact same “external enemies” narrative that BN and PN used for decades — blame outsiders, arrest critics, and call it national unity. Ramanan held this press conference flanked by PKR officials, making it a party event masquerading as a ministerial statement. The Human Resources Minister has no portfolio responsibility for racial harmony — that belongs to Aaron Ago Dagang. So why is Ramanan leading the charge? Because this is a PKR political operation, not a governance response.

📜 Previous Cases

This follows Anwar’s March 3 Parliament speech where he blamed a “prominent Zionist group” and Bloomberg for trying to destabilise his government — a claim Daim’s family has since sued him over, calling it an “outright lie.” Before that, PH used the Sedition Act and Communications and Multimedia Act against social media users who criticised the government — the same laws they once promised to repeal. The “paid agents” narrative is a recycled BN playbook: Najib’s government routinely blamed racial tensions on “DAP provocateurs” and “foreign-funded NGOs.” PH has now adopted the same framework, just with different villains. Every Malaysian government, from Mahathir 1.0 to Muhyiddin to Anwar, has discovered that blaming racial tensions on shadowy outsiders is easier than fixing the system.

🎯 PH Callout

You ran on a platform of protecting civil liberties, repealing oppressive laws, and building a “Malaysia Madani” where citizens could speak freely. Now your ministers are holding press conferences warning Malaysians to shut up or face prosecution, while refusing to name the people they accuse. If these “agents” are real, name them, charge them, and let the courts decide — you said it yourself, Ramanan. If you cannot name them, then stop using vague threats to chill public discourse. You are not protecting national unity. You are protecting yourselves from accountability.

Kejutan mendadak ini adalah intinya. Seruan boikot Syahredzan kelihatan sangat tidak masuk akal sekarang: patutkah AP turut memboikot acara kedutaan Iran?

Kerajaan berayun antara menarik pelaburan Barat dan mengecam kemunafikan Barat, antara melibatkan China dalam perdagangan dan menolak isu Laut China Selatan, bergantung kepada penonton domestik. PH, jika anda mahu Malaysia dipandang serius di pentas dunia, pilih satu laluan. Anda menjadikan Parlimen perhimpunan solidariti untuk Iran tujuh hari lalu. Ahli parlimen belakang anda sendiri menyeru boikot acara kedutaan AS. Kini anda menelefon Kuwait untuk mengutuk negara yang sama yang anda pertahankan. Apakah pendirian sebenar Malaysia? Rakyat layak mendapat dasar luar negeri, bukan papan mood.

[BM] Anwar Lancar Pelan Induk Pasaran Modal, Kata 'Reformasi Tidak Boleh Dirunding' — Sementara Setiap Reformasi Sebenar Ditangguh Atau Dibunuh

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister

Category: Economy

Date: 2026-03-09

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

PM Datuk Seri Anwar Ibrahim menyampaikan ucaptama di pelancaran Pelan Induk Pasaran Modal 2026–2030 (CMP4) Suruhanjaya Sekuriti pada 9 Mac 2026. Beliau mengisytiharkan bahawa “kerajaan telah menjadikan reformasi tidak boleh dirunding” dan pasaran modal Malaysia “mesti memacu kemakmuran bersama, bukan sekadar keuntungan.” CMP4 menyasarkan pertumbuhan pasaran modal Malaysia ke RM6.3 trilion menjelang 2030.

🔥 Mengapa Ia Penting

Frasa “reformasi tidak boleh dirunding” akan berkuasa jika ia datang daripada kerajaan yang sebenarnya menyampaikan reformasi. Sebaliknya, ia datang daripada PM yang sama yang kerajaannya, dalam dua minggu lepas sahaja:

  • Gagal meluluskan rang undang-undang had tempoh PM kerana 8 MPnya sendiri tidak hadir (3 Mac)
  • Menangguhkan rang undang-undang pemisahan AG-PP untuk semakan jawatankuasa 3 bulan (4 Mac)
  • Enggan menggantung ketua SPRM Azam Baki walaupun dakwaan semakin memuncak (9 Mac)
  • Menangguhkan reformasi RON95 berulang kali — Anwar sendiri mengakui beliau hanya mampu mengekalkan harga “satu atau dua bulan lagi” (7 Mac)

💩 Mengapa Ia Karut

Anwar menggunakan “reformasi” seperti jenama mewah menggunakan “eksklusif” — ia perkataan pemasaran yang terpisah daripada produk. Pelan Induk Pasaran Modal adalah dokumen Suruhanjaya Sekuriti. Ia peta jalan teknokratik untuk pertumbuhan pasaran. Tetapi Anwar tidak boleh berdiri di podium, mengisytiharkan “reformasi tidak boleh dirunding,” dan menerima pujian untuk reformasi apabila:

  • Peguam Negara masih merangkap Pendakwa Raya — konflik kepentingan yang PH berjanji perbaiki sejak 2018.
  • Ketua SPRM kekal di pejabat walaupun dakwaan.
  • Reformasi institusi yang memerlukan keberanian politik ditangguhkan secara sistematik. Pertumbuhan pasaran modal bukan reformasi. Menaikkan penilaian PLC bukan reformasi. Reformasi adalah apabila anda mengubah peraturan yang melindungi golongan berkuasa. Anwar tidak melakukan itu.

📜 Kes Terdahulu

  • 2018–2020 (PH 1.0): Kerajaan PH pertama juga berjanji pemisahan AG-PP, IPCMC, dan reformasi institusi. Tiada yang selesai sebelum Langkah Sheraton.
  • 4 Mac 2026: Fahmi umumkan rang undang-undang pemisahan AG-PP ditangguhkan untuk semakan jawatankuasa parlimen 3 bulan.
  • 3 Mac 2026: Pindaan perlembagaan had tempoh PM gagal kerana PH tidak mampu menghimpunkan kehadiran penuh.

🎯 Teguran PH

Anda kata reformasi “tidak boleh dirunding.” Mengapa setiap reformasi akhirnya dirunding dan ditangguhkan? Pemisahan AG-PP — ditangguhkan. Had tempoh PM — gagal atas kehadiran anda sendiri. Azam Baki — masih di pejabat. RON95 — masih bersubsidi tanpa jadual. Anda ada majoriti parlimen. Anda ada mandat institusi. Yang anda tidak ada ialah kehendak politik untuk membelanjakan modal gabungan anda pada sesuatu yang tidak menguntungkan gabungan itu sendiri. Berhenti memberitahu penonton Suruhanjaya Sekuriti bahawa reformasi tidak boleh dirunding. Mulakan memberitahu Kabinet anda sendiri.

[BM] Anwar Percepatkan Bantuan Tunai STR RM15 Bilion — Perbelanjaan Rekod, Masa Hari Raya, Bau Pilihan Raya

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister and Finance Minister

Category: Economy

Date: 2026-03-09

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Pada 9 Mac 2026, PM Anwar Ibrahim mengumumkan Fasa 2 program pemindahan tunai Sumbangan Tunai Rahmah (STR) akan dipercepatkan ke 10 Mac — berminggu-minggu lebih awal daripada jadual asal. Pembayaran awal ini memberi manfaat kepada 5.2 juta penerima. Anwar membingkaikan langkah itu sebagai membantu rakyat Malaysia bersedia untuk Hari Raya Aidilfitri. Jumlah peruntukan 2026 untuk STR dan SARA berjumlah RM15 bilion — tertinggi dalam sejarah program bantuan tunai langsung Kerajaan Persekutuan.

🔥 Mengapa Ia Penting

Tiga perkara menjadikan ini penting:

  1. Perbelanjaan rekod bagi pemindahan tunai. RM15 bilion bukan jumlah kecil — dan ia datang daripada PM yang berkempen atas disiplin fiskal dan menamatkan budaya bantuan.
  2. Masa yang mencurigakan. Reuters melaporkan kerajaan mungkin memanggil PRU kilat seawal Julai 2026. Mempercepatkan bayaran tunai tepat sebelum Hari Raya adalah politik tunai pra-pilihan raya klasik.
  3. Reformasi struktur tiada di mana-mana. Pemindahan tunai bukan dasar ekonomi. Ia penawar kesakitan. Rasionalisasi subsidi RON95 telah ditangguhkan berulang kali.

💩 Mengapa Ia Karut

Anwar menghabiskan bertahun-tahun — berdekad — menyerang program bantuan tunai BR1M BN sebagai pembelian undi, rasuah populis, dan ketidakbertanggungjawaban fiskal. Beliau memanggilnya “tongkat” yang membuatkan rakyat bergantung kepada kemurahan kerajaan. Kini beliau menjalankan buku panduan yang sama, kecuali beliau membelanjakan lebih banyak wang dan mempercepatkan bayaran untuk mendarat tepat sebelum Hari Raya. STR adalah BR1M dengan penjenamaan semula.

“Peruntukan tertinggi dalam sejarah” bukan satu kebanggaan — ia pengakuan. Jika ekonomi berprestasi seperti dakwaan kerajaan, mengapa kerajaan perlu membelanjakan RM15 bilion rekod untuk bantuan tunai? Sama ada pertumbuhan tidak sampai kepada rakyat biasa, atau ini tentang pilihan raya, bukan ekonomi.

📜 Kes Terdahulu

  • BR1M di bawah Najib (2012-2018): Anwar dan PH tanpa henti menyerang BR1M sebagai pembelian undi yang tidak mampan. Kini mereka menjalankan versi lebih besar program yang sama.
  • Bantuan Raya 2024: Anwar mempercepatkan bayaran STR sebelum Hari Raya 2024 juga. Ini kini corak tahunan.
  • Kelewatan subsidi RON95 (2024-2026): Kerajaan berulang kali menangguhkan reformasi subsidi minyak bersasar.

🎯 Teguran PH

Anda berjanji “reformasi, bukan derma.” Anda memberitahu rakyat Malaysia PH akan membina ekonomi di mana orang tidak perlu bantuan. Anda mengejek Najib kerana BR1M. Kini anda membelanjakan RM15 bilion untuk plaster yang sama — dan anda bangga dengannya. Ini bukan keprihatinan. Ini perbelanjaan kempen yang difailkan di bawah baris bajet yang berbeza.

[BM] Anwar Taknak Gantung Azam Baki — Biar Saja Kontrak Tamat Mei Sementara Negara Terbakar

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-09

Ini bukan kemenangan. Ini kawalan kerosakan berpakaian keputusan. Anwar membiarkan Azam Baki berkhidmat dua bulan lagi sementara SPRM kekal di bawah awan dakwaan bahawa ia beroperasi sebagai sindiket peras ugut. [BM] Anwar Ulang 'Bantuan Berdasar Keperluan, Bukan Kaum' Di Dewan Negara — Skrip Sama, Sesi Lain, Sifar Perubahan Struktur

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-09

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Perdana Menteri Datuk Seri Anwar Ibrahim memberitahu Dewan Negara pada 9 Mac 2026 bahawa bantuan kerajaan diperuntukkan berdasarkan keperluan, bukan kaum. Beliau menegaskan kerajaan Madani akan “kekal adil kepada semua negeri” dan bantuan menyasarkan mereka yang paling memerlukan tanpa mengira latar belakang. Ini bukan kali pertama Anwar menggunakan ayat yang sama. Pada Ogos 2025, beliau memberitahu Dewan Negara yang sama perkara yang sama — hampir perkataan demi perkataan.

🔥 Mengapa Ia Penting

Ini adalah salah satu slogan PH yang paling kerap diulang. Ia menjadi teras tawaran Ekonomi Madani, dan ia bergema dengan pengundi yang bosan dengan sistem pengagihan berasaskan kaum. Tetapi slogan bukan dasar. Soalannya mudah: adakah kerajaan sebenarnya menyusun semula mana-mana program peruntukan khusus Bumiputera utama sejak berkuasa? Adakah mana-mana kuota berasaskan kaum diperbaharui? Jawapannya, berdasarkan semua bukti yang ada, adalah tidak. Seni bina pengagihan berasaskan kaum tidak disentuh.

💩 Mengapa Ia Karut

Berkata “bantuan berdasarkan keperluan, bukan kaum” sambil mengekalkan setiap struktur peruntukan berasaskan kaum bukan reformasi. Ia adalah penjenamaan. Anwar tidak berbohong — secara teknikal, beberapa program bantuan seperti SARA menyasarkan isi rumah berpendapatan rendah tanpa mengira kaum. Tetapi item besar — sasaran ekuiti Bumiputera, peruntukan MARA, perolehan khas Bumiputera, pengambilan pekerja keutamaan GLC — kekal tidak disentuh. Apa yang Anwar lakukan adalah pengelakan klasik: tunjuk program kebajikan kecil yang berasaskan keperluan, ulang slogan, dan harap tiada siapa bertanya tentang seni bina struktur.

📜 Kes Terdahulu

  • Ogos 2025: Anwar memberitahu Dewan Negara “peruntukan berdasarkan keperluan, bukan kaum.” Dewan sama, skrip sama.
  • Julai 2025: RMK-13 dilancarkan dengan pembingkaian peralihan daripada dasar berasaskan kaum kepada berasaskan keperluan.
  • Timbalan Menteri Ekonomi Hanifah (Disember 2025): Memberitahu Parlimen kerajaan “mengkaji” penggunaan pendapatan boleh guna sebagai penanda aras lebih adil — bermakna alat pengukuran asas pun belum diperbaharui.

🎯 Teguran PH

Anda bertanding atas “Reformasi.” Anda berjanji Malaysia di mana kaum tidak menentukan akses kepada peluang. Tiga tahun kemudian, setiap program struktur berasaskan kaum kekal utuh. Tidak satu pun diperbaharui. Dan anda berdiri di Dewan Negara — buat kali kedua dalam setahun — mengatakan ayat yang sama. Jika itu benar, tunjukkan kepada kami kertas dasar. Tunjukkan program yang disusun semula. Sehingga itu, ini bukan dasar. Ini kenyataan akhbar.

[BM] Anwar Umum Bantuan Tunai RM500 Untuk Penjawat Awam Dan Kakitangan Masjid — Musim Pilihan Raya Hari Raya Bermula

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister and Finance Minister

Category: Economy

Date: 2026-03-09

📌 APA YANG BERLAKU: Pada 9 Mac 2026, Anwar Ibrahim mengumumkan Bantuan Kewangan Khas (BKK) 2026: RM500 untuk penjawat awam Gred 15 ke bawah, RM250 untuk pesara dan veteran, dan RM500 untuk kira-kira 70,000 kakitangan masjid dan agama — imam, bilal, siak, marbut, guru KAFA dan takmir. Bayaran masuk pada 13 Mac, beberapa hari sebelum Hari Raya Aidilfitri, apabila perbelanjaan isi rumah memuncak dan muhibah politik paling ketara. Anwar memegang kedua-dua portfolio PM dan Menteri Kewangan, memberikannya kuasa sepihak ke atas masa dan sasaran pengeluaran sedemikian.

🔥 KENAPA IA PENTING: Ini perbelanjaan kitaran pilihan raya yang jelas: disasarkan, populis, dan diwaktukan untuk impak emosi maksimum semasa cuti agama. Menggabungkan penjawat awam dan kakitangan masjid dalam pengumuman yang sama mengukuhkan dua bank undi kritikal serentak — birokrasi dan penubuhan agama. RM500 untuk kakitangan masjid adalah percubaan telus untuk memotong monopoli PAS terhadap undi agama, dibiayai bukan oleh reformasi institusi tetapi oleh pindahan tunai terus daripada wang awam.

💩 KENAPA IA KARUT: Bantuan tunai sebelum musim perayaan adalah buku panduan BN yang PH kritik selama berdekad. BR1M Najib dipersendakan sebagai “beli undi” oleh PH ketika pembangkang. Kemasukan kakitangan masjid amat sinis: peranan ini secara tradisinya dikaitkan dengan kawasan luar bandar dan separa bandar yang sejajar PAS, menjadikan ini bidaan terus untuk bersaing dengan PAS atas dasar agama menggunakan wang awam. Jika anda mahu menambah baik kehidupan pekerja masjid, anda akan memperbaharui syarat pekerjaan dan skala gaji mereka secara kekal — bukan melemparkan bonus pra-Raya apabila anda perlukan undi mereka.

📜 KES TERDAHULU: BR1M Najib (kemudian BSH) adalah program bantuan tunai perayaan asal, dikritik meluas oleh PH sebagai pembelian undi populis. BPN Muhyiddin semasa COVID turut diwaktukan untuk impak politik. Setiap PM Malaysia menemui kuasa pilihan raya pindahan tunai sebelum cuti besar. Anwar berjanji akan berbeza. Dia tidak.

🎯 PANGGILAN PH: Anda mempersendakan BR1M bertahun-tahun. Memanggilnya rasuah. Memanggilnya tidak mapan. Kini anda melakukan perkara yang sama, diwaktukan pada kalendar yang sama, menyasarkan bank undi yang sama — dengan bonus tambahan membeli undi masjid dengan wang pembayar cukai. Jika inilah rupa ‘Malaysia Madani’, ia sekadar 1Malaysia dengan penjenamaan lebih baik.
