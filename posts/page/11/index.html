Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister

Category: Economy

The rakyat continues to pay the debt while the man responsible serves a comfortable sentence.

Highway Concessions: Rakyat Pays Tolls to Make Cronies Rich — Toll Abolition Promise Broken AGAIN

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim Administration / Transport Ministry

Category: Economy

Date: 2025-12-01

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: The PH government’s core manifesto promise to abolish or significantly reduce highway tolls has been systematically broken.
