It's March 2026 — Where Is Anwar's Promise to Eradicate Hardcore Poverty by Q1?

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim, Prime Minister and Finance Minister

Category: Economy

Date: 2026-03-09

📌 WHAT HAPPENED: On 7 December 2025, Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim publicly declared that Malaysia would eradicate hardcore poverty by the first quarter of 2026. Speaking at the closing of the Rancakkan Madani programme in Putrajaya, Anwar cited a hardcore poverty rate of 0.09% — roughly 7,000 households — and said the remaining cases could be resolved within months. “We are one of the countries in the world… we have managed to reduce the national rate of hardcore poverty to just 0.09 per cent,” Anwar said. “We can end it by the first quarter of 2026.” The promise was reported by Bernama, Malay Mail, The Star, New Straits Times, The Edge, and The Sun. It was a flagship claim — repeated during Budget 2026 and the Rancakkan Madani roadshow. It is now 9 March 2026. Q1 ends in three weeks. There has been no official announcement that hardcore poverty has been eradicated. No press conference. No Bernama flash. No triumphant PMO statement.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS: This was not a casual remark. Anwar made the eradication of hardcore poverty a centrepiece of the Madani economic narrative. It was embedded into Budget 2026 messaging, cited in international forums, and used to distinguish Malaysia from Western economies plagued by homelessness. The World Bank even weighed in, commending Malaysia’s progress while noting that “further improvements in education and health outcomes are crucial.” The December 2025 promise was designed to be a legacy moment — the PM who ended extreme poverty. If the target has been met, the silence is bizarre. If it hasn’t been met, the silence is damning. Either way, the rakyat deserves an update. Poverty eradication claims also carry methodological risk. Economists have previously questioned whether Malaysia’s hardcore poverty line (RM1,169/month per household) is set too low, making “eradication” a statistical exercise rather than a lived reality. A government that declares victory without addressing these concerns is doing PR, not policy.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT: This is a textbook case of promise inflation followed by quiet deflation. Anwar made the pledge when the numbers looked good and the cameras were rolling. He set a specific, time-bound target — Q1 2026 — knowing full well it would either be met or missed in public view. Now that the deadline is here, the government has gone quiet. No update. No revised timeline. No acknowledgement that the target even exists. This is the same administration that loudly claims credit for every ringgit of GDP growth and every decimal point drop in inflation. But when it comes to a concrete promise with a concrete deadline? Silence. If hardcore poverty has been eradicated, say so. Publish the data. Show the methodology. Let independent economists verify it. If it hasn’t been eradicated, say that too. Explain what went wrong. Revise the timeline. Be honest. What you don’t get to do is make a grand promise, ride the PR wave, and then pretend the deadline doesn’t exist.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES: This is not PH’s first vanishing deadline:

  • RON95 fuel subsidy reform has been delayed repeatedly. Anwar himself admitted in early March 2026 that the government could only hold the price for “one or two more months” — yet no concrete reform plan has been announced.
  • The AG-Public Prosecutor separation bill was postponed in March 2026 for a 3-month committee review, after being a signature reform promise.
  • The PM term limit constitutional amendment failed in Parliament in March 2026 — despite PH’s commanding majority — because 8 government MPs didn’t even show up to vote. The pattern is clear: big promises, loud announcements, quiet retreats. The Madani government treats reform pledges as PR events, not binding commitments. Malaysia’s own history is littered with poverty eradication claims that didn’t survive contact with reality. The Najib administration claimed similar progress; independent audits later showed significant undercounting.

🎯 PH CALLOUT: You told 33 million Malaysians that hardcore poverty would be gone by Q1 2026. You said it on camera, in Putrajaya, at your own showcase event. Bernama carried it. Every major outlet reported it. It’s March 9. Q1 is almost over. Where’s the announcement? Where’s the data? Where’s the press conference where you pop the champagne and show us the zero? Or did you hope we’d forget? Here’s the thing about specific promises with specific deadlines: people remember. You don’t get to coast on vibes when you gave us a date. Either deliver the results or deliver an explanation. The rakyat who are still living on less than RM1,169 a month deserve more than silence.

PKR's Own MP Warns Anwar Risks Legitimacy by Ignoring Nurul Izzah and Backbenchers on MACC

Said by: Hassan Abdul Karim, PKR MP for Pasir Gudang

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-09

📌 What Happened

On 9 March 2026, PKR’s Pasir Gudang MP Hassan Abdul Karim publicly warned Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim — his own party president — that ignoring concerns raised by MPs and PKR deputy president Nurul Izzah Anwar about the Malaysian Anti-Corruption Commission (MACC) puts the PM’s legitimacy at risk. Speaking to Malaysiakini, Hassan said Anwar cannot keep turning a deaf ear to internal party concerns about the MACC’s conduct, particularly after Nurul Izzah called for the agency to be placed under parliamentary oversight and criticised the “high-handed manner” of investigations including the RM1.1 billion Arm Holdings deal probe.

🔥 Why It Matters

This is not an opposition attack — it is a PKR backbencher going on record to say the party president is damaging his own credibility. When your own coalition MPs are publicly questioning your legitimacy over an anti-corruption agency that you once promised to reform, the institutional rot is no longer a perception problem — it is an internal party crisis. Nurul Izzah’s March 7 call for MACC reform via Bernama, FMT, and NST was already a rare public break from the PM’s daughter and deputy president. Hassan’s statement escalates it further: from polite disagreement to a warning about the PM’s fitness to lead.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

Anwar spent decades as the face of reformasi. He promised clean governance, institutional independence, and accountability. Three days before Hassan’s warning, Anwar himself dismissed Bersih’s criticism of the MACC as “unfair” and told Malaysians to wait for reports — the same delay tactic every Malaysian PM has used to kill accountability momentum. Now his own party members are telling the press that his refusal to listen is a legitimacy problem. The man who once led street protests against abuse of power is now the man whose backbenchers have to beg him, through the media, to pay attention to abuse of power.

📜 Previous Cases

This is not the first time PH’s internal cracks have gone public. In December 2025, DAP threatened to “reconsider” its position in government if Anwar failed to deliver reforms within six months — a move prompted by DAP’s wipeout in the Sabah state election. Nurul Izzah herself had called for an independent MACC oversight committee as early as February 2026. The MACC crisis — centred on chief commissioner Azam Baki’s unresolved shareholding scandal — has been a slow-burning credibility fire since 2022. Every time a coalition MP speaks up, Anwar’s response is the same: wait, trust the AG, don’t be emotional. The pattern is unmistakable: delay as strategy, loyalty as silence.

🎯 PH Callout

You promised reformasi. You promised that no one is above scrutiny. You promised institutional independence. Now your own daughter is calling for MACC oversight, your own backbenchers are warning you in the press, and your response is to tell everyone to calm down and wait for a committee. Here is the reality, Prime Minister: when your party members have to use Malaysiakini to get your attention on corruption, you have already lost the moral authority you once claimed. Either act on MACC reform or admit that “reform” was always just a campaign slogan with an expiry date.

Sports Minister Says 'Learn Lessons and Move On' From Heritage Player Scandal — Where's the Government Accountability?

Said by: Dr. Mohammed Taufiq Johari, Youth and Sports Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-09

📌 WHAT HAPPENED

On March 9, 2026, Youth and Sports Minister Dr. Mohammed Taufiq Johari called on the Football Association of Malaysia (FAM) to “take lessons” from the heritage player scandal and move forward with grassroots football development.

“The legal process has taken place and we accept the decision that has been made. We will now examine what follow-up steps need to be taken,” Taufiq said at an Iftar Ramadan event in Tikam Batu.

This follows the Court of Arbitration for Sport (CAS) upholding FIFA’s sanctions against seven players — Facundo Garcés, Rodrigo Holgado, Imanol Machuca, João Figueiredo, Gabriel Felipe, Jon Irazabal, and Héctor Hevel — who were found to have used forged documents to represent Malaysia internationally. All seven received 12-month suspensions. FIFA also overturned the results of three international matches and fined FAM CHF350,000 (RM1.8 million).

Critically: none of the seven players had any Malaysian roots. The “heritage player” label was itself a fiction.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS

This is not just a football scandal. It is a governance scandal that implicates the Malaysian government at the highest levels.

These seven players were granted Malaysian citizenship through official government channels. The Home Ministry, under Home Minister Datuk Seri Saifuddin Nasution Ismail, previously stated that all citizenship applications were “processed in strict accordance with Malaysian law” under Article 19 of the Federal Constitution and National Registration Department SOPs.

But if the documents were forged — as FIFA found, as CAS confirmed, and as the players were sanctioned for — then either:

  1. The government’s vetting process failed catastrophically, approving citizenship based on documents that could not withstand FIFA’s scrutiny, or
  2. Someone inside the system knew the documents were fraudulent and approved them anyway. Either way, the government has a serious accountability problem. And the Sports Minister’s response? Learn lessons and focus on grassroots.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT

“Learn lessons and move on” is the Malaysian government’s universal get-out-of-jail-free card. It is what they say when they want the public to stop asking uncomfortable questions.

Let’s be very clear about what the Sports Minister is not saying:

  • He is not naming who in government approved the fraudulent citizenship applications.
  • He is not calling for an investigation into the Home Ministry’s vetting process.
  • He is not explaining how forged documents passed through NRD’s “standard operating procedures.”
  • He is not demanding accountability from Saifuddin Nasution, whose ministry signed off on these citizenships. Instead, he is telling FAM — an independent sports body — to “learn lessons.” FAM did not grant these men citizenship. The government did.

The Sports Minister is deflecting blame onto football administrators while the actual institutional failure — a government ministry approving citizenship based on forged documents — goes completely unexamined.

And let’s not forget: the Home Ministry’s earlier claim that everything was done “lawfully” is now directly contradicted by an international sports court’s finding of document fraud. Someone is lying. Nobody in government seems interested in finding out who.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES

This pattern — government complicity followed by blame-shifting to non-governmental actors — is a PH speciality:

  • MACC/Azam Baki scandal (2025-2026): Bloomberg exposed shareholding irregularities. Anwar’s response? A “special committee” — not a suspension, not an independent inquiry. When DAP pushed for more, they were told to wait.
  • 1MDB under PH: Anwar halved Najib’s sentence while telling the public the debt was being managed. The rakyat still pays RM50 billion.
  • Lynas rare earth (2026): The government renewed Lynas’s license for 10 years despite unresolved radioactive waste issues. When pressed, ministers said the “regulatory process” was followed. In each case, the formula is identical: bad thing happens on government’s watch → government says process was followed → when process is shown to have failed → government says learn lessons and move forward. Rinse. Repeat.

🎯 PH CALLOUT

You came to power promising institutional reform and accountability. You said the days of rubber-stamp government approvals were over. You said transparency would be your hallmark.

Seven men with zero Malaysian roots were given Malaysian citizenship under your watch. They played for the national team using forged documents that your Home Ministry certified as lawful. When FIFA caught the fraud, Malaysia was sanctioned, fined, and humiliated on the international stage.

And your response is to tell football administrators to “focus on grassroots”?

Who in the Home Ministry approved these applications? What happened during the vetting process? Will there be an internal investigation? Will Saifuddin Nasution answer for this?

Until you answer those questions, “learn lessons and move on” is just another way of saying: we got caught and we’d rather you forget about it.

[BM] Anwar Dakwa Lobi PKR Di AS Pada 2016 'Hanya Soal Pembebasannya' — Dosier 84 Muka Surat Tunjuk Ia Desak Washington Campuri Urusan Malaysia

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Pada 8 Mac, MalaysiaNow mendedahkan kandungan dosier 84 muka surat bertajuk White Paper on the case of Anwar Ibrahim: Citizen of Malaysia v. Government of Malaysia, yang disediakan oleh pemimpin kanan PKR Sivarasa Rasiah bersama konsultan Amerika Jared Genser dari Perseus Strategies pada April 2016. Dokumen ini, yang disahkan seorang Ahli Parlimen kanan PKR telah dihantar kepada beberapa ahli Kongres AS, mendesak pentadbiran Obama untuk lebih campur tangan dalam politik Malaysia, menegur kerajaan Najib secara terbuka, dan menuntut pembebasan Anwar. Anwar sebelum ini mendakwa pengambilan Genser oleh PKR adalah “semata-mata untuk mendesak pembebasannya dari penjara.”

🔥 Kenapa Ini Penting

Pendedahan ini bersifat letupan dari segi politik kerana kerajaan Anwar kini sedang mendakwa keluarga mendiang Daim Zainuddin — khususnya balu beliau Na’imah Khalid — di bawah undang-undang berkaitan “menjatuhkan kerajaan” kerana melibatkan firma PR asing. Polis melancarkan siasatan di bawah Seksyen 124B terhadap Na’imah berdasarkan dakwaan bahawa kerja komunikasi beliau adalah sebahagian daripada komplot untuk menumbangkan Anwar. Namun PKR melakukan perkara yang sama tepat pada 2016: mengupah konsultan asing untuk melobi kerajaan asing agar menekan Perdana Menteri Malaysia yang sedang memerintah. Dosier itu jauh melampaui kes peribadi Anwar — ia menyerang tadbir urus, badan kehakiman, ekonomi, dan hala tuju dasar Malaysia merentasi 84 muka surat, dan dihantar terus kepada ahli-ahli Kongres AS.

Dakwaan Anwar bahawa lobi 2016 itu “hanya tentang pembebasannya” runtuh di bawah berat dokumen itu sendiri. Dosier itu menyerang tadbir urus Najib dari setiap sudut — ekonomi, badan kehakiman, kebebasan akhbar, dan hala tuju dasar. Ia mendesak Presiden AS untuk menegur secara terbuka sebuah kerajaan berdaulat. Nurul Izzah menulis artikel bertajuk What Obama must do in Malaysia, menuduh Najib meninggalkan negara dengan “peningkatan perbalahan perkauman, ketidakseimbangan ekonomi dan suasana subur untuk pertumbuhan ekstremisme.” Genser menuduh Najib melakukan “gerrymandering melampau” dan mengekalkan kuasa melalui dasar-dasar yang memecahbelahkan. Ini bukan kempen tahanan hati nurani — ia adalah operasi politik menyeluruh yang direka untuk mendapatkan Washington bagi menjejaskan kestabilan kerajaan yang sedang memerintah. Perbuatan yang sama yang kini Anwar panggil pengkhianatan apabila dilakukan lawannya.

📜 Kes-Kes Sebelum Ini

Parti politik Malaysia mempunyai sejarah panjang meminta sokongan asing apabila menjadi pembangkang, kemudian menangis kedaulatan apabila berkuasa. Anwar sendiri memupuk hubungan rapat dengan badan pemikir Washington, media Barat, dan pertubuhan hak asasi antarabangsa semasa menjadi ketua pembangkang selama bertahun-tahun. Kemenangan PH pada PRU 2018 sebahagiannya dibantu oleh tekanan antarabangsa terhadap Najib berhubung 1MDB. Kini berkuasa, Anwar telah mempersenjatakan taktik yang sama terhadap keluarga Daim, mengerahkan siasatan polis di bawah undang-undang era hasutan dan secara terbuka mendakwa “Zionis” dan “jurubank” berada di sebalik rancangan PR Na’imah. Polanya setua politik Malaysia: lobi pembangkang adalah “advokasi untuk demokrasi,” tetapi saat anda pegang kuasa, ia menjadi “komplot untuk menjatuhkan kerajaan.”

🎯 Panggilan Kepada PH

Anda berkempen atas nama ketelusan, kedaulatan undang-undang, dan hak untuk bersuara. Parti anda sendiri mengupah konsultan asing untuk meminta kuasa besar campur tangan dalam politik Malaysia — lengkap dengan dosier 84 muka surat yang menyerang tadbir urus, badan kehakiman, dan hala tuju dasar negara anda sendiri, dihantar terus kepada ahli Kongres AS. Anda kini mendakwa seorang balu tua kerana mengupah firma PR, mendakwa “Zionis” berada di sebalik komplot untuk menumbangkan anda, dan menggunakan Seksyen 124B terhadap aktivis antirasuah dan masyarakat sivil. Jika melibatkan konsultan asing untuk menekan kerajaan Malaysia adalah jenayah terhadap demokrasi berparlimen, mulakan dengan parti anda sendiri. Jika tidak, gugurkan pertuduhan terhadap Na’imah Khalid dan berhenti menggunakan polis untuk menakut-nakutkan pengkritik anda.

Pada 6 Mac, Perdana Menteri Anwar Ibrahim bercakap dengan pemberita selepas solat Jumaat di IOI City Mall Putrajaya dan menasihati rakyat Malaysia supaya “berhemat, belajar berjimat cermat dan menyimpan” sebagai “langkah berjaga-jaga” menghadapi kesan ekonomi konflik AS-Iran. Beliau merujuk kepada harga minyak yang melambung tinggi, lebih 200 kapal tangki disekat di Selat Hormuz, dan kos makanan serta baja dari Ukraine, Rusia dan Eropah yang melonjak naik. Menjelang 8 Mac, nasihat ini menjadi tajuk utama nasional: “Berbelanja bijak, kata PM.”

🔥 Kenapa Ini Penting

Perdana Menteri minta rakyat biasa ikat perut pada masa kerajaan sendiri longgarkan pili fiskal. Dalam kitaran berita yang sama, kerajaan komit RM15 bilion bantuan Sumbangan Tunai Rahmah (STR), umumkan bantuan khas RM500 kepada 1.7 juta penjawat awam dan kakitangan masjid (dibayar 13 Mac), dan telah ikrarkan RM200 juta secara keseluruhan untuk Palestin. Mesej kepada rakyat: kami boleh berbelanja sesuka hati, tapi kamu kena berjimat. Inilah percanggahan asas dalam mesej ekonomi kerajaan Madani — populisme fiskal untuk tajuk berita, khutbah berjimat cermat untuk rakyat.

💩 Kenapa Ini Karut

Seorang Perdana Menteri yang baru sahaja meluluskan berbilion ringgit bantuan tunai musim pilihan raya tidak layak berceramah kepada rakyat tentang berjimat cermat. Bonus RM500 untuk penjawat awam sahaja menelan ratusan juta. Program STR RM15B adalah pemindahan tunai terbesar dalam sejarah Malaysia. Peruntukan RM200J untuk Palestin — walau apa pun meritnya — adalah perbelanjaan budi bicara yang diumumkan di perhimpunan. Kerajaan Anwar tidak mengamalkan apa yang dikhutbahkan. Kalau krisis ini cukup serius untuk minta rakyat berjimat menjelang Hari Raya, ia juga cukup serius untuk potong perbelanjaan naungan politik, bekukan bonus GLC, dan tangguhkan projek tidak perlu. Sebaliknya, kerajaan berbelanja dan suruh rakyat berjimat.

📜 Kes-Kes Sebelum Ini

Corak ini bukan baru untuk Anwar. Pada 2023, beliau suruh rakyat “ubah gaya hidup” untuk menangani rasionalisasi subsidi — sambil mengekalkan Kabinet bengkak lebih 60 menteri dan timbalan menteri. Pada 2024, kerajaan teruskan potongan subsidi diesel bersasar sambil meluluskan projek mega baru seperti SEZ Johor-Singapura. Buku panduan sentiasa sama: kerajaan komit kepada projek politik mahal, kemudian suruh rakyat biasa sesuaikan diri. Mahathir buat begitu. Najib buat begitu. Anwar buat dengan PR yang lebih cantik.

🎯 Panggilan Kepada PH

Kamu janji “Belanjawan Rakyat.” Kamu janji disiplin fiskal. Kamu janji kerajaan Madani akan berbeza — tiada lagi naungan, tiada lagi perbelanjaan membazir, tiada lagi suruh rakyat sengsara sementara elit berpesta. Jadi inilah ujiannya: kalau krisis Timur Tengah cukup serius untuk suruh rakyat tak pergi shopping Raya, ia juga cukup serius untuk batalkan bonus RM500, bekukan perjalanan luar negara menteri, hentikan semua projek mega GLC baru, dan terbitkan akaun terperinci ke mana setiap ringgit perbelanjaan krisis pergi. Apa-apa kurang dari itu hanyalah seorang ahli politik yang suruh rakyat makan kurang supaya kerajaan boleh makan lebih.

[BM] PKR Keluarkan Surat Tunjuk Sebab Kepada Rafizi, Kemudian Berundur — Parti Tak Mampu Disiplinkan Pengkritik Paling Terkenalnya

Said by: Rafizi Ramli, Pandan MP / Fuziah Salleh, PKR Secretary-General

Category: Politics

Date: 2026-03-08

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Pada 19 Februari, AP Pandan Rafizi Ramli mendedahkan beliau menerima surat tunjuk sebab daripada Setiausaha Agung PKR Fuziah Salleh selepas memberitahu Sin Chew Daily beliau akan bertanding di Pandan pada PRU16 tetapi berkemungkinan bukan sebagai calon PKR. Rafizi menjawab bahawa maksud komennya “boleh difahami oleh sesiapa yang mahir bahasa Melayu” dan beliau bebas membuat rancangan sendiri selepas Parlimen dibubarkan. Pada 6 Mac, PKR menyampaikan keputusannya melalui emel — tiada tindakan diambil, hanya peringatan mematuhi perlembagaan parti dan kod etika. Pada 8 Mac, Rafizi secara terbuka menggambarkan ini sebagai “pusingan U,” berkata surat tunjuk sebab dikeluarkan “dengan niat memberikan beliau tendangan” tetapi parti akhirnya gentar.

🔥 Kenapa Ia Penting

Episod ini mendedahkan kereputan institusi dalam PKR — parti yang pernah melaungkan dirinya sebagai barisan hadapan reformasi. Surat tunjuk sebab sepatutnya menjadi permulaan proses disiplin, bukan ancaman persembahan diikuti undur senyap. Ketidakmampuan PKR sama ada melibatkan kritikannya secara bermakna atau menguatkuasakan mekanisme disiplinnya sendiri menunjukkan parti yang lumpuh — terlalu takut memecat pengkritik paling terkenalnya, tetapi terlalu sombong untuk mengakui dia ada betulnya.

💩 Kenapa Ia Karut

PKR mahu Rafizi pergi tetapi tiada keberanian melakukannya. Seperti yang Rafizi sendiri tunjukkan, memecatnya tidak akan menjejaskan kerusi parlimennya — hanya peletakan jawatan sukarela akan mencetuskan kekosongan. Jadi PKR terperangkap: pecat dia dan dia kekal di Parlimen sebagai agen bebas yang memalukan mereka setiap hari, atau berundur dan kelihatan lemah. Mereka pilih lemah. “Peringatan ikut peraturan parti” adalah setara politik nota Post-it pasif-agresif. Ini parti yang sama yang memberi kuliah negara tentang reformasi institusi dan tadbir urus baik sementara ia tidak mampu menguruskan proses disiplin dalaman asas tanpa tunduk di bawah tekanan.

📜 Kes Terdahulu

PKR mempunyai sejarah panjang dan tidak membanggakan pembersihan puak yang berselindung di sebalik disiplin — dan disiplin yang runtuh menjadi komedi. Parti memecat timbalan presidennya sendiri Azmin Ali pada 2020 selepas Langkah Sheraton, tetapi hanya selepas kerosakan berlaku. Rafizi sendiri pernah menjadi anak emas PKR — pemberi maklumat yang mendedahkan skandal Perbadanan Fidlot Nasional. Kini beliau suara parti yang paling menyusahkan.

🎯 Panggilan PH

Anwar dan PKR menjanjikan rakyat Malaysia politik baru — telus, berprinsip, dan bertanggungjawab. Tetapi apabila bekas timbalan presiden anda sendiri secara terbuka mengatakan beliau tidak akan bertanding di bawah bendera anda, dan yang terbaik parti anda mampu hasilkan ialah surat tunjuk sebab diikuti emel peringatan yang lemah, anda bukan menjalankan parti politik — anda menguruskan situasi tebusan. ‘Reformasi’ pernah bermakna sesuatu. Kini ia bermakna menghantar emel bernada tegas dan berharap masalah hilang sendiri.
