[BM] Krisis Kemampuan Perumahan: B40 Dan M40 Terkunci Kerana Harga Kekal Di Luar Jangkauan

Said by: Multiple economists and NAPIC

Category: Housing

Date: 2025-10-07

Janji Bumbung Yang Tidak Pernah Tiba

Meskipun kerajaan Anwar mengehadkan rumah mampu milik pada RM300,000, realiti bagi rakyat Malaysia B40 dan M40 kekal suram. Krisis kemampuan perumahan — salah satu masalah sosioekonomi paling serius abad ke-21 — hanya bertambah dalam di bawah PH.

Fakta-Fakta

Fakta: Menggunakan pendapatan median B40 sebagai penanda aras, rumah perlu berharga di bawah RM300,000 untuk dianggap mampu milik. Kerajaan menetapkan ini sebagai harga siling.

Fakta: Harga rumah nasional meningkat secara kumulatif 15.2% dari 2019 hingga S3 2025, tetapi dalam terma sebenar (diselaraskan inflasi), kenaikan hanya 4.2%.

Fakta: 54.3% transaksi kediaman KL adalah di bawah RM500,000, manakala 22.8% melebihi RM1 juta — menunjukkan pasaran yang sangat terbelah.

Fakta: Penyelidikan menggambarkan satu “dinamik tidak mampan, mengunci sebahagian besar rakyat — terutamanya M40 dan B40 atas — daripada pemilikan rumah.”

Kenapa Ia Penting

  • Menetapkan had harga bukan sama dengan membina rumah pada harga itu. Di mana unit-unitnya?
  • Rakyat Malaysia muda sedang diketepikan daripada bandar mereka sendiri. Ini adalah krisis generasi.

Housing Affordability Crisis: B40 and M40 Locked Out as Prices Remain Beyond Reach

Said by: Multiple economists and NAPIC

Category: Housing

Date: 2025-10-07

The Promise of a Roof That Never Comes

Despite Anwar’s government capping affordable housing at RM300,000, the reality for B40 and M40 Malaysians remains bleak. The housing affordability crisis — one of the most serious socio-economic problems of the 21st century — has only deepened under PH.

The Facts

Fact: Using B40 median income as a benchmark, homes need to be priced below RM300,000 to be considered affordable. The government set this as the ceiling price.

Fact: National house prices increased by a cumulative 15.2% from 2019 to Q3 2025, but in real (inflation-adjusted) terms, the increase was just 4.2%.

Fact: 54.3% of KL residential transactions were below RM500,000, while 22.8% were above RM1 million — showing a deeply split market.

Fact: Research describes an “unsustainable dynamic, locking up a significant portion of the people — particularly the M40 and higher B40 — out of homeownership.”

Why It Matters

  • Setting a price cap is not the same as building homes at that price. Where are the units?
  • Young Malaysians are being priced out of their own cities. This is a generational crisis.
  • PH’s housing promises — like so many others — exist on paper but not on the ground. *Sources: The Edge · *Research

📌 APA YANG BERLAKU

Tetapi entah bagaimana ia adalah kesalahan “sektor swasta.” Apabila Khairy mengeluarkan bukti, Tiong beralih kepada menyatakan “penyesalan mendalam” tanpa benar-benar memohon maaf.

Dan respons Anwar? “Amaran tegas.” Anwar yang sama yang pernah berjanji menteri-menterinya akan dipegang kepada standard tertinggi. Seorang menteri yang berbohong secara terbuka mengenai acaranya sendiri mendapat tamparan ringan. Jika ini menteri BN di bawah Najib, PH akan menuntut peletakan jawatan dalam 24 jam.

Di bawah BN, menteri yang berbohong mengenai acara awam dikoyak-koyak oleh pembangkang ketika itu. Amnesia terpilih PH mengenai standard akauntabiliti adalah tema berulang.

Seorang menteri yang berbohong tentang siapa menganjurkan acara rasmi, ditangkap basah, dan hanya menghadapi “amaran tegas” bukan integriti. Ia adalah kekebalan. Sama ada standard anda terpakai kepada semua menteri anda — termasuk rakan gabungan dari Sarawak — atau ia tidak bermakna apa-apa. Tiong sepatutnya digugurkan daripada Kabinet. Hakikat bahawa dia tidak memberitahu rakyat Malaysia segala-galanya yang mereka perlu tahu tentang di mana kelangsungan gabungan berada berbanding akauntabiliti.

Tiong King Sing Serves Alcohol at Official Tourism Gala, Then Lies About Who Organised It

Said by: Tiong King Sing, Tourism, Arts and Culture Minister

Category: Politics

Date: 2025-10-05

📌 WHAT HAPPENED

Tourism, Arts and Culture Minister Tiong King Sing came under fire in October 2025 after alcoholic beverages were served at a gala dinner during the Global Travel Meet — an event organised under the Tourism Malaysia banner. Video clips showed beer and wine being served to Muslim guests, prompting several to leave early.

Tiong initially denied responsibility, claiming the private sector organised the catering and that it was “not part of Tourism Malaysia’s official programme.” But former Health Minister Khairy Jamaluddin posted screenshots on Instagram showing Tourism Malaysia was listed as the organiser of the gala dinner, mocking Tiong with “Liar, liar pants on fire.”

Anwar eventually issued a “stern warning” to Tiong but did not sack him.

🔥 WHY IT MATTERS

This is a minister lying on record about his own ministry’s event, getting caught with documentary evidence, and facing zero consequences beyond a “stern warning.” In a government that promised integrity and accountability, a minister who lies publicly and gets caught should face real consequences — not a pat on the wrist.

The incident also exposed the deep contradictions in Anwar’s coalition management. Tiong is from GPS (Gabungan Parti Sarawak), a critical coalition partner. Sacking him would risk losing Sarawak’s support. So accountability takes a back seat to coalition arithmetic.

💩 WHY IT’S BULLSHIT

Tiong’s defence was a masterclass in gaslighting. The Tourism Malaysia logo was on the event backdrop. Tourism Malaysia staff were present. But somehow it was the “private sector’s” fault. When Khairy produced the receipts, Tiong pivoted to expressing “deep regret” without actually apologising.

And Anwar’s response? A “stern warning.” The same Anwar who once promised that his ministers would be held to the highest standards. A minister who lies publicly about his own event gets a slap on the wrist. If this were a BN minister under Najib, PH would have demanded a resignation within 24 hours.

📜 PREVIOUS CASES

Tiong King Sing is the same minister who screamed “Do you know who I am?” at an airport in February 2025 — a pattern of entitlement and arrogance. He was also previously embroiled in controversies linked to alcohol consumption before this gala incident. The man has a track record of embarrassing the government and facing no real consequences.

Under BN, ministers who lied about public events were torn apart by the then-opposition. PH’s selective amnesia about accountability standards is a recurring theme.

🎯 PH CALLOUT

PH promised Kerajaan Madani — a civilised government built on integrity. A minister who lies about who organised an official event, gets caught red-handed, and faces only a “stern warning” is not integrity. It’s impunity. Either your standards apply to all your ministers — including coalition partners from Sarawak — or they mean nothing. Tiong should have been dropped from Cabinet. The fact that he wasn’t tells Malaysians everything they need to know about where coalition survival ranks versus accountability.

[BM] Bajet Sukan Dipotong — Atlet Bergelut Sementara Kerajaan Belanja Berbilion Untuk GLC

Said by: National Sports Council / Athletes

Category: Other

Date: 2025-10-01

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Atlet Malaysia secara konsisten mengadu tentang pembiayaan yang tidak mencukupi, kemudahan latihan yang lemah, dan sistem sokongan yang tidak memadai, walaupun kerajaan menemui berbilion untuk penyelamatan GLC, pelantikan politik, dan faedah perkhidmatan awam. Bajet pembangunan sukan telah dihimpit apabila tekanan fiskal meningkat, menyerahkan atlet dalam sukan Olimpik dan Sukan Asia untuk bergantung kepada penajaan swasta atau pembiayaan sendiri.

🔥 Kenapa Ia Penting

Kemudahan latihan dalam banyak sukan sudah lapuk berbanding pesaing serantau seperti Thailand, Indonesia, dan Vietnam. Prestasi pasukan bola sepak kebangsaan telah menjadi sumber malu negara, dengan kemudahan dan pembangunan belia ketinggalan meskipun sukan ini mempunyai pengikut popular yang besar. Apabila atlet Malaysia berjaya — seperti yang kadangkala berlaku dalam badminton, terjun, atau berbasikal — kerajaan cepat menuntut kredit. Apabila mereka gagal, kekurangan pembiayaan sistematik yang menjadikan kegagalan tidak dapat dielakkan tidak diperiksa.

💩 Kenapa Ini Karut

Perbezaan antara bajet JAKIM RM2.6 bilion untuk hal ehwal agama dan peruntukan yang jauh lebih kecil untuk pembangunan sukan memberitahu anda segala-galanya tentang keutamaan kerajaan. Malaysia bercita-cita menjadi negara sukan tetapi mendanainya seperti perkara sampingan.

🎯 Teguran Kepada PH

Anda mahu pingat emas tetapi mendanai atlet seolah-olah mereka adalah baris perbelanjaan yang perlu diminimumkan. Anda mahu kebanggaan negara dalam sukan tetapi membelanjakan lebih banyak untuk birokrasi agama daripada pembangunan atletik. Pilih satu: sama ada danai sukan dengan sewajarnya atau berhenti berpura-pura peduli apabila pingat tidak datang.

[BM] Sekolah Cina Dan Tamil Dijadikan Bola Politik Setiap Bajet — Dijanjikan Jutaan, Diberikan Sedikit

Said by: Dong Zong / Tamil Foundation / MCA / MIC

Category: Education

Date: 2025-10-01

📌 Apa Yang Berlaku

Setiap kitaran bajet Malaysia menghasilkan teater yang sama: komuniti sekolah Cina dan Tamil melobi terdesak untuk dana penyelenggaraan, pembinaan dan pembangunan. Ahli politik mengumumkan peruntukan dengan penuh kemeriahan. Kemudian pencairan sebenar tidak mencukupi, tertangguh, atau disertakan syarat yang mengehadkan penggunaan. Kitaran ini berulang setiap tahun, dengan komuniti bukan Melayu menunjukkan rasa syukur terhadap peruntukan yang tidak memadai.

🔥 Mengapa Ia Penting

1.3 juta pelajar menghadiri sekolah aliran Cina (SJKC) dan ratusan ribu menghadiri sekolah aliran Tamil (SJKT). Sekolah-sekolah ini mendidik sebahagian besar penduduk bukan Melayu dan secara konsisten mengatasi sekolah kebangsaan dalam metrik tertentu. Namun mereka menerima pembiayaan kerajaan yang jauh lebih rendah bagi setiap pelajar secara sistematik, memaksa komuniti membiayai sendiri infrastruktur yang sepatutnya menjadi tanggungjawab kerajaan.

💩 Mengapa Ia Karut

PH, yang sangat bergantung kepada undi Cina dan India, sepatutnya telah menyelesaikan ketidaksamarataan yang telah berdekad lamanya ini. Sebaliknya, PH bermain permainan yang sama seperti BN: gunakan pengumuman bajet sebagai peluang bergambar dengan pemimpin komuniti Cina dan India, hasilkan tajuk berita penuh kesyukuran, kemudian gagal menyampaikan. Jurang struktur dalam pembiayaan setiap pelajar antara sekolah kebangsaan (SK) dan sekolah vernakular kekal kerana menanganinya memerlukan sama ada lebih banyak wang (yang kerajaan dakwa tiada) atau mengakui bahawa sistem semasa adalah diskriminasi (yang ia enggan lakukan).

📜 Kes Terdahulu

BN menggunakan peruntukan sekolah vernakular sebagai naungan politik — menawarkan dana sebagai pertukaran sokongan komuniti. MCA dan MIC wujud terutamanya untuk mendapatkan peruntukan ini, mengekalkan kebergantungan. PH sepatutnya memecahkan kitaran ini dengan membiayai sekolah secara saksama tanpa mengira bahasa pengantar. Sebaliknya, PH mengekalkan model BN: sekolah Cina dan India mendapat apa yang PH putuskan untuk diberikan, bila PH putuskan untuk memberikan.

🎯 Teguran PH

Rakyat Malaysia bukan Melayu tidak mengundi PH untuk meneruskan model naungan BN dengan tampilan lebih baik. Mereka mengundi untuk LAYANAN SAMA RATA. Biayai sekolah vernakular pada kadar setiap pelajar yang sama seperti sekolah kebangsaan. Bukan sebagai ihsan. Bukan sebagai peluang bergambar pengumuman bajet. Sebagai kewajipan perlembagaan di bawah Perkara 152 dan kesaksamaan pendidikan asas. Atau akui bahawa ‘Madani untuk semua’ ada tanda bintangnya: *terma dan syarat tertakluk berdasarkan etnik.

Chinese and Tamil Schools Used as Political Football Every Budget — Promised Millions, Delivered Scraps

Said by: Dong Zong / Tamil Foundation / MCA / MIC

Category: Education

Date: 2025-10-01

📌 What Happened

Every Malaysian budget cycle produces the same theatre: Chinese and Tamil school communities lobby desperately for maintenance, construction, and development funds. Politicians announce allocations with fanfare. Then the actual disbursement falls short, is delayed, or comes with conditions that limit use. The cycle repeats annually, with non-Malay communities performing gratitude for inadequate allocations.

🔥 Why It Matters

1.3 million students attend Chinese-medium schools (SJKC) and hundreds of thousands attend Tamil-medium schools (SJKT). These schools educate a significant portion of the non-Malay population and consistently outperform national schools in certain metrics. Yet they receive systematically less government funding per student, forcing communities to self-fund infrastructure that should be the government’s responsibility.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

PH, which depends heavily on Chinese and Indian votes, should have resolved this decades-old inequity. Instead, PH plays the same game as BN: use budget announcements as photo opportunities with Chinese and Indian community leaders, generate grateful headlines, then under-deliver. The structural disparity in per-student funding between national schools (SK) and vernacular schools remains because addressing it would require either more money (which the government claims it doesn’t have) or admitting that the current system is discriminatory (which it won’t).

📜 Previous Cases

BN used vernacular school allocations as political patronage — offering funds in exchange for community support. MCA and MIC existed primarily to secure these allocations, perpetuating dependency. PH was supposed to break this cycle by funding schools equitably regardless of medium of instruction. Instead, PH has maintained the BN model: Chinese and Indian schools get what PH decides to give them, when PH decides to give it.

🎯 PH Callout

Non-Malay Malaysians didn’t vote PH to continue BN’s patronage model with better optics. They voted for EQUAL treatment. Fund vernacular schools at the same per-student rate as national schools. Not as a favour. Not as a budget announcement photo op. As a constitutional obligation under Article 152 and basic educational equity. Or admit that ‘Madani for all’ has an asterisk: *terms and conditions apply based on ethnicity.

Sports Budget Slashed — Athletes Struggle While Government Spends Billions on GLCs

Said by: National Sports Council / Athletes

Category: Other

Date: 2025-10-01

📌 What Happened

Malaysian athletes consistently complain about inadequate funding, poor training facilities, and insufficient support systems, even as the government finds billions for GLC bailouts, political appointments, and civil service perks. The sports development budget has been squeezed as fiscal pressures mount, leaving athletes in Olympic and Asian Games sports to rely heavily on private sponsorship or self-funding.

🔥 Why It Matters

Training facilities in many sports are outdated compared to regional competitors like Thailand, Indonesia, and Vietnam. The national football team’s performance has been a particular source of national embarrassment, with facilities and youth development lagging despite the sport’s massive popular following. When Malaysian athletes succeed — as they occasionally do in badminton, diving, or cycling — the government is quick to claim credit. When they fail, the systemic underfunding that made failure inevitable goes unexamined.

💩 Why It’s Bullshit

The contrast between the RM2.6 billion JAKIM budget for religious affairs and the significantly smaller allocation for sports development tells you everything about government priorities. Malaysia aspires to be a sporting nation but funds it like an afterthought.

🎯 PH Callout

You want gold medals but fund athletes like they’re an expense line to be minimised. You want national pride in sports but spend more on religious bureaucracy than athletic development. Pick one: either fund sports properly or stop pretending to care when the medals don’t come.
