RON95 Subsidy Savings Slashed — From RM8 Billion Target to Actual RM950 Million

Hantu Raya

September 23, 2025

Said by: Anwar Ibrahim / Reuters

Category: Economy

Date: 2025-09-23

RON95 subsidy savings target slashed three times: RM8B to RM4B to RM950M actual. Barely 12% of the original target. The government spent more political capital debating the reform than it saved in ringgit.
